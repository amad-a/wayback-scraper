Late Mayor

Aown S. Shawa
(1934 - 2001)

 

         After a life full of contribution and struggle for Gaza City ... Mayor Shawa passed away, Leaving behind him a long record of achievements that, Gaza Citizens will never forget ...

 

About Mr. Shawa:

  • A graduate in Economics and Business Administration from the American University of Cairo (AUC).

  • President of the Association of Palestinian Local Authorities (APLA), (1998 - 2001).

  • President of Delegate, United Towns Organization (UTO).

  • Mayor of Gaza City, (1994 - 2001).

  • Passed away on Friday morning, the 30 th of November, 2001.
