Thank you for stopping by! Palestine is home to the threemonothiestic faiths. The pre-colonial and pre-occupationcomposition was less than 7% Jewish (reached 8% by 1918) , around10% (in 1918) Christian and the rest were Muslims. Almost allspoke, understood and communicated in Arabic (some spoke otherlanguages as well). Things changed with the British Mandate thenthe Israeli occupation in favor of the occupation at the expenseof Muslims and Christians.
