FEBRUARY PROGRAM

 

FEBRUARY, 2000 :

**RAMALLAH STRING QUARTET CONCERT:
TUESDAY 1/2 @ 7:30PM
The 3rd in our ongoing concert series featuring full length pieces,performed bythe instruments the music was originally written for. Performers come fromthebest UK & US musical institutions. Program of Beethoven, Bach, & Grieg. 

** ORIENTAL MUSIC CONCERT: AHMED KHATIB & BASSEL ZAYED:
THURSDAY 3/2 @ 7:00 PM
The trio of Ahmad al Khatib (Oud), Bassel (Oud & voice) and Youcef Zayed(Daff)will give a performance featuring traditional, classical, and modern musicalpieces,making the listener move between different periods of musical history. 

** PERU CULTURAL DAY:
SATURDAY 5/2 FROM 6PM
The program starts w/ an exhibition of paintings by Peruvian artists, aceramicscrafts exhibit -for sale-, & a buffet of Peruvian delicacies. At 7pm, therewill bea musical program: classical music recital, solo ballet by a Peruvianballerina, and afolkloric dance performed by an award winning dancing couple.. 

** POETRY READINGS: "REMARKABLE VOICES" :
TUESDAY 8/1 @ 6:00 PM
A showcase for new talents in poetry, focusing on young poets who have had their works recently published and/or that appear regularly in the local press.
Participating poets will be: Mahmoud abu Hashhash, Tareq al Karmi, Waleed Esheikh & Youcef el Mahmoud. 

** CLASSICAL MOVIES CYCLES: NEW
HITCHCOCK FOCUS:
A new program introducing world movie classics from the 1930s to the 70s to the Palestinian public. We will present cycles showcasing the works of the best directors, actors & movie genres. The films will be shown on mondays & wednesdays. Arabic summaries of the movies & information on the theme showcased will be distributed. We start w/ a focus on the master of suspense Alfred Hitchcock.

* "PSYCHO":
WED. 9/2 & MON. 21/2. @ 7PM
The master's most famous film, telling the story of vicious stabbings and disappearances in the sinister Bates Motel. Starring Anthony Perkins, Janet Leigh & Vera Miles. (B&W;, 1960. 108min.)

* "VERTIGO":
MON. 14/2 & WED. 23/2 @7PM
One of his best movies: Haunting sequences, a story of love beyond death, double identity, murder, and fear of heights.. A newly restored copy. The screening will also feature a short documentary on the film & the original trailer. Starring James Stewart and Kim Novak. (1958. color, 128min)

* "DIAL M FOR MURDER":
WED. 16/2 & MON. 28/2 @ 7PM.
Originally shot & showed in 3D, this suspenseful play-turned movie stars the original cool blonde & Hitchcock favorite, Grace Kelly (later princess of Monaco) in a gripping tale of murder, blackmail & treason. Starring also Ray Milland (Color, 1954. 101min). 

** SCREENING: FIRST 5 FILMS OF THE MILLENNIUM..:
SAT. 19/2 @ 7PM
We are proud to present in co-operation with Bethlehem 2000 the 5 filmsshown on Nativity Square December 31st at midnight. The 45min film features 5 shorts by Palestinian directors Mai Masri, Elie Suleiman, Subhi Zubeidi, Azza Hassan, and Rachid Masharawi. 

** BOOK REVIEW: EDWARD SAID'S "OUT OF PLACE":
SAT 26/2 @ 6PM
A book discussion of Edward Said's acclaimed -& in some quarterscontroversial - memoirs. Will be reviewed by Dr. Ibrahim Abu Lughod and novelist Ahmed Harb.
Copies of the book will be available for sale. 

**ORIENTAL MUSIC APPRECIATION WORKSHOP: REGISTRATION ISOPEN.
Following the success of the western classical music appreciation workshop, we are happy to offer this workshop focusing on oriental music, given by performer/composer Samir Jubran. Jubran is also an honors graduate from Cairo's prestigious Conservatory of Arabic Music. The 2-month course starts in March, will be given in Arabic, & will meet once a week for an hour and a half. Day of the week & time to be determined.
