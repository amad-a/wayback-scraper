Zakaria Mohammed

Zakaria Mohammed started sculpting five yearsago, without any formal training. His sculptures appear to have been foundin archeological excavations: Their strong primitive style recalls thesmall propitiatory statues used in the early antiquity period in the FertileCrescent. The sculptures’ forbidding aspect, and tortured style, add totheir power of expression. They reflect the dominant themes of Mohammed’sliterary works: Canaanite folklore ghouls and demons, fertility rites,etc..

The sculptures are executed in three media principally: Jerusalem stone(pink and white), wood, and insulating material. Sculptures are small tomedium in size, the largest are only 65cm. high. A number of small watercolorsand prints were exhibited. For biographical information on Z. Mohammed,please consult the Poets’ section
