Palestinian Poets

MaySayigh

Sayigh was born in 1940 in Gaza. She obtained in BA insociology from Cairo University. She was the president of the Union ofPalestinian Women and has been very active in the cause of Palestinianwomen. She has published a number of poetry collections, as well as a proseaccount of the 1982 Israeli invasion of Lebanon in "The Siege".She lives in Paris.
