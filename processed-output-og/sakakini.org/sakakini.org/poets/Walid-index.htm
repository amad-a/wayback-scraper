PalestinianPoets

WalidKhazindar  

Khazindar was born in 1950 in Gaza City. He obtained alaw degree in Beirut in 1978. He is considered one of the best Palestinianpoets, his poetry is characterised by metaphoric originality and a novelthematic approach unprecedented in Arabic poetry. He was awarded in1997 the first Palestine Prize for Poetry.
