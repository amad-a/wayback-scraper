Day Nine

 
Day 9 : Friday 06.10.2000

In  al-Aqsa  Mosque  , Israeli soldiers fired rubber-coated bullets on
Palestinian youths who chased the Israeli out of Aqsa, injuring twenty
protesters. The youths set fire on an Israeli police tower overlooking
the  al-Haram  al-Sharif  and  raise  the  Palestinian  flag in top of
adjacent building and in the Dome of the Rock Mosque .
Tens  of  thousands of Palestinians took to the streets throughout the
West  Bank  and Gaza Strip to express their outrage and indignation at
Israeli   atrocities   against   the   Palestinian   people.   Massive
demonstrations  and  marches proceeded from mosques , forming huge and
long  marches,  clashed  with  Israeli  occupation soldiers in several
"friction  spots"  in  Hebron, Bethlehm, Ramallah, Nablus and Tulkarm.
Israeli  soldiers,  taking  positions of rooftops, fired rubber-coated
steel  bullets, stun grenades on the demonstrators, injuring as may as
fifty protesters, three of them seriously.
