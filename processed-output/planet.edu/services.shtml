  Connectivity 

Currently Planet provides access to its services to about 600 users.  Users are of the following categories :

  • Higher Education Institutes
  • Academic & Non-Academic Research Centers
  • University faculty members
  • College students
  • School teachers

U niversities are connected through VSAT links. VSAT link provides unlimited email service accounts and about 10 to 15simultaneous Internet browsing accounts. Research Centers and other categories areconnected through dial-up links.

U niversity campuses that are within the  Jerusalem area are connected through leased lines. Now Planet offers leased lines connection throughpaltel between and within cities.

U niversities and colleges in the West Bank and Gaza are connected through wireless communication links. 

T hree of the campuses of Al-Quds University in Jerusalem are connected through leased lines.

A bu Deis campus and Medical Professions are connected throughVST. Al-Najah University, Hebron Polytechnic, Islamic University in Gaza are connectedthrough VSAT.

T he Ministry of Higher Education and Alteri Women’s college(UNRWA) are connected through  microwave links. 

  R esearch centers like ARIJ, Arab Studies, Mass, Almultaqa, and others are connected through different communication means/links.

Urgent Needs

Subscription to Electronic Journals: what makes Planet unique in itsservices is that it always looks for fund to buy databases and electronic Journals to the academic community. Our most urgent project is to subscribe to electronic Journals like Current Content and install it on the server for fast and efficient  access for Palestinians. Fund is urgently needed since Palestinian universities are in very much need for such service as printed journals are not possible to get due to financial problems. Bibliographical data on books, journals, andresearch reportsare always in big demand.
