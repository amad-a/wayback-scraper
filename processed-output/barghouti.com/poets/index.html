Poets From Palestine
P oets From Palestine is an attempt to introduce a group of Palestinian poets who, in their poems, told the story of The Occupation and The Resistance. They talked about the love of the land and its people. They sang for The Revolution, The Struggle, and The Sacrifice. They dreamed about The Victory and The Return . Their poems were carried from one place to another everywhere Palestinians went. From the cities and villages of Palestine to the camps in Lebanon passing by the Arabian Gulf to the rest of the world.
Ibrahim Tukan
Abdelrahim Mahmud
Abdelkarim Al-Karmi (Abu Salma)
