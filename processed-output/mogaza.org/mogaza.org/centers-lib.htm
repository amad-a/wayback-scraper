Municipality's Public Library
Overview

    Gaza Municipality Public Library (GMPL) was established in 1999 by Gaza Municipality in collaboration with the Urban Community of Dunkerque, Cites Unies France, the World Bank through PECDAR, Caisse des depots et consignations (France). The present collection came mainly as gift and donation from different local and Arab institutions. It is the first of its kind in the city. It contributes to the cultural, social, educational development of Gaza Community. It is located in a building consists of 2 floors and basement. The total space is about 1410 square meters.

Objectives

  • To promote culture and knowledge in broad sense.

  • To help in preserving the national cultural heritage.

  • To encourage the reading habits, especially among children.

  • To provide materials which meet the need and requirements of the users.

  • To provide fast and easy access to all materials in the library.

  • To give access to the global information through the multimedia tools.

  • To organize cultural and educational events like lectures, seminars, group discussions, exhibitions, etc.

 Library Collection

    The collection of the books and periodicals totals more than 10000 volumes. The library has books and periodicals in three languages Arabic, English, and French. The library subscribes to about 100 periodical titles. Audiovisual materials collection consists of videotapes and audiocassettes. In addition, there are some electronic materials of CD-ROMs.
