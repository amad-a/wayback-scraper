What Systems does Squid run on?
Squid runs on most versions of Unix and OS/2It is known to work on: 

      AIX, Digital Unix, FreeBSD, HP-UX, Irix, Linux, NetBSD, Nextstep, SCO, Solaris

If you get it running on another system, please mail me andgive me any special details as to how you got it to compile, and I willadd it to this list.
