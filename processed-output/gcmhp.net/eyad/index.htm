Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj
  Welcome

Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj is the founder anddirector of the Gaza Community Mental Health
Programme (GCMHP). He is also the Commissioner-Generalof the Palestinian
Independent Commission for Citizens'Rights. He was born in Beersheva, Palestine,
and currently lives in Gaza. He is marriedand has two children, Sayf & Wasseem.
