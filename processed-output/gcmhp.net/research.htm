Research Center

Immediately after the opening of the Programme, the research team was established inorder to investigate and analyze the effects of violence, on individualsand families. The purpose of this is also to aid our clinical staff indetermining the most effective methods of treatment.
We also hope that knowledge gained from our experiencein dealing with large-scale trauma, particularly among children, will beof use to mental health workers elsewhere.

Our on-going projects are:

1- "Trauma, Parenting, and Bedwetting"
2- "The Longitudinal Study for the Long-termEffects of Traumatic Events"
3- "Codification of MMPI2 Test"
4- "Schizophrenia and Social Support"
5- "A Study on Some Neurotic Symptoms of victimsof Violence in the Gaza Strip
