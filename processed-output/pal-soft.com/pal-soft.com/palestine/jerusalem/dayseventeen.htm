Day Seventeen
 
  
Day 17 : Saturday 14.10.2000
Palestinian  leadership  agreed  to  participate  in  Sharm  el-Sheikh
summit.  PA  officials  assure the people that PA participation in the
conference would not be at the expense of the intifada.
The  total  palestinian  killed  in the ongoing Israeli bloody clashes
with the Palestinian intifada reached to 110 and the number of injures
reached more then 4000.
Clashes  between  Palestinian youths and Israeli soldiers broke-out in
Hebron  ,  the  Israeli  soldiers  used live bullets and rubber coated
steel  bullets  against the rocks throwing Palestinian injuring number
of them . (Photo 1)
In  Hebron  ,  Huge number of Palestinians carry the coffin of Monsiur
Sid  Ahmed  (22  Years  old)  covered by Palestinian flag , during his
funeral.  Monsiur  Sid  Ahmed was killed by Israeli soldiers on Friday
during Palestinian demonstration . (Photo 2)
In  Gaza city, Thousands of Palestinians took to the streets in a huge
demonstration , Palestinian were carrying a coffin with the picture of
Clinton  and  the USA flag , also another coffin with Israeli flag and
Barak  photo,  demonstrators  were protest against the PNA decision to
participate in Sharm el-Sheikh summit. (Photo 3)
Another  Palestinian  was  martyred by Israeli soldiers bullets Friday
night  in  confrontation  between  Palestinian  protesters and heavily
armed  Israeli  soldiers  near  Al  Fawar  refugee  camp south West of
Hebron.  Shadi  Alwawi  was  killed  when  Israeli occupation soldiers
randomly opened live bullets fire at Palestinian shacks in the refugee
camp,  Eyewitness  reported  that  Alwawi was standing in front of his
home  when  he  was  hit  by  an  Israeli  live  bullet  in  the head.
Palestinian march in his funeral. (Photo 4)
PNA  police  have  sent  re-arrest  warrants  to  a  number of Islamic
activist  who  have  been  released from prisons in the last few days.
Hamas  Movement  has  rejected  accusations  that the Movement was the
cause  of  the  burning of "Tourism sites" such as liquor stores , few
days  back group of young men, who separated from a march organized by
Hamas  ,  had  attacked  two  liquor  stores  along  with  a hotel and
restaurant.
