Isabel de la Cruz Exhibit

Isabel de la Cruz - Shahwanwas born in 1963 in Germany to a German mother and a Peruvian father .She obtained a BA in history from the University of Virginia , and an MAin Arab Studies from Georgetown University . She took her first formalinstruction in photography at U.Va. and set up her own darkroom in Germany.

She worked in Sudan and Egypt with NBC News , and acquiredexperience in news photograpy . She then worked as a freelance jounalistfor Deutsche-Welle TV , and as a photo editor for the European Press Agencyin Frankfurt . Isabel also attended two courses at the Maine photographicworkshops to fine tune her skills in black and white photography and inprinting processes . Isabel has focused on photography in the last twoyears , her first project was the publication of a 1996 black and whitecalendar entitled Palestiniana containing photographs of palestinians, the West Bank and Gaza from a non-news perspective. At the same time,she completed a slide and music show by the same title which was screenedat the Wasiti Arts Center in April 1996. She held her first soloexhibit in Jerusalem in May 1996. Of her work she says: "I wantedto crysllise all the positive aspects of Palestinian culture, tradition,and history through consciously beautiful photography as a counter-balanceto the grim realities of the day".
