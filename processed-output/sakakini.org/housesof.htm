Houses of BethlehemExhibit


The KSCC resumed its program of exhibitswith the opening of the Houses of Bethlehem show on February 17 in cooperationwith the newly opened French Cultural Center of Ramallah. This show featuresphotographs, drawings and architectural croquis of old Bethlehem houses.The show has toured in Gaza, Jerusalem and Hebron, will tour other Palestiniancities, and will end in Paris in June, where the exhibit will coincidewith the Palestinian Spring cultural activities due to begin all over Francein March 1997.

The show aims to showcase local architecture by outlining the intrinsiccharacteristics of Palestinians vernacular architecture. The show was thefruit of the labor of a team of Palestinian and French architects froma number of Palestinian and French architecture schools, led by Serge Santelliof Paris-Belleville Architecture school, Catherine Weill-Rochant of Jerusalem,Elias Deick of Bethlehem University, and Shadi Ghadbane of Bir Zeit University.
