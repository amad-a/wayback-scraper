Short Story Writers
Gharib Asqalany


Cover of "The Seagull.."


Writings:
Asqalany ‘s strongly realistic style, serves the subject matter of his works, mostly focused on life under occupation, and in Palestinian refugee camps. He is a novelist, but is best known for his short stories. In addition to the books listed below, he has one unpublished novel, & 2 unpublished short story collections. He has also published two anthologies of the Palestinian short story in 1977 & in 1992. Some of his stories have been translated in English, Russian, & French publications.

1979: (Breaking the Silence):
Short story collection published by Al-Bayader publishing house in Jerusalem. "The Hunger" a short story from this collection, is translated into English in S. K. Jayyusi’s "Anthology of Modern Palestinian Literature". 1992, Columbia U. Press, NY, NY.

1979: (The Closure):
Novel, published by Dar el Kitab in Jerusalem

1983: (Time of Vigilance):
Novel, published by the Palestinian Writers’ Union (PWU) in Jerusalem

1991: (Stories on the Buds of Days):
Short story collection, published by the PWU in Jerusalem.

1992: (The Child & the Small Sun):
Short story collection, published by the PWU in Jerusalem.

1996: (The Seagull Heads North):
Short story collection published by the Palestinian ministry of culture, in Ramallah.

1997: (The Navigator’s Star):
Novel serialized in the weekly Al-Bilad from 18.12.97.
