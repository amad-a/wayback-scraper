Current Activities

Septmeber, 1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

* The new novel of Ahmad Rafiq Awad, "Maqamatal Uchaq wal Tujar" was reviewed at the Centre, in a book discussionwith poet Hussein Barghouti, and critic Mazen Saadeh.

* A symposium on the life & legacy of Khalil Sakakiniwas organized, on the occasion of the 44th anniversary of his death. Speakersincluded former pupils, and researchers of his work such as: Dr. IbrahimAbu Loghod, Salah Baranssi, Wassim Kurdi, and Ibrahim Abu Ghosh. A heateddiscussion followed the proceedings on the state of the Palestinian educationalsystem, with calls for the resumption of the teaching of the Khalil Sakakinimanuals in schools.

* Painter Samia Halaby made a gift to the Centre of fourprints of her computer kinetic paintings. The prints will be part of thepermanent collection of the Centre.
