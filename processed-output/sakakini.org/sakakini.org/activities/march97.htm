Current Activities

  March, 1997:

The Centre hosted this month an exhibition ofmodern Russian Art, loaned by Palestinian collector Daoud Barakat. Thecollection contains works by Tymour, Vukolov, Dudnig, Kashishvili, DianaVoba, Sergei Borsov, etc. The Centre also hosted the following events:

-A conference by Palestinian American author Lisa Majajon Identity construction in Palestinian American literature,

-A poetry reading evening by poet Ali el Khalili ,

-A lecture on the destructive Israeli excavations underthe Jerusalem holy mosques esplanade by Issam Awwad, the engineer supervisingthe renovation work on the mosques since 1972,

-A meeting with Artist Tayseer Barakat with a slide displayshowing the evolution of his work from his mid-1980s oil paintings, towork on paper, to his current phase of working with wood and installations.

- The Centre alsohosted a tribute organized by the Ministry ofCulture, honoring veteran Palestinian painters Ismail Shammout and hiswife Tamam el Akhal. Shammout and Al Akhal are considered the PalestinianRivera/Siqueros for their portrayal of the Palestinian struggle, and pre-1948Palestinian scenes. After a 30 year exile, they returned to Palestine forthe first time, and visited their hometowns of Yaffa and Lid.
