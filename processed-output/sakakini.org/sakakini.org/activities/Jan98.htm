Current Activities

January, 1998:


Mounira Zreiqi in "Um Robabika". KSCC Photo  

- The Sakakini hosted an exceptional representation of " Umm el Robabika ", the Emile Habibi monodrama, about awoman who remained in Haifa after the 1948 exile. The play is a new (1997)production by Jordan’s Darat el Funoun,  starring stage & TV actressMounira Zreiqi, & was the second directorial effort by award winningstage actress Kifah Salameh.

- ‘Oud player Samir Jubran gave a solo music recital,playing excerpts from his new CD : Taqaseem. Jubran is a young musicianfrom Nazareth,  who graduated with honors from Cairo’s prestigiousArab Music Institute, and has performed in Paris, the Netherlands, andat the Palestine and Jerash festivals.
