Current Activities

April,1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

-A lecture by Dr. Saleh Abdel Jawad from Bir-Zeit Universityon the sociological development of the City of El Bireh. The lecture wasco-sponsored by Riwaq.

-A poetry reading by British poet Benjamin Zephaniah.

-Isabel de la Cruz photo exhibit.

-Screening of two films on Norwegian painters Edvard Munchand contemporary artist Frans Widerberg.

-A classical music recital featuring visiting Americanviolist, and London Symphony Orchestra member Peter Sulski, and young Palestinianpianist Lara Harb, on her first public concert. The recital featured worksby Bach, Schubert, Francois Francoeur, and contemporary American composerPaul Gregory.
