Azzi was born in 1955 in Shefa Amr, and lives in Haifa. He obtainedan MA from Tel Aviv University in 1985, and has widely exhibited in Palestine,Israel, Europe and the United States. He is one of the rare Palestinianpainters to have participated in the Venice Biennial (1986).
