VladimirTamari  

Tamariwas born in Jerusalem in 1942. He studied fine arts at the American Universityof Beirut and at St. Martin's Academy in London. He now lives in Tokyo.He is a multi talented artist, having created a new Arabic typeface called"Jerusalem", invented a three dimensional drawing instrument,participated in the making of a documentary film on Jerusalem, and in thedesign of various publications. He works mainly in watercolours . He has participated in a number of personal and groupexhibits in Japan, the United States, Jerusalem and Jordan.
