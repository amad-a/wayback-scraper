JawadEl Malhi

ElMalhi is considered one of the most promising young painters of thenew generation. He was born in Jerusalem in 1969. He studied painting privatelythere and in the West Bank. He has held exhibits in Ramallah, Jerusalem,Tokyo, the United States, Germany, and Stockholm.
