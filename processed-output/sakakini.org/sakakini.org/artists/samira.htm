Samira Badran

Badranwas born in 1954 in Libya, to an artistic Palestinian family. She studiedat the Cairo University fine arts faculty, and at the Florence Fine ArtsAcademy. She was very active in the Palestinian art scene in the late 1970sand eighties. She now lives in Spain. In 1980 she undertook a mural paintingproject at Belgium's Liege University. She has exhibited in Jerusalem,Ramallah, at the London Barbican, in Paris, Spain, in a number of Arabcountries, and at the Washington Museum of Women in the Arts. Her worksare filled with an "explosive anger, characterized by a savage psychodramaof paint". She has recently experimented with works based on photocopies,and with painting on photographs.
