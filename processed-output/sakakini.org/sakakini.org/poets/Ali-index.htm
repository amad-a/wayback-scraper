Palestinian Poets

Ali elKhalili  

El Khalili was born in Nablus in1943. He graduated in 1966 from the Arab University of Beirut with a degreein Business Administration. He worked as a teacher, a journalist, and editorin chief of newspapers, both In Palestine and in Arab countries; and isnow a Director General in the Palestinian Ministry of Culture. A prolificwriter, he is also a short story writer, a novelist, and has publishedbooks on Palestinian folklore.
