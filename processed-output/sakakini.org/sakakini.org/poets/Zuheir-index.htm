Palestinian Poets

ZuheirAbu Shayeb  

Abu Shayeb was born in the village of Deir Ghusun in 1958,and obtained a BA in Arabic Literature from the Jordanian Yarmouk Universityin 1983. He worked as a teacher, a graphic designer, and is also an artcritic. He has several poetry collections, and is considered one of themost promising young poets in the Arab world.
