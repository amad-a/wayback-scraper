Khaled Hourani

Hourani was born in Hebron in 1965, and graduatedwith a BA in history from its university. He has participated in a numberof group exhibits in Jerusalem, Gaza, Ramallah and Hebron. He held hisfirst solo exhibit in Jerusalem in 1990, and participated in the 6th annualCairo biennial in 1997. He was selected by the Swiss arts foundation Pro-Helvetiato spend six months in Geneva as a resident artist. His second solo exhibitwas held at the Sakakini in May 97, it showcased the works completed inSwitzerland, consisting mainly of collages of papyrus, paper with acrylic,and an installation centered around a large chessboard, in addition tofree standing works made of wood and/or rubber. Hourani works at the Palestinianministry of culture, & runs the Matall gallery in Ramallah.
He also participated in a Palestinian art group exhibit at Norway's Drammens Museum in August 1999.
