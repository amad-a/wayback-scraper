Samir Salameh

The Artist:
Salameh was born in Safad in 1948, and was raised outside Palestine. He studied fine arts in Damascus and Paris. He has held more than 12 personal exhibits in Paris, Kuwait, Algiers, Damascus, Tunis, Cairo, Athens, etc.. He also participated in a number of group exhibits and biennials in Jerusalem, Berlin, Havana, and Kuwait.
