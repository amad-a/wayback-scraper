Ismail Shammout


The Artist:
Shammout was born in 1930 in Lydda, Palestine. His family sought refuge in Khan Yunis after the Nakba. He studied at Cairo’s Fine Arts College and at Rome’s Fine Arts Academy. He was appointed in 1965 as head of the PLO’s fine arts department, and obtained a special Palestine Award in 1997. Shammout has exhibited widely throughout the world, and now lives and works in Amman, Jordan.
