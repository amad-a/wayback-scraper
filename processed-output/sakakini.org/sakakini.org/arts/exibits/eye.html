“An Eye on Nature” (Photo exhibit)
Ziad Izzat
6/2000


Thirty-four colored photographs of flowers, greenery, plant and nature from all over Palestine are included in Ziad Izzat's exhibit "An Eye on Nature". Photography is a hobby for Ziad Izzat, a physics teacher at Birzeit University. Izzat hopes that after looking at his pictures, people will be tempted to visit the real thing-the villages, valleys and plains that Izzat photographed. He believes that getting to know Palestine by visiting its nature is the best way to build a connection between man and land (place).
