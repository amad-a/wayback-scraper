Palestinian Novelists

Anton Shammas

Biography

Anton Shammas is a novelist, playwright, poet, essayistand translator. He was born in 1950 in Fassuta, Israel, and lived in Haifaand Jerusalem, where he studied at the Hebrew University in Jerusalem.He edited the Ash-Sharq Arabic monthly literary magazine in Jerusalemfrom 1970 to 1975, and worked as a free-lance journalist and columnistfor the Hebrew newspapers Kol-Ha’eer, and Ha’eer. He hasbeen living since 1987 in the United States, and teaching at the Universityof Michigan in Ann Arbor since 1989.
