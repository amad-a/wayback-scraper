Yahyia Yakhluf


Biography

Yahyia Yakhluf was born in 1944 in the village of Samakh, borderingLake Tiberias.
In May 1948, when the city was shelled and fell to the Israelis, hisfamily fled to Jordan. Samakh was demolished, & Israeli settlers occupiedit.

Yakhluf joined the PLO in 1967 & obtained a BA in Arabic literaturefrom the Beirut Arab university in 1971. For many years, he was the secretary-generalof the Palestinian Writers Union, assistant secretary-general of the ArabWriters Union,  and director general of the PLOâ€™s cultural department.Since 1994, he has been  deputy minister of culture in the PalestinianNational Authority. He is a married father of three.
