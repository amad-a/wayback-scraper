Current Activities

  June,1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

A book discussion of the new critically acclaimed shortstory collection by Iman Bassir, Jassad min Bakhour, with a numberof critics.

A slide show by Palestinian artist LatifaYoussef , centered around the development of her creative process throughthe different stages of her life in Beyrouth, Tunis, Amman, the Gulf, andnow Palestine.

An evening of painting and music with Palestinian artistSamia Halaby and US musician Kevin Nathaniel. While Nathaniel played rhythmicAfrican instruments, Halaby created on PC, with a software of her design,beautiful moving images, in tableaux evocatively titled "Rain","Yaffa", "Brass woman", etc.. The images were projectedvia LCD projector on a large screen.

A lecture by Dr. Nasir Arafat from Riwaq on the historicalarchitectural development of the City of Nablus.

Screening of a documentary on Literature Nobel prize winner,Toni Morrisson
