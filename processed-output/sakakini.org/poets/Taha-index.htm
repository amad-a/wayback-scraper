Palestinian Poets

TahaMohammed Ali  

Ali was born in 1931 in Saffouria, a village in the Nazarethdistrict razed to the ground in 1948. He now lives in Nazareth. He is selftaught and began his poetry career late (in 1983). His poetry is very popularin Palestine and among the Arabs of Israel, and he has published severalcollections of poetry. 

He is also a short story writer.
