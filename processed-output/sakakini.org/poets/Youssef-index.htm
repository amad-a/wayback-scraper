Palestinian Poets

YoussefAbd al -Aziz  

Abd al-Aziz was born in Beit Inan near Jerusalem in 1956,and graduated from the Arab University of Beirut in 1986. He lives in Jordanand has published several collections of poetry. His experimentalist poemsare considered to be among the best in Palestinipoetry.
