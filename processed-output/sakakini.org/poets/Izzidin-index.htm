Palestinian Poets

Izzidinal- Manasra  

Manasra was born in Hebronin 1946 and obtained in 1981 a Ph.D in Slavic literature from the BulgarianAcademy of Science. He worked as an editor, and has published many volumesof poetry. He is regarded as one of the foremost Palestinian poets of protestand resistance.
