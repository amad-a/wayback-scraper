PalestinianPoets

Mohammedal Qaissi  

Al Qaissi was born in 1944 near Jaffa, and was raisedin refugee camps. He has a BA in Arabic literature. He worked as a teacherand as a journalist, and lives in Jordan. He is one of the most prolificpoets of his generation , his poetry is varied stylistically as he usesthe free verse as well as the two hemistich verse. Thematically, he occasionallyincludes literary and historical allusions and folklore .  Aftera thirty year career as a poet, he announced in 1998 his decision to devotehimself solely to prose writing.
