Palestinian Poets

  TaherRiyad  

Riyad was born in 1956 in Amman and was unable to finishhis University education because of financial difficulties. He has translatedworks by Eliot, Becket, Dylan Thomas and others into Arabic. He is co-directorof a publishing house in Jordan, and has published a number of poetry collections.His works are considered to be among the most original of the young generation,combing the eloquence of classical poetry and the innovation of modernistArabic poetry.
