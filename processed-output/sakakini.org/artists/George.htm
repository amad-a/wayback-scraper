PalestinianVisual Artists

  GeorgeAnastas  

Anastas is the most wellknown Palestinian sculptor working today. Born in Bethlehem in 1952, hestudied fine arts at the Iniaza Institute in Italy. He also has a BA infine arts and has participated in a number of group exhibits in Jerusalem,Gaza City and Italy. He is a member of the Yabous artists group.
