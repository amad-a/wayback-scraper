Kamal Boullata

Boullatawas born in 1942 in Jerusalem where he grew up. He graduated from the RomeFine Arts Academy and from the Corcoran Museum Art School, and now livesin Washington. He works mainly in silk screen . His compositions are basedon the angular Kufi script which he usesas a representational form of art. He has had a number of personal exhibitsin the United States, Kuwait, Holland, and Canada, as well as in the UK,Morocco, and Jerusalem. He participated in the FrenchPalestinian spring exhibit at the Paris Institut du Monde Arabe, andwrote the exhibit's catalogue.
