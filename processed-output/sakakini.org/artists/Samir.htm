PalestinianVisual Artists

Samir Salameh

Salamehwas born in Safad in 1948, and was raised and lived outside Palestine.He studied fine arts in Damascus and Paris. After living in Paris for along time, he has recently returned to Palestine. He has held more than12 personal exhibits in Paris, Kuwait, Algiers, Damascus, Tunis, Cairo,Athens, etc.. He has also participated in a number of group exhibits andbiennials in Jerusalem, Berlin, Havana, and Kuwait. His bold works canalso be found in public collections such as theTunisMuseum of Art, the Postdam Museum, and Jordan's National Gallery. He participatedin the 1998 Stockholm Fine Arts Academy “Palestinian Art” exhibit. Mal'ula. 1994. Acrylic/canvas. 190x160cm.
