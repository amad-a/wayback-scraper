PalestinianVisual Artists

KhalilRabah

Rabah was born in 1961 in Ramallah, and is one of the most avant garde Palestinianvisual artists; working exclusively with installations, live and performanceart. He graduated in architecture and fine arts from Texas University.He has held a number of exhibits in Jerusalem, Amman, France, Korea, theUS and Israel. His installations have used objects emblematic ofPalestinian identity: Olive trees, olive oil, stones, silk embroidery threads,etc.. He participated in the French Cultural Springexhibits
