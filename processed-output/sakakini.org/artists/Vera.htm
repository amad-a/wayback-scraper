VeraTamari


 


Tamari is a ceramist born in Jerusalem in 1945. She studied fine arts in Beirut,ceramics in Florence, and obtained an M.Phil in Islamic Art and Architecturefrom Oxford University. She is now a lecturer at Birzeit University inIslamic Art. She held personal exhibits in Amman, Jerusalem, and Ramallah.She has participated in a number of group exhibits in Palestine, Jordan,London, and Baghdad. She is a founding member of the "New Visions" artistsgroup, and of Al Wasiti Art Center in Jerusalem. Some of her new workscan be seen on the internet.

After working on ceramic objects, she is now working on"sculpted paintings", representing sceneries, people, and old family photographsin clay. She participated in the 1998 Stockholm Fine Arts Academy “PalestinianArt” exhibit. She is a member of the Sakakini's General Assembly.
