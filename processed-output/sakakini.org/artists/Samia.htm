Samia Halaby

Halabywas born in Jerusalem in 1936, and was raised in Haifa. She left Haifawith her family hoping to return, but they emigrated instead to the UnitedStates. She now lives in New York and teaches at Yale. She has participatedin a number of group exhibits in Jerusalem, Jordan, Austria, Cuba, Norway, Sweden, and the UK, and in the United Sates notablyat the Guggenheim in 1975, and at the Museum of Women in the Arts in Washington,in 1994. She's also held a number of personal exhibits in the UnitedStates. She participated in the 1998 Stockholm Fine Arts Academy “PalestinianArt” exhibit. She is a member of the Sakakini's General Assembly.
