Issam Bader  

Bader is a ceramist and painter born in 1948 in Hebron. He graduated from theBaghdad fine arts academy, and obtained a Masters in Ceramics from theTbilisi Academy of Fine Arts in the Soviet Union. He has held 10 personalexhibits, and has participated in a number of group exhibits in Palestine,Cairo, and Tokyo. He runs the Gallery 79 in Ramallah, and is an adviserat the ministry of culture.
