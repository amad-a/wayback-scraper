PalestinianVisual Artists

Leila Shawa  

Shawawas born in Gaza City in 1946. She graduated from the Cairo Fine Arts Schooland the Rome Fine Arts Academy. She now lives in London, before movingthere, she worked as an art instructor, and designed a number of books.She has held a number of personal exhibits in Palestine, Beirut, Kuwaitin Amman, and has participated in a number of group exhibits in Amman,London, Paris, and at the Washington Museum of Women in the Arts in 1994.Her work can be found in private and public collections around the worldsuch as the British Museum's 20th Century Collection, and the NationalArt Galleries of Jordan and Malaysia. She participated in the FrenchPalestinian spring exhibit at the Paris Institut du Monde Arabe.
