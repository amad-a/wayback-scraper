HussniRadwan

Radwan is one of the boldest Palestinian colorists. His large canvassesmix shards of pottery, pieces of paper and cloth, with acrylic and oilcolors. He has also experimented recently with wood assemblages. Radwanwas born in 1955 in Baghdad, where he obtained a BA from its fine artscollege. He works as a graphic designer for many local quarterlies andpapers. He has exhibited widely in Tunisia, Iraq, Cyprus, Palestine,  and participated in a graphic art Biennials in Sharjah in the United ArabEmirates, and in Berlin.
He also participated in a Palestinian art group exhibit at Norway's Drammens Museum in August 1999.



Space. Acrylic on Canvas. 120cmx80cm.1998
