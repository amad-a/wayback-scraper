Suha Shoman


 
Suha Shoman was born in Jerusalem in 1944, and studied lawin Lebanon. She studied painting in Amman, Jordan, with artist FahrelnissaZeid. Since 1986 her bold and large sized works (canvas, paper, installations)have been inspired by the colors and textures of the ancient city of Petra,south of Jordan. She has participated in a number of solo and group exhibitsin the Arab world, Asia, Europe, the US, as well as in the PalestinianCultural Spring art exhibit of 1996 She founded and works at the artisticcomplex Darat el Funun in Amman (library, exhibit space, artistsâ€™ residences,performing arts venue, etc..). She is an honorary member of the SakakiniGeneral assembly.
