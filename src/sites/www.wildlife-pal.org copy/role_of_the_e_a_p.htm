The Role of the Environmental Awareness program:

            The program tries to consolidate the environmental concepts that are related to the man's life, which are expressed as behaviors by the child as well as by the adult, through directing the programs activities to:

       1.The Students: in order to reach the program target that will carry the burden of the protection of nature in the Palestinian community in the future.

       2.The Community: through the awareness leaders, as well as to enhance the role of the Palestinian institutions in the protection of nature for a better future for the generations to come.
