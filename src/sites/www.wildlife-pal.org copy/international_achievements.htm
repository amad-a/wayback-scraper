International Achievements:

  •  Birdlife International: An Affiliation in 1999; Partner Designated in 2001, with all what this title gives from rights.

  • United Nation of Convention to Combat Desertification- UNCCD: Accreditation status with UNCCD to Palestine Wildlife Society in the year 2001. PWLS has been implementing the criteria of the UNCCD in different areas in Palestine such as the Jordan Valley and the Eastern Slops.

  • Euronatur- Germany: a full partner in the year of 1999.

  • Ornithological Society for the Middle East-OSME-UK: Country Contact about the general situation of birds in the year 2001.

  • OISCA- Japan International: Palestine Chapter in the year of 2001.

  • MEEEF (United States): Partner in the year of 1998

  • Participated in providing the information and preparing the book of "Global Threatened Bird" the Palestinian chapter.

  • Accreditation from the Global Environmental Facility(GEF) in 2003.
