THE "BIRZEIT, PALESTINE AND THE WORLD WIDE WEB" MAILING LIST AND ARCHIVE

Subscribers to the list will receive occasional mailings related to the work of Birzeit and other Palestinian institutions on the Internet, including:

  • Announcements and reviews of new Palestinian websites and mailing lists.

  • Notification of important updates to Birzeit's website, including Birzeit News items.

  • News of important developments and events in the Palestinian Internet community.

  • Access to analysis and commentary about the development of the Internet in Palestine.

    To be added to the mailing list, please click here and press send.

    To unsubscribe, click here and press send.

    Note: If you are sending from an e-mail address other than that from which you wish to be subscribed, send an e-mail to the Birzeit Webmaster with the correct e-mail address.

    The mailing list serves as an excellent companion to the COMPLETE GUIDE TO PALESTINE'S WEBSITES, ensuring you are kept up-to-date with the newest and best of the Palestinian Internet. If for some reason you miss an issue, an archive of the e-mailings is available.

    For those wishing to keep up to date with developments in the country through e-mail from Palestine, a list of local institutions that have mailing lists is offered in the Palestinian mailing lists link in the right column.

    Don't forget to check out BIRZEIT, PALESTINE AND THE WORLD WIDE WEB section of the Birzeit website for archived papers and reports, articles and links.

    Your comments on Birzeit's website and Internet work are welcomed

    Nigel Parry
    Birzeit Webmaster
