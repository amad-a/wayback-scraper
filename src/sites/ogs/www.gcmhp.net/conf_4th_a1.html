Gaza Community Mental Health Programme
4th International Conference
"Women in Palestine"
21-23 November, 1999

Themes will include:

 
 - Gender and Development
 - Women and Health, Education, Environment and Culture
 - Palestinian Law and Women
 - Women and Labor
 - The Role of Women in the Developing Community
 - Women in the Political Struggle
 - Women and Human Rights
 - Women in Islam

Researchers, academics, and professionals are invited to submit abstracts of papers for presentation at the conference.  The date for submission of abstracts is no later than 1 July, 1999.

The Conference reception party will be held on the evening of 20 November.
