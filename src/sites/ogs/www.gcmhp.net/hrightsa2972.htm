1.1.d Diploma course

The curriculum will place the study of Mental Health within the wider social, political, and environmental and biology contacts. It will analyses the length between Mental Health and Human Rights. In January 1997, 15 students started therapist graduate Diploma in Community Mental Health, The Diploma aims equip candidates with knowledge and skills related to Community Mental Health.

1.1.e Workshops and Study days:

- On 6th July 1997, a member of HRC participated in a workshop in cooperation with Fatah Youth Movement in Alazhar University with title of “Factors affecting marriage and violence in families”.

-On 15th July 1997, Mr. Ahmad Abu Tawahina participated in a workshop with title; “Five year Health Plan” which did the Ministry of Health organize.

- On 16th July, a member of Human Rights Committee participated in a workshop for children care givers with title” communication in Palestinian Society”.

- On 28th August 1997, Mr. Ahmed Abu Tawahina, Dr. Mustafa El Masri, Mr. Hasan Ziada have conducted a workshop on “Honor Killing of women”. Professionals attended it from GCMHP and women from Women Empowerment Project.

- On 9th September 1997, Mr. Ahmad Abu Tawahina, Dr. Tyser Diad have conducted a study day about personality and social rearing was held at GCMHP.

- On 6th November 1997, Mr Saber El Neirab and Mr Ziad Awad participated in a workshop entitled” the counseling” in AL Ahli Hospital in Gaza.

- On 13 November 1997 Human Rights Team conducted a workshop entitles “Case management of VHR cases”. All GCMHP participated in this workshop.

- On 28th December, Mr. Ahmad Abu Tawahina participated in a workshop in Palestinian Red Crescent entitled ”Networking among NGO in Gaza Strip”.

- On 28th December, Dr. Adel Aziz Thabet participated in a workshop in Palestinian Ministry of Health entitled ”Health five years plan” in Gaza.
