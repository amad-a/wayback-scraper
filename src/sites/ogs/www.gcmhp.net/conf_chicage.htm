Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj Participates in the University of Chicago Conference on:
    “50 Years after 1948: Human Rights in Israel and Palestine”

    The Center for International Studies in the University of Chicago held a conference on “50 Years after 1948: Human Rights in Israel and Palestine” last May. Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj presented a paper on “The Nakba of Palestine and Human Rights” in which he talked about the establishment of Israel at the expense of the Palestinian people, who were systematically uprooted. He also pointed to Israel’s policies of humiliation, torture, and destruction of the Palestinian individual and society.
