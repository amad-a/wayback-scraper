Dr. Rami Heilbornn
Dr. Heilbronn was born in Haifa. For many years, he has been working for justice and the rights of the Palestinian people. He is a friend of the GCMHP since it started and he is a member of its Advisory Board. He works as a staff supervisor in the Medical Foundation for the Care of Victims of Torture and Organized Violence in the UK.
