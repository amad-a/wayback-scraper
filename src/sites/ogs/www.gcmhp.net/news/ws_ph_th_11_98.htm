Workshop on
"The Role of Physiotherapy in Rehabilitating Victims of Torture"
 
 
On 8 November, the Physiotherapy Department at GCMHP organized a workshop on "The Role of Physiotherapy in Rehabilitating Victims of Torture" in coordination with the Rehabilitation and Research Council for Victims of Torture (RCT) in Denmark.  Dr. Lone Salem, Senior Physiotherapist at RCT, participated in the workshop where she gave a lecture on the "Sequel of Torture and the Best Physiotherapeutic Techniques to be used in Treatment".

The 60 participants of the workshop worked in groups to come up with recommedations on the best techniques to be used in rehabilitating sample cases of torture victims presented by Mr. Ali El-Bahri, GCMHP Physiotherapist.
