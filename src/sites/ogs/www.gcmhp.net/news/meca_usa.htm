A Delegation from
the Middle East Children’s Alliance (USA) visits GCMHP
 

Twenty professionals, activists, and university academics who are members of the Middle East Children’s Alliance visited GCMHP on 21 June 1998.  The group met Mr. Ahmad Abu Tawahina (Deputy Director), Mr. Husam El-Nounou (PR Coordinator), and Dr. Khalid Dahalan (Children’s Committee).  The guests listened to a presentation about the Program’s different activities and the general situation in the Gaza Strip after Oslo.  After the presentation, the GCMHP staff members answered the questions of the guests.
