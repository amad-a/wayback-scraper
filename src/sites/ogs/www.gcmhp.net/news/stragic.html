GCMHP Holds a Workshop on
"Strategic Planning for the Next 5 Years"

    On 28-30 April 1999, GCMHP held a workshop on "Strategic Planning for the Next 5 Years".  The workshop was organized in order to lay the basis for GCMHP's plans till 2004.
    Over the three-day event, all the activities, projects, and departments were discussed in detail.  In addition, the participants discussed all the outstanding professional, administrative, and financial problems and laid down plans in order to overcome and resolve them.
    Moreover, the workshop included establishing a vision and defining the strategic approach through which services, training, research should be conducted.
    Dr. Khaliel Nijim of Bisan Center for Research and Development facilitated the workshop in which 20 GCMHP department heads, project officers, administrators, and volunteers participated.
