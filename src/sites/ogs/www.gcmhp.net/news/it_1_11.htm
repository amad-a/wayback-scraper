GCMHP Presents a Paper
on Mental Health in an International Conference in Italy
 
 
Dr. Abdel Aziz Thabit, Projects Manager at GCMHP, participated in the First International Mental Health Conference in Trieste (Italy) organized by the Italian Ministry of Health.  Dr. Thabit presented a paper on the "Philosophy, Work, and Vision of GCMHP" in which he discussed the pioneer experience of GCMHP in the provision of community mental health services to the Palestinian people.  The paper also stressed the importance of developing day care centers and closing mental asylums.  In addition, Dr. Thabit visited a number of Italian community mental health centers to get acquainted with the most recent advancements and methods utilized, which aim at replacing Psychiatric hospitals with community mental health centers.
