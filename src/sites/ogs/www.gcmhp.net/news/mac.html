Dr. Mac Mullin Teaches the "The second part of the Counseling and Communication Skills  " course in the  Diploma

  • From 17-21 April 1999 Dr. Colin MacMullin, senior lecturer in Special Education in Flinders University Australia, participated in teaching the second part of the Counseling and Communication Skills course. The local trainer Abdel Hamid Afana assisted Dr. MacMullin in the course. The course was presented to the third group of diploma students.
