2.2 Indictors of the quality of the treatment

2.2.1 Outcome of the treatment:

From the data we have, there was 7 cases of indirect VHR closed their files, 3 direct VHR were controlled, 11 cases of direct VHR cured, 24 cases of direct VHR were improved, 27 of indirect VHR improved, 6 cases of direct VHR were new, 13 cases of indirect VHR were new, one case of indirect VHR relapsed and one case of indirect VHR referred.
