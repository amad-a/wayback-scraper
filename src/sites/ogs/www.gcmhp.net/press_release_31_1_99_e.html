Press Release

Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj stated that it is unacceptable that some Palestinian citizens remain imprisoned because of their political affiliations, without charges or trial, and that they are being deprived of their most basic rights.  He said that these detentions are a violation of the respect of human rights as specified by the Palestinian Basic Law and Declaration of Independence, as well as by all international declarations on human rights, which guarantee the freedom of expression and affiliation.

Dr. El-Sarraj called on the Palestinian Authority to immediately release the political prisoners, some of whom are on hunger strike in Juneid prison.  He also implored the Palestinian Authority to implement the decisions of the Palestinian Legislative Council and rulings of the judiciary.  He also stated that the danger in the Palestinian Authorityâ€™s behavior lies in the disregard for democracy and human rights in order to please the whims of the Israeli Government, which has abandoned its commitments towards the Palestinian Authority and the peace process.
