Kanafani, Ghassan
( - 1972)

Writer and journalist from Acre. Editor of al-Hadaf. Member of the Political Bureau of PFLP and its spokesperson. Published PFLP newspapers Al-Ray (the Opinion).

Author of many books, such as Men in the Sun and Other Palestinian Stories (London: Heinemann, 1978).

Killed by a car bomb on 8 July, 1972 in Beirut.
