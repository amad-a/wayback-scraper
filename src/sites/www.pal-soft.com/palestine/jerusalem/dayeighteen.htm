Day Eighteen
 
  
Day 18 : Sunday 15.10.2000
Israeli  occupation  troops  continued to impose a hermetic closure on
most  Palestinian towns and villages throughout the West Bank and Gaza
Strip.
Hizbullah  had  captured  an  Israeli  colonel,  in  a unique security
operation  .  An  Israeli spokesman reported that the a former colonel
might have been abducted by Hizbullah from Europe.
Thousands  of Palestinians took to the streets in a huge demonstration
in Gaza Strip and number of cities in the West Bank protesting against
the  PNA  decision  to participate in Sharm el-Sheikh summit scheduled
for Monday.
