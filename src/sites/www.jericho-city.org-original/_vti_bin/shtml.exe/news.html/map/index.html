Jericho Municipality News


Jericho Municipality has been Awarded the Certificate of ISO 9002 which is built on Total Quality Management

 

From our faith in improvement and continuos development of the services that are provided to the residents of Jericho by using modern administrative methods under the umbrella of ISO 9002 which is built on TQM started to take steps into implementing the idea and working it out. at the beginning of 1999 the idea was formulated and actions took place, in June. 1999 the First official contact was made with the Industrial Details Company “ a specialized company in the ISO issues” .

Many training courses were made to the Departments Heads, officials and workers, they attended lots of sessions, and many paper work was made, new plans and new procedures depending on ISO 9002 & the concept of TQM .

On Thursday May 18. 2000 The Final auditing on implementing the procedures and instructions of ISO was made in all Departments and Sections by a specialized Committee formed from SGS International for the ISO then the Committee recommended that Jericho Municipality should gain the Certificate of the ISO 9002.


Jericho Municipality Sings an Agreement with UNDP

On Saturday April 29. 2000 and Under the Patronage of his Excellency Dr. Saeb Ereqat the Minister of Local Government An agreement between the Municipality of Jericho and the United Nations Development Program has been signed stating that an amount of $8,82,000 is to be donated for the infrastructure in the Oldest City in the World, building new roads, streets maintenance. Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr the Mayor of Jericho and Mr. Timothy Rothermel the Special Representative of the UNDP signed the Agreement.

Dr. Ereqat gave his sincere thanks and appreciation to the Japanese Government as the donor Government and to the UNDP for their continuous efforts to support those important projects in Palestine.

A Delegation represents UNISCO visits Jericho

A Delegation from the UNISCO visited Jericho Municipality on March 29. 2000. the Delegation was constituted of Mrs. Costanza Farina, Head of UNISCO Coordination office in Ramallah, and Mr. Omar Masalhah the Director General of Palestinian People Assistance Unit in UNICEF, they visited the Municipality and met with the Mayor Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr, after the meeting the Delegation visited some of the tourist and old sites and ruins in Jericho including to some newly implemented projects.

 

Mayor of Mississauga of Canada visits Jericho

 

On April 22. 2000. The Mayor of Mississauga of Canada Mrs. Hazel McCallion accompanied by Mr. Bashar Saleh who represents the Palestine House Association in Canada and Mr. Shaher Sheeban who represents the Cooperation and Planning Ministry visited the Municipality , they were welcomed by the Mayor of Jericho Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr. Mr. Mohammed Abdel-Ruhman the Head of Public Relation Section and Mr. Basil Hijazi the Head of the Technical Department. They had some explanation of the Municipality role and needs then had a tour in the Oldest City in the World.

 

Mayor of Milano visits Jericho

A Delegation from Milano in Italy constitutes Mr. Masimo de Crolies, Mayor of Milano. Mr. Franko kalamida Deputy President of the City Council. some other members of Milano City Council, the Delegation was accompanied by Mr. Hani Jaber the Palestinian Representative in Northern Italy, They were welcomed by Dr. Ali Huseini the Acting Mayor and a member of Jericho City Council, the Delegation got some explanations about the Municipality and the City of Jericho including the possibilities of making Twinning Agreements, then they had a tour in the City.

 

An Israeli Tourist Delegation visits Jericho

A number of 40 members of an Israeli Delegation visited the Municipality and were welcomed by Mr. Mohammed Abdel-Ruhman the Head of Public Relations Section in the Municipality and they got some ideas about the Municipality role in the City, the Delegation showed their prodigy and were so thankful for the great reception they received.

The Municipality receives a copy of the book “Jericho...the trip of a day and 10,000 years”.

In a special ceremony in the Office of the Mayor in which Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr Mayor of Jericho received a copy of the new book “ Jericho...a Trip of a day and 10,000 years” the book was rewarded to Jericho Municipality by Mrs. Jihan El-Hilou, Director General of Tamer Educational Association who published the book.

A French Delegation visits Jericho

 

A French delegation visited Jericho on May 11. 2000 in order to coordinate for the visit of French Delegation of 40 Mayors from various Municipalities in France which is due on June 1.2000. the Delegation was Mr. Erwan Huchet and his assistant including Mr. Abdel-Salam Abu Rmeileh from the Association Regional Pays de Loire-Gaza-Jerusalem.
