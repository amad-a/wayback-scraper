A Delegation from the Italian NGO "Salaam Children of the Olive Tree"
visit GCMHP

On 11 August a delegation from "Salaam Children of the Olive Tree", an Italian NGO, visited GCMHP. The guests met Mr. Husam El-Nounou, the Public Relations Coordinator, who gave them a detailed presentation of the different activities of the Program and its various projects. Mr.El-Nounou also informed the delegation of GCMHP's future plans in the fields of therapy, rehabilitation, training, and research. Moreover, he explained the psychosocial effects of the peace process and Israeli policies on the Palestinian people in the Gaza Strip.
The guests expressed their solidarity with the Palestinian people in light of the economic and political hardships that they face.
"Salaam Children of the Olive Tree" had previously contributed to GCMHP's activities in rehabilitating children and solving their psychological and social problems. In addition, this organization also assists in resolving some children's economic problems through adopting some of them.
