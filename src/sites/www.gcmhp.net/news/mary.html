Dr. Maryanne Loughry Teaches the part one of the "Counseling and Communication Skills" course in the  Diploma

  • From 10-14 April 1999, Dr. Maryanne Loughry, Pedro Arrupe Tutor at the University of Oxford, participated in teaching part one of the Counseling and Communication Skills course to the third group of Diploma students.  Dr. Loughry was accompanied by the local trainer Abdel Hamid Afana.
