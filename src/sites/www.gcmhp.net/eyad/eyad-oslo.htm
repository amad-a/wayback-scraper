International Seminar on
"Violence in Algeria and the International Community"

Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Director of GCMHP, participated in the International Seminar on "Violence in Algeria and the International Community" in Oslo, organized by Amnesty International Norway and the Norwegian Institute of Human Rights.  Dr. El-Sarraj addressed the topic of "Victims of Violence/Torture and Their Need of After-care" in this three-day seminar which hosts numerous European and Arab human rights professionals.
