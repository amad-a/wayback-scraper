Palestinian Poets

Walid Khazindar 

Khazindar was born in 1950 in Gaza City. He obtained a law degree in Beirut in 1978. He is considered one of the best Palestinian poets, his poetry is characterised by metaphoric originality and a novel thematic approach unprecedented in Arabic poetry. He was awarded in 1997 the first Palestine Prize for Poetry.
