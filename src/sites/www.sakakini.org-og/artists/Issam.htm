Palestinian Visual Artists

Issam Bader 

Bader is a ceramist and painter born in 1948 in Hebron. He graduated from the Baghdad fine arts academy, and obtained a Masters in Ceramics from the Tbilisi Academy of Fine Arts in the Soviet Union. He has held 10 personal exhibits, and has participated in a number of group exhibits in Palestine, Cairo, and Tokyo. He runs the Gallery 79 in Ramallah, and is an adviser at the ministry of culture.
