PLACES TO VISIT

Historical and Religious Sites    
Desert Monasteries


In and around Bethlehem a number of exciting sites are waiting to be discovered: The Church of Nativity, one of the oldest churches in the world, built by St. Helena in 334 above the Grotto of the Nativity; the Shepherds' Field in Beit-Sahour, where the Angel announced the Good News to the Shepherds; Solomon's Pools with the Ottoman Fortress, which provided Bethlehem and Jerusalem with water throughout the past 2000 years and the Desert Monasteries which are the living witnesses of early Christianity.
