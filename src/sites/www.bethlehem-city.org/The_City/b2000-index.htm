Project News

The Bethlehem 2000 project has been launched in order to commemorate the third millennium of the birth of Jesus Christ. It provided an opportunity to highlight, some of the issues of culture, tourism and economy in Bethlehem. The project covered different areas of the town which are vital to its development: rehabilitation of the street facades and squares of the old city, rehabilitation of the market-place, redesign and construction of? the Nativity Square, identifying pedestrian zones, parking areas and construction of the Peace Center and a Bus Station.
