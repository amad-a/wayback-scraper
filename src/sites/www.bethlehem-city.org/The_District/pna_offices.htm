The Bethlehem Governorate is made up of several departments administered by qualified personnel. At the top of the hierarchy is the governor who assumes the overall responsibility of supervising the work not only of his governorate but also of the various government departments in the Bethlehem District. The main departments of the Bethlehem Governorate comprise the following: Administration, Planning and Projects, Legal Affairs Municipality Affairs, Public Relations, Political Parties, Media Activities and Services. These departments include sections entrusted with research, legislation, translation and the drawing up of contracts. A section dealing with refugees and tribal issues has been set up. The Governor has a consultative and an executive council. He has more than one assistant and more than one advisor.

Office of the Ministry of Local Government

The office of the Ministry of Local Government in the Bethlehem Governorate supervises municipal and village councils and follows up the performance of these councils especially in the following areas: administrative affairs, engineering and technical affairs and city and village planning, financial affairs and budgets. The director general of local government in the Governorate heads the central committee for planning and building.

The office of the Ministery in the Bethlehem Governorate directly supervises ten municipalities, 21 village councils and 5 projects committees that represent the 140,000 inhabitants of Bethlehem district. The Ministery also supervises he management of the Water and Sewerage Authority of the Bethlehem , Beit-Sahour and Beit Jala Municipalities. The Ministery also encourages municipalities and local bodies to establish effective relations with several foreign and Arab cities.

 

Office of the Ministry of Culture

The Directorate of Culture seeks to supervise cultural activities in the district, and museums in the Bethlehem Governorate. In cooperation with the Women's Union and with Japanese funding, it established the Contemporary Art Museum in Bethlehem in addition to taking interest in other museums in the Governorate. In addition the office organizes and sponsors different activities for writers, poets , artists and musicians. The directorate of the culture supports all the cultural activities held in the Govenorate, mainly, summer entertainment camps and organizing cultural contests in literature, art and music, as well as youth creativity, children's drawings and women creativity. art workshops and sponsors theatrical activities During the past two years the Ministery of Culture established six libraries for children in the Bethlehem and provided them with furniture, books, computers and other facilities. It also trained specialized technical cadres to work in them. It also granted $3000 to each library for the infra-structure.

The working team in the office consists of the office director, Public Relations Director, Director of activities, Cultural Centers official, and Woman and Child officer
