Palestinian Visual Artists

 George Anastas 

Anastas is the most well known Palestinian sculptor working today. Born in Bethlehem in 1952, he studied fine arts at the Iniaza Institute in Italy. He also has a BA in fine arts and has participated in a number of group exhibits in Jerusalem, Gaza City and Italy. He is a member of the Yabous artists group.
