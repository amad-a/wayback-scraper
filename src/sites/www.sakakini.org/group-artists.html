Group Exhibit of Contemporary Palestinian Artists

The Sakakini exhibited recent works by Diaspora and local artists; among them: Ceramist Vera Tamari from Ramallah, from Nazareth Sharif Waked, from Japan Vladimir Tamari, from the US Kamal Boullata and Samia Halaby, and from Jordan Suha Shoman.

Suha Shoman was born in 1944 in Jerusalem. She is also the head of the Darat El Funun arts center in Amman.
