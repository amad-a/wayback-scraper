NEW ! August, 1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

* Opening of a comprehensive exhibit of contemporary Palestinian artists, as part of the Ramallah Festival. Please check the Exhibits section.

* Due to the closure imposed by the Israeli authorities on Palestinian cities, the symposium due to be organized on the life & legacy of Khalil Sakakini on the occasion of the 44th anniversary of his death was canceled.

* Lecture by Dr. André Mazawi from Tel Aviv University & the League of Arab Jaffa Affairs on the history of the city of Jaffa, from Napoleon's conquest to the 1948 exodus, and current Israeli policies. The play was adapted and directed by Yacoub Ismail, its text based on Syrian playwright's text "Hal el Dunia".
