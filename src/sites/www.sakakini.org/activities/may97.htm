Current Activities

 May, 1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

A lecture by Dr. Nazmi Ju’beh from Bir-Zeit University on the historical architectural development of the City of Hebron. The lecture was co-sponsored by Riwaq.

A poetry reading by poet Faycal Qarqati, reading from his latest, & third poetry collection: Sajdat el Hinna’, (Henna Prostration) .

Khaled Hourani exhibit.

Screening of the 1984 film version of the Opera Carmen

Screening of a documentary on the hand made Japanese Washi paper.

Singer-composer Mustapha Kurd and his ensemble of Palestinian and German musicians, in a concert of Palestinian poetry put to music by M. Kurd.

A lecture by visiting Texas University archeology professor, Dr. Denise Schmandt-Besserat on her discoveries of the origins of writing in the Sumerian civilization.
