Palestinian Poets

Youssef Abd al -Aziz 

Abd al-Aziz was born in Beit Inan near Jerusalem in 1956, and graduated from the Arab University of Beirut in 1986. He lives in Jordan and has published several collections of poetry. His experimentalist poems are considered to be among the best in Palestinipoetry.
