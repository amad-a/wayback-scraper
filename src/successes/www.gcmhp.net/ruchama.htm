Dr. Ruchama Marton 
Born in Jerusalem, Dr. Marton is a psychiatrist who, since 1960, has worked with a variety of groups struggling for peace with their Palestinian neighbors. She has worked with both the committee for Birzeit University and the committee against the war in Lebanon. In January 1988, she came to Gaza and visited Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj and Dr. Haider Abdel Shafi and afterwards established the Israeli-Palestinian Physicians for Human Rights. The organization started with 12 persons, but now has more than 35. Recently she got a scholarship to Harvard University for peace studies. She is also a member of the GCMHP Board of Advisors.
