The Public Committee Against Torture in Israel

Ms. Hannah Friedman received the award on behalf of the organization. A group of Israeli human rights activists founded the Public Committee against Torture in Israel. They call for the end of using torture in Israel. Moreover, the PCAT support the basic human rights of the Palestinian people and have represented many Palestinian political prisoners is Israeli courts. Moreover, the PCAT advocates a two-state solution for the Palestinian-Israeli conflict.
