Ms. Lea Tzemel
Ms. Tsemel is a lawyer who was born in Haifa. She became active in groups struggling for a two-state solution in the early seventies. She believes that security detainees are fighters for their independence and against the occupation and not terrorists. She rendered legal services to the Palestinian prisoners in Israeli prisons. She has been a member of all committees against the deportation decisions taken against Palestinians.
