A Delegation from Geha Hospital in Israel visits GCMHP

On 25 May 1999, a 20-member delegation from Geha hospital in Israel visited GCMHP.  The delegation consisted of physicians, psychologists, social workers, and nurses.  They met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj who talked to them about the activities of GCMHP and its different projects.  He also answered their questions that centered on the psychosocial problems of the Palestinians in the Gaza Strip.
