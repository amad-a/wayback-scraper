A Delegation from the European Federation for Psychoanalysis
Visits GCMHP
 

A delegation of 12 psychoanalysts from the European Federation of Psychoanalysis visited GCMHP on 27 August to know more about its activities and discuss the possibilities of mutual cooperation.  The group met Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana, Director of Training and Education, and Mr. Husam El-Nounou, Public Relations Coordinator, who gave them details about the different projects and activities of GCMHP and accompanied them on a tour to the Egyptian border and Beach Camp.

The delegation investigated the possibilities of cooperation between the Federation and GCMHP in the fields of training, supervision, and research.
