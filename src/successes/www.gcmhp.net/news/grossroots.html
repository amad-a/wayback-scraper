A Delegation from Grassroots International visits GCMHP

On 22 February 1999, a delegation from Grassroots International visited GCMHP as part of the ongoing cooperation between the two organizations.  The visiting delegation consisted of Mr. Kevin Murray, the Executive Director, Ms. Sona Bari, the Communications Coordinator, and Ms. Nuhad Jamal.  The guests met GCMHP Deputy Director, Mr. Ahmad Abu Tawahina, and GCMHP Project Officer, Ms. Bradley Brigham.  They discussed the various activities conducted by GCMHP over the past year and the development in the work of the Program.  In addition, the two parties discussed the ongoing coordination between the two organizations, and the methods of enhancing the ties.

The visitors then visited the GCMHP clinic in Jabalia, which Grassroots International funds.  They met the medical team and staff in the clinic.  At the end of their visit, the delegation toured Jabalia camp.
