An American Delegation Visits GCMHP
 
 
On 2 November, a delegation of American university students visited the Gaza Community Mental Health Program.  The visit, through the Middle East Council of Churches, is part of the students' education to get them acquainted with the different facets of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.

The delegation met Mr. Husam El-Nounou, PR Coordinator, who put the guests in light of the different activities of the Program and the difficult living conditions the Palestinian people live in.  In addition, Mr. El-Nounou explained to the visitors the various problems that affect the Palestinian people.
