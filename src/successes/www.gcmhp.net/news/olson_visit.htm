Dr. John Olson from Oslo University Visits GCMHP
 
 
Dr. John Olson from Oslo University visited the Training and Education Department at GCMHP from 6-11 November.  Dr. Olson taught the "Trauma and Stress" module to the students in the Post-Graduate Diploma in Community Mental Health.
