A Delegation of European Parliamentarians Meets
Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj
 

On 22 March, A group of European parliamentarians from the Party of European Socialists visited the Programme, and met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj. Mr. Hannes Swoboda headed the delegation that also included Mr. Y. Roubatis, Mr. Jorn Donner, Mr. M. Hindley, Mr. Guntes Luttge, Mr. R. Speciale, and Mrs. Terron Cusi.
 
 

Dr. El-Sarraj briefed the delegation on the general socio-economic and political situation in the Palestinian areas in light of the deteriorating conditions and stalemate in the peace process. He also informed the guests of the improvements in the human rights situation in the Palestinian areas, while stressing the need to have the combined local and European efforts in confronting the remaining human rights violations. In addition, Dr. El-Sarraj explained the work of the Palestinian Independent Commission for Citizens’ Rights, its relationship with the Palestinian Authority (PA), and its achievements in the past three years. Finally, Dr. El-Sarraj thanked the European Community for its continuous aid to the Palestinian people, and called for a greater European role in the peace process.
 

The members of the delegation expressed the importance of the issue of preserving the integrity of people’s human rights in Palestine, and indicated their support to local initiatives that work in this field. In addition, they stressed the importance of continuous contact in order to study possible cooperation and coordination between the European Parliament and Palestinian human rights organizations.
