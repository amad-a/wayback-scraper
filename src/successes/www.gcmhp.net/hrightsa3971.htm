Summary of Activities : 1st January 1997- 30th June 1997
  
 
Sixteen visits have been delivered to different institutions for the purpose of cooperation and networking. As a result some torture victims have been referred for rehabilitation in GCMHP clinics, but due to lack of well organized referral system in our data base system, the number of those cases were not documented. This issue well is tackled in the new data information system, which will be ready and implemented in 1998.
