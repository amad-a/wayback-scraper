Day Twinty Two
 
 
  
Day 22 : Thursday 19.10.2000

At  least  three Palestinians were killed and over 15 other injured in
two separate incidents in the West Bank.
In  the  West  Bank village of Beit-Ymar near Hebron, the flag-wrapped
body of Ibrahim Allame (25 Years old), who was killed by Israel fire ,
were  carried by number of Palestinians during a funeral procession in
the streets of the village. (Photo 1)
In  Hebron  , a huge Palestinian demonstration led to clashes with the
Israeli  army  officers  how  replay to the stones by live bullets and
teargas. (Photo 2)
In  Gaza  city, Palestinian women's shout anti-Israeli slogans as they
wave  Palestinian flags during a protest march near the Israeli Jewish
Netzarim settlement junction in southern Gaza city. (Photo 3)
Israeli  occupation army soldiers opened fire on Palestinians who were
trying to repulse Jewish settlers seeking to overrun the Joseph's tomb
in  heart  of  Nablus.  Zahi  Alarba  (35  year old), was shot dead by
Israeli  soldiers  stationed  atop  the  Ibal hill overlooking Nablus.
Israeli  settler  was  killed  and  four  others  were injured in this
clashes  ,  when the Palestinian police were defending the Palestinian
people in the site.
Five  Israeli  helicopter  gunships  bombarded  for over one hour with
rockets  and  heavy  machine  guns Asker Palestinian refugee camp near
Nablus, causing a number of casualties.
In  (Photo  4) , a Palestinian paramedic pushes a stretcher with a man
injured by Israel fire .
In  Bethlehem,  at  least  two  Palestinian  officers  were killed and
several others injured when a powerful blast rocked a building housing
the  Force-17,  the Presidential guards of PNA chairman Yasser Arafat.
The blast was likely caused by a gas leak.
