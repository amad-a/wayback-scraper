Day 14 : Wednesday 11.10.2000
In  Gaza  city  ,  during  a  rally,  a  huge  number  of  Palestinian
demostrators burn Israeli and American flag.
The  number  of  Palestinian  martyres  today  has  reached  four, the
martyres  are  Sami Salameh, 17, of Qalkilya, Karam Qanan, 19, of Khan
Younis, Saleh Uthman, 38 of Kufl Haris, near Nablus ( deliberately run
over  by  a settler jeep) and fourth unidentified Palestinian was also
martyred.
Israeli  helicopter  gunships continued to pound Palestinain towns and
villages  with heavy machinegun rounds, iflicting heavy causalties and
damage.  Settlers  mobs  protected by the Israeli army are attack Arab
home  in  number  of  villages in various parts of the West Bank, with
Palestinian repulsing them .
Israeli  occupation  authorities  sealed all the towns and villages in
the Occupied Territories and barring Palestinians from entering Alqods
(Jerusalem)   and  1948-Palestine  .  Israeli  authorities  have  also
cancelled all 45,000 Israeli work permits issued to Palestinians.
