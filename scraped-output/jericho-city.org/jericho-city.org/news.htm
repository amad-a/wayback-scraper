Jericho Municipality has been awarded the Total Quality Management Certificate ISO 9002

From our faith in improvement and continous development of the services that are provided to the residents of Jericho by using modern administrative procedures under the umbrella of the International High Quality the Municipality started to take steps into implementing the idea and working it out. at the beginning of 1999 the idea was formulated and actions took place, in June. 1999 the First official contact was made with the Industrial Details Company " a specialized company in the ISO issues". Many training courses were made to the Departments Heads, officials and workers, they attended lots of sessions, and many paper work was made, new plans and new procedures depending on the rules of Total Quality Management ISO 9002.

On Thursday May 18. 2000 The Final inspection on implementing the rules and instructions of the Total Quality Administrations was made in all Departments and Sections by a specialized Commitee formed from SGS International for the ISO and Total High Quality and then the Commitee recomended that Jericho Municipality should gain the Certificate of the ISO 9002.

An Italian delegation from the Union of Italian Municipalities visits the Municipality of Jericho
A delegation from the Union of Italian Municipalities visited the Municipality of Jericho on April 16th 2002. The delegation consisted of four members of the City Council of Pisa which is bound to the city of Jericho by a sisterhood agreement. The delegation was part of a larger group consisting of 39 members from different Italian municipalities who came for the purpose of visiting the Palestinian cities to show support and to express solidarity. Mr. Abdel- Kareem Sidr and the members of the City Council of Jericho welcomed the delegation and gave its members an explanation about the deteriorating economic situation in Jericho caused by besieging the city and isolating it. The delegation denounced the Israeli actions against Palestinians and conveyed the will of the Municipality of Pisa to fund some immediate as well as long term projects for the city of Jericho.

The Leader of the Norwegian Socialist Left Party visits the Municipality of Jericho
On March 17th 2002, the Leader of the Norwegian Socialist Left Party and member of the Committee of International Affairs visited the Municipality of Jericho accompanied by other members of the Committee of Foreign Affair in the Norwegian Parliament. The Mayor, Mr. Abdel Kareem Sidr, welcomed the delegation along with Mr. Sa'eb Nazeef, a member of the city Council. Mr. Basel Hijazi, the head of the Engineering Department, made a presentation showing the projects of the Municipality of Jericho which are funded by the grant donated by the Norwegian government. Mr. Sidr, Mr. Nazeef, and some of Municipality's staff from the Engineering and Public Relations Departments accompanied the delegation in a tour showing the impact of the closure on the economy of the city. In the end of her city, the Parliament member expressed her appreciation for the hospitality and passed her wishes for peace and prosperity; she also promised to work on supporting the Palestinian cause in the Norwegian Parliament.

(2-4-2002)

The Head of Gaza- Jerusalem Pays de Loir association visits the Municipality of Jericho

The Head of Gaza - Jerusalem Pays de Loir in France along two members of the association visited the Municipality of Jericho for the purpose of showing solidarity with the Palestinian people and with Jericho city which has been under siege for more than 15 months. During the meeting of the delegation with mayor, they discussed a number of projects binding the Municipality of Jericho with the association such as donating the Municipal library books and encyclopedias and presenting financial aids to some kids in Jericho.
