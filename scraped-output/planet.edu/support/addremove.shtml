    If you do not have Dial-up Networking already installed onto your Windows 95 machine; you will need to install this portion of your Operating System. You can check to see if this is already installed by double clicking on the My Computer icon, which should have a Dial-up Networking icon there. otherwise follow the directions below. Place your Windows 95 CD into your CD-ROM drive or have your installation disks handy.

  1. Place your Windows95 CD into your CD-ROM drive or have your installation disks handy.

  2. Click on START, SETTINGS, and then CONTROL PANEL.



  3. Double-click on ADD/REMOVE PROGRAMS.


  4. Click on the WINDOWS SETUP tab folder (along the top of the window)

  5. Click on COMMUNICATIONS, then DETAILS button.



  6. Make sure the DIAL-UP NETWORKING box is checked by clicking on the box.



  7. Click OK on all the menus back to the Control Panel.

  8. Windows95 will install files from the CD-ROM or ask you to insert the installation disks.
