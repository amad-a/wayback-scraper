Cat Stevens WWW Site

Former British pop music star Cat Stevens, who converted to Islam and is now know as Yusuf Islam. Cat Stevens performed one of the greatest disappearing acts in pop music history two decades ago, when he quit his day job to devote his life to Islam. Known for such folk hits as 'Moon Shadow' and 'Wild World', Stevens sold his rock star baubles, changed his name to Yusuf Islam and became Britain's best known Muslim.
