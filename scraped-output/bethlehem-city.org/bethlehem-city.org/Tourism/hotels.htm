Hotels

There are several hotels and guest houses in the Bethlehem Area. The majority are a few minutes walk from the Manger Square. Rooms with single, double, or three beds are available for US$30, 50, 70 per night. Breakfast is included. Accommodation in Bethlehem is under-priced in comparison to Jerusalem. Bethlehem is booked out at Christmas and Easter. At these times it is advisable to book well in advance, although you can usually find a bed somewhere less popular.
