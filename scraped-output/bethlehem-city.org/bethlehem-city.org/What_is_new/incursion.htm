March 12th, 2002

Statement by the Municipal Council of Bethlehem

            At dawn of Friday March 8th, the Israeli forces covered by tanks and helicopters invaded the cities of Bethlehem, Beit-Jala and Doha from several cores and penetrated into the Deheisha and Aida refugee camps in the district of Bethlehem.  The incursion came after the tyrannical rocket bombardment for four consecutive days to our PNA headquarters in Bethlehem using F 16 war planes the matter which led to total destruction of the security offices, severe damage to the surrounding houses, injury of innocent civilians and displacement of scores of families.  

            Until this minute, the Israeli tanks continue to surround and occupy the two refugee camps, the villages of Al-Khader, Artas, the towns of Doha, Beit-Jala and the western part of Bethlehem.  The random Israeli gun-fire and tank artillery in these areas killed eleven Palestinians including the head of the Yamama hospital in Bethlehem Dr. Ahamed No'man Al Khudari, injured over  fifty, and destroyed public and private  property.  The Vatican sponsored Bethlehem University was not spared by the Israeli bombardment.  Two guided missiles landed in its campus inflicting great damage to the classrooms and to the library.  

            The atmosphere inside Bethlehem is terrifying.  The town is totally besieged.  There is no way of entering or leaving the town.  Bethlehem is totally isolated from the outside world.  Shops, schools, universities and institutions are closed.  There is a big concern about food and medicine shortage not to mention the diffusion of fear in our peopleï¿½s hearts and especially children and women.  

            In this critical time, the Municipal Council of Bethlehem strongly condemns this flagrant aggression and confirms upon the right of our Palestinian people in freedom, self-determination and establishment of our own independent state on our national soil with East Jerusalem as its capital according to the Security Council resolutions and the international legitimacy.  It appeals to the Arab and the Islamic world, to the international community and to the United Nations organization to assume their historical responsibilities and to quickly and effectively intervene with the Israeli government to stop this savage attack and these unprecedented ugly massacres committed against an unarmed people who looks toward living in freedom and dignity in light of a just and comprehensive peace and asks for ceasing of the state organized terrorism and to immediately withdraw from our cities, villages and refugee camps.
