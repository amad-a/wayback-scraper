Economy

    Gaza's economy depends on agriculture and light industries. The major agricultural products are citrus, olives, dates, flowers, strawberries, and other vegetables and food products, plastics, construction materials, furniture, textile, garment and other traditional industries such as pottery, bamboo furniture, carpets, glass coloring, sea fishing and embroidery.
