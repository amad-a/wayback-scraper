Eng. Ayed Abu Ramadan
The Mayor's Assistant for Projects Coordination & Follow Up Affairs, Head of Projects Coordination & Follow Up Section

 

Responsibilities:

  • To follow up foreign and international relations and prepare twinship projects with different international municipalities.
  • To coordinate with donor parties in relation with the running and future projects.
  • To lead the project of building skills and prepare the strategic and investment plan of the municipality.
  • To coordinate with the ministries and local organizations.
  • To follow up the projects under implementation and prepare for them with the different departments of Gaza municipality.
  • To pay field visits to the projects under implementation.
  • To follow up the work of the new cultural centers of the municipality (the Public Library, the Holst Center, the Museum of Arts and Heritage, the Project of Qattan Library for Children, the Children's Happy Park, Qubbeh Children Center).
  • To submit proposals for administrative and technical development of the different sections of the municipality.
  • To document the projects by using computers and files.
  • To document training courses and coordinate future training courses.
  • To prepare a city map with sites of the projects.
  • To be a member of the technical assessment committees for tender .
  • To receive, transfer and follow up the complaints of the residents.
  • To complete the applications of the residents for cutting the asphalt.
