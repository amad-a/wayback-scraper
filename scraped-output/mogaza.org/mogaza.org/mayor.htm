Nasri M. Khayal
Mayor of Gaza City
 

  • Graduate of the American University of Cairo, B. A. degree in Economics.
  • Member of Administrative Board of the Agricultural Cooperative Society for Marketing Gaza Strip Citrus Fruits, Treasurer.
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Arab Company for Packing Citrus Fruits in Gaza Strip.
  • Ex-director of the Arab Bank in Gaza and ex-member of the Financial Committee of the Palestinian Red Crescent and other organizations.
  • Head of the Municipal Committee of Financial Affairs.
  • Head of the Municipal Committee of Claims and Project Judgment.
  • Head of the Municipal Committee of Supplies and Purchasing.
  • Head of the Municipal Committee of Lease and Rents.

 

 


Mr. Nasri M. Khayal
