General Administratio0n Department
Director: Mr. Mohammad S. Hillis

Responsibilities:

  • To appoint, promote, modify social cases, give vacations, terminate services, give loans, provide health insurance and give rewards.
  • To give all kinds of allowances, prepare competency annual reports, punishment, working hours ..., etc.
  • To implement the instructions of the Mayor in relation to daily work and other instructions related to work in general.
  • Public Services Office
  • Court Office
  • Officer of the General Out Issues
  • Officer of the Sports Activities

Public Services Office
Head of the Office: Waleed Khayal

Responsibilities:

  • To offer all kinds of municipality services to the public, receive all kinds of application (water, electricity, drainage, building licenses, vocations and advertisements, public sites and surveys), transfer the applications to the related sections, and follow them up until completed Residents can not go to any other section; they are limited to this office.
  • To collect fees from the residents for the services offered by the office.
  • To issue clearance papers to be presented to the land registry or tax offices.

Court Office
Head of the Office: Hani Khayyal

Responsibilities:

  • To decide on cases related to violating the laws and regulations.
  • To issue sentences related to intruding upon the streets.
  • To issue decisions to remove vending stalls, and to facilitate the traffic movement in the city streets.

Office of the General Out Issues
Officer of the General Out Issues: Intissar Al-Far

Responsibilities:

  • To send out the correspondence of the municipality sections.
  • To receive the correspondence of the municipality sections.
  • To assist the sections in photocopying their documents if they do not have photocopy machines.

Office of Sports Activities
Officer of Sports Activities: Sa'di Snunu

Responsibilities:

  • To activate all kinds of sports for all employees and to arrange internal matches among the sections.
  • To organize championships with institutions, municipalities and unions and to encourage sports among them.
  • To organize the administration of the Yarmuk Playground and prepare it for official celebrations and matches.
