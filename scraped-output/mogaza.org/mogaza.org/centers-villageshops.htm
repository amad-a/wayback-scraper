Workshops

  Rug House ..
       The resident rug maker displays his wonderful skill, using an authentic loom to produce the most beautiful rugs, bags, cushion covers and bedspreads, Variety of other woolen products are created by hand from both sheep and goats wool, which has been dyed by hand.

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

  Embroidery House ..
       Here the weaver can be seen at work producing hand made Majdalawi fabries, using a loom which originates from Ashquelon and is over 70 years old.

    This fabric is traditionally woven and embroidered by Palestinian women to make the most beautiful cushion covers, dresses, bags and table linen.

 

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

  Copper House ..
       Copper work is one of the less popular art forms in the Gaza Strip. The village provides a venue where copper antiques dating from a variety of historical periods are exhibited with the aim of promoting this craft and its beautiful products.

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

  Glass House ..
       The glass house in the village exhibits glass, which has been produced in Hebron and Nablus and painted in Gaza City. It is possible to request special orders. A variety of sizes, colors and designs to suit individual tastes.
