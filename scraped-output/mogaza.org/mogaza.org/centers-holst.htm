Holst Cultural Park
Overview
 

    In the memory of the late Norwegian foreign minister J.J. Holst and his contribution to building a long and lasting peace, Gaza Municipality has created the Holst Park with financial assistance of the Norwegian ministry of foreign affairs.

    Holst Park has been built for the children of Gaza (from 6 to 15). It is a place where the children can play and learn, where they can read, practice and play music, where they can develop their creativity through painting or acting, where they can increase their abilities in sports or computer through its different activities and training. Holst Park aims at strengthening the Palestinian identity as well as encouraging the discovery of the outside world by providing activities such as: Computer Training, Sport, Music, Library, Theatre and Arts & Crafts.
