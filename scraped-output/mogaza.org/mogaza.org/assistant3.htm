Nihad M. Almughany (Ph.D.)
Assistant to The Mayor for Urban Planning & Building Control

Responsibilities & Duties:

  • Drawing and follow-up the required development plans for the city of Gaza.
  • Coordinating efforts with the Dept. of planning to increase the proficiency of work performance.
  • Follow-up the public inquiries and make sure to facilitate citizens' applications.
  • Follow-up the performance of the following sections at the Municipality to achieve the highest standards:

       - Building License Sec.
       - Urban planning sec.
       - Surveying Sec.
       - Professions and Advertising Sec.
       - License Revenues Sec.
       - Streets Naming and Numbering Sec.
       - Public Inquiries Sec.

  • Monitoring the performance of the mentioned sections through the analysis of weekly, monthly and yearly reports.
  • Studying and recommending to the Mayor all training and technical needs of the employees of the Dept. of planning & Building Licenses.
  • Evaluation of the personnel's performance.
  • Building-up a cooperation network with relevant national and international institutions & organizations.
  • Any other duties committed by the Mayor.
