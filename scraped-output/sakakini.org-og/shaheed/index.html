Welcome to the online version of "100 SHAHEED-100 LIVES", the memorial exhibit to the fallen of the Aqsa Intifada. The exhibit showcases 100 personal photographs and objects belonging to the first 100 Shaheed (martyrs) of the Intifada which started on September 28th, 2001. A companion catalogue features the personal photographs, as well as photographs of the personal objects, and short biographies in Arabic and English.

The memorial opened at the Sakakini Centre in Ramallah on February 20th, 2001. Due to the siege laid to Palestinian towns it only travelled locally to Nablus & Nazareth. Internationally, it was held in March 2001 at Abu Dhabi's Arts Complex in the United Arab Emirates, in the summer months at Amman's Darat al Funoun/Shoman Foundation in Jordan; & in October, at Manama's Art Center in Bahrein.
