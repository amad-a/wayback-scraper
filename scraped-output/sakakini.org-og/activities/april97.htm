Current Activities

April, 1997:

The Sakakini hosted the following events:

-A lecture by Dr. Saleh Abdel Jawad from Bir-Zeit University on the sociological development of the City of El Bireh. The lecture was co-sponsored by Riwaq.

-A poetry reading by British poet Benjamin Zephaniah.

-Isabel de la Cruz photo exhibit.

-Screening of two films on Norwegian painters Edvard Munch and contemporary artist Frans Widerberg.

-A classical music recital featuring visiting American violist, and London Symphony Orchestra member Peter Sulski, and young Palestinian pianist Lara Harb, on her first public concert. The recital featured works by Bach, Schubert, Francois Francoeur, and contemporary American composer Paul Gregory.
