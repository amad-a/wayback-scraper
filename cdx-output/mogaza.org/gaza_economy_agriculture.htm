Economy
Agriculture

    Barley, wheat, and cotton farming and their export to both, the Arab and international, worlds are well known to Gaza. In addition, Gaza has plentiful of citrus farms such as lemons and oranges, which are considered amongst the best produce in the world. Bee-honey farms, tomatoes, potatoes, cucumbers, and several other fruits such as grapes, strawberry, figs, watermelons, and honeydew are also distinguished productions of the city.

    Gaza's agriculture depends mainly on underground water wells, and several studies for the desalination of the Mediterranean waters are underway throughout the Strip's municipalities. Furthermore, it is worth noting that the economy in Gaza depends considerably on flower farming and export to several countries around the world.
