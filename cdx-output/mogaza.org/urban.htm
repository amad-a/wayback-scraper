Department of Urban Planning & Building Licenses
Director: Eng. Fuad Al-Shurafa
 

Responsibilities:

   The Department of Urban Planning & Building License is responsible for all categories of planning, development and building license and control of the city of Gaza through the activities of eight specialized sections of the department as follows:

1. Building License Section:
Responsible for issuing building licenses according to planning laws and regulations.

2. Construction Follow- up Section:
Responsible for observing the construction of buildings, issue warnings for non-license construction and approval of providing services such as water, electricity and professions.

3. Urban Planning Section:
Responsible to prepare master plans, detailed plans, development plans and building regulations for the city of Gaza.

4. Surveying Section:
Responsible for all surveying works such as leveling, defining plot’s borders, description of existing situation for buildings and roads.

5. Professions & Advertising Section:
Responsible for issuing new license for all professions and industry in the city of Gaza. Observation for the professions & industry to make sure the implementation of the license conditions. Control of advertising means in the city.

6. Street Naming & Numbering Section:
Responsible for providing names for the streets of the city after the approval of the Municipal Council. It is also responsible for providing building numbers and plot numbers.

7. License Revenues Section:
Responsible for preparing all calculations of building license, penalty fees and deposits.
