    For sustainable prosperity of Gaza; For its remaining as a jewel, immunity and wealth for its people; And for continuing to carry-on the message of humanity for its people and neighbors; The Municipality of Gaza is striving to improve the living standards of the Citizens through the comprehensive development of infrastructure, economical, social and cultural aspects.

    With the cooperation and participation of its citizens, Gaza Municipality is working to improve the standards of the city and provide a clean and beautiful environment for its people, to uphold its heritage and values, and catching-up with scientific development in all aspects.

    In our work, we are driven by our strong belief in the importance of the participation of our citizens in decision-making, strengthening our relations with friendly nations, the cooperation with national and international institutions and the partnership between private and public sectors, and also in our belief in carrying-out our work, honestly and respectfully, in a democratic, transparent and efficient manner.

    The Municipality is committed to delivering its services through teamwork, custom focus and continuos improvement. In our Municipality, and in-order to achieve the set goal, we believe in respecting the working individuals, building their capacities and providing them with the appropriate work climate, which encourages creativity, innovation and dedication to their city.

Municipality of Gaza:

   There were a number of municipal councils in Gaza city starting from the Ottoman period in 1893, the British Mandate, the Egyptian administration and ending with the Israeli occupation.

   When the Palestinian National Authority took over, for the first time in the history of Gaza, President Yaser Arafat issued a decree to form a new municipal council to run the affairs of the city. At that time, the city was in a very bad condition with no infrastructure.


    Since the present council has taken charge of the city, it formed and established a number of new departments, sections and units to build the city on scientific, developed bases. As a result of the increase in construction, the number of the Municipality employees increased to 1,500, including 190 highly educated persons.

   These employees work in 11 departments which contain 45 sections. This enables the municipality to provide the different necessary services for the residents of the city and to increase its productive ability and give it an appropriate administrative, technical and financial credibility.
