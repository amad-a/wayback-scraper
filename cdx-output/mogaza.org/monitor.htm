The General Inspection Department
Director: Maher Abul Gumbuz


Responsibilities:

  • To inspect the work of the different sections, units and departments of Gaza municipality.
  • To define the reasons for faults and deficiencies and the persons involved in them.
  • To submit suggestions in coordination with the departments, sections and units under inspection on reforming the faults and deficiencies and preventing their future occurrence.
  • To develop the systems where deficiencies or deviation might occur in accordance with the municipality regulations.
  • To provide reports on the inspection and its results.
  • To follow up the remedy of the deficiencies revealed in the reports of the General Inspection Committee and the Ministry of Local Governments.
  • To inform the Mayor about the remedy of the deficiencies stated in the report.
  • To attend to the complaints and follow them up.
  • To participate in the different committees of the inspection members in accordance with the instructions of the Mayor.
  • To carry out any duties requested by the Mayor.
  • To work on decreasing negative struggle and disagreement among the departments and sections.
  • To work on developing procedural methods in the different departments of the municipality in order to improve the services and their efficiency.
