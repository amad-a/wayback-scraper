Mohammad Akram Hillis
General Director of the Municipality,
 The Mayor's Assistant for Personnel Affairs and Director of the Personnel Affairs Department

 

Responsibilities:

  • Full supervision on all the Municipality departments.
  • Appointment, promotion, modifying social status, vacations, terminating jobs, loans, health insurance, rewards.
  • All types of substitute, annual competency reports, punishment, work hours.
  • Implementing the instructions of the Mayor in relation to daily work, or any instructions related to work in general.
