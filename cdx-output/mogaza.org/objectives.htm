Objectives

The overall objective of the project is to enhance social and economic development in the area. The improvement of access to basic urban services constitutes its major output.. Specific objectives are:

1. Serve as a testimony of the friendship and twinning between Dunkerque and Gaza cities.
2. Create a public space to allow families and their children to spend their leisure time in safe and useful activities.
3. Find an outlet to the energy built up of the children due to living in heavily dense area.
4. Develop a sense of the public property and create the pre-requisites for a more well-balanced city.
5. Set a pilot project to be developed in other areas of the city.
6. Strengthen the capacity of the Municipality staff in administering and maintaining such projects
7. Provide a space for public gathering and community activities.
8. Create direct and indirect job opportunities.
9. Complement other projects in the neighborhood.
