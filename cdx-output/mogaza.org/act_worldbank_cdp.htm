Community Development Program "CDP"

    This program is a loan from the World Bank for financing small-scale projects. These projects have been implemented under supervision of the Palestinian Economic Council for Development and Reconstruction (PECDAR). This program aimed at developing the social and economic aspects as well as improving the environment and health. The estimated cost of executed projects under this program is 1,199,712 $.
