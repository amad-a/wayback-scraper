Department of Veterinary & Slaughter House
Director: Dr. Sayyed Al-Jadba

Responsibilities:

  • To receive the animals brought for slaughtering, observe the slaughtering process and to ensure sanitary conditions so that it is appropriate for human consumption and stamp them with the municipality stamp.
  • To destroy the meat which is not appropriate for human consumption using healthy methods and making sure that they do not reach the hands of people.
  • To have strict health inspection of the meat and its products offered for human consumption within the borders of Gaza city.
  • To decrease environmental pollution by preventing slaughtering animals outside the slaughter house and by disposing the remains in a sanitary way.
  • To apply the systems and regulations of the municipality.
  • To prepare the monthly reports on the work of the slaughter house and the reports on urgent issues.
  • To observe the cleanliness of the slaughter house and prepare it for the work of next day.
  • To contact other sections of the municipality so as to solve problems which might delay the work like sewage draining, water, electricity and purchases.
  • To do any other work which the director sees necessary for public interest.
  • To carry out campaigns to rid the city of stray dogs.
