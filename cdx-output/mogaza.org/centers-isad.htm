Is'ad Al-Tofola Park Center
Overview

 

   This center is driven by the belief that the ability to play and the right to play are crucial to the healthy development of all children. Only healthy children, children who grow up with anger, hatred, violence and fear, are children who can make a peace that will truly be lasting for decades. Palestinian children have had their childhood taken away from them. With Is'ad Al-Tofola Park Center, we hope to give the children back their childhood, and to provide them with  chance for a life filled with laughter, fun, healthy development and peace.

    This center is a result of the efforts, meetings, and workshops between the Gaza Municipality and the Welfare Association and many other social associations interested in the childhood sector in Palestine.

    The center represents a symbol of Palestinian culture and heritage, covering some of the different aspects of Palestinian life. The center was built by local experts.

    Similar centers exist in Nablus, El-Bireh, and Hebron, which shows organic relationship between the Palestinian cities.
