Department of Provisions & Storage
Director: Abdul Rahman Al-Kafarnah

Responsibilities:

To supervise the following sections of the department:

  • Storage Section
  • Purchasing Section
  • Contracts and Tenders Section

Storage Section
Head of the Section: Tal'at Al-Wheidi

Responsibilities:

  • To store and keep all materials and pieces of equipment needed for the municipality projects.
  • To provide the daily needs from the storage under official receipts.

Purchase Section
Head of the Section: Marwan Shawa

Responsibilities:

  • To receive all purchase orders from the different sections of the municipality.
  • To purchase all the needs of the municipality after following all of the official procedures such as preparing the tender, or the direct order and advertising in the newspapers.
  • To give the purchased materials to the storage of the municipality.

Contracts and Tenders Section
Head of the Section: Abdul Rahman Seyam

Responsibilities:

  • To advertise bids for tenders in newspapers.
  • To complete and follow up the required procedures like distributing tenders, opening the envelopes, making decisions on tenders, issuing assignment orders; preparing the proceedings of the project negotiations and preparing the contracts for the projects.
  • To prepare the payments of the contractors and follow up on the expenditure procedures with the financial department.
