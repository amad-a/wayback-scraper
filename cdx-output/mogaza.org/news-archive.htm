The first anniversary of the Intifada
   Under the patronage of the President Arafat, the State Information Center, the Ministry of health and the Municipality of Gaza have organized a festival for the first anniversary of the Intifada at 28th Sep. 2001. Many activities were conducted during the festival which was held in Rashad Shawa Cultural Center. The Participants have strongly condemned the brutal Israeli policy of the Israeli army against the Palestinian people.
 

Me and my mother in war, a theatre play organized by Holst Park
   The Holst Patk/ Municipality of Gaza, in cooperation with Theatre Day Production has organized many theatre shows for the play Me and my mother in war. A large crowd of children and families attended the shows. The play concentrated on the suffering of Palestinian children, especially during the recent Intifada.
 

Signing of a grant agreement with the Government of Japan
     On the 22nd of August, the Municipality of Gaza and the Embassy of Japan have signed a grant agreement according to which Japan will finance the project of procurement and installation of metal plates for houses numbers inside Gaza City. The project aims at improving services rendered by various Palestinian ministries and institutions to citizens of Gaza. The project will be managed by the Computer and Information Center of Gaza Municipality.
 

The Municipality of Gaza implements infrastructure projects in Gaza
   With the help of various International agencies, the Municipality has recently started implementing some important projects in Gaza City such as the sewage network in Al Zaitoon area (World Bank), Palestine street upgrading project (World Bank), development of various streets in the Beach Camp (Save the Children), and Port Saed street (European Community).
 

A twinning agreement between Gaza and Tromso
   On the 25th of July, and during the official visit of the Mayor of Tromso to the City of Gaza, a twinning agreement was signed between the Norwegian City Tromso and Gaza City. The two cities have mutually agreed on cooperating in various cultural and technical fields. A joint cultural project will be identified and implemented later by both cities. The Mayor of Gaza considered this agreement very important as it comes at a time where Gaza City needs an International support from all friendly peoples and cities around the world.
 

A coordination meeting of European cities twinned with Gaza
   On the 20th of July, the Municipality of Barcelona has hosted the first coordination meeting of European cities twinned with Gaza City. Dunkerque, Barcelona, Cascais, Turino and Gaza participated in this meeting. The European cities have stressed their support to Gaza people at this difficult time. Some common projects inside Gaza City were identified, and they will be subject for further development in order to help Gaza City. The next coordination meeting will be held during the month of November in Cascais, Portugal.
 

Implementation of phase II of Al Qubba Park
   In cooperation with the twin City of Dunkerque, Gaza Municipality has started implementing phase II of Al Qubba Park located in Shijaiya area in Gaza city. The Park will serve more than 40,000 inhabitants. It is worth mentioning that the Municipality is doing a considerable effort for increasing the green areas inside the city.

 

The inauguration of the Brotherhood Public Park
   On the 2nd of June, the Municipality of Gaza and the Pontifical Mission in Jerusalem have inaugurated the Brotherhood Public Park located between the Beach Refugees Camp and the Naser area. The Park was totally financed by George and Marie Doty through the Pontifical Mission in New York and it includes green areas, sport playground and an open amphitheatre. The neighborhood committee thanked Gaza Municipality and the Pontifical Mission for their great effort in implementing this project with a total budget of USD 600,000.
 

Gaza Municipality and CHF launch the program for environment improvement
   The Municipality of Gaza and the CHF have launched the USAID funded program for the environment improvement in Gaza City. With a total budget of USD 3,000,000, the program aims at cleaning and beautifying Gaza City as well as increasing the awareness of its citizens with respect to environment.
 

Holst Park organizes various summer cultural activities
   As part of the Norwegian funded cultural program for the year 2001, the Holst Park/ Municipality of Gaza has organized various activities for children and youth. One of these activities was first aid course done in cooperation with the Palestinian Red Crescent Society. The course focused on the scientific and technical aspects of first aid.
 

10 Years IN Mud
   The Arts and Crafts Village/ Municipality of Gaza has organized an exhibition - 10 years in mud- for the artist Solaiman Mansour. The exhibition included various works and sculptures. The Village has organized many successful exhibitions this year for both Palestinian and foreign artists.
 

Signing of a grant agreement with UNESCO
   On the 1st of June 2001, a grant agreement was signed between the Gaza Municipality and the UNESCO. According to this agreement, the Government of Saint Marino will provide a financial assistance to Issad Ettofola Park-Municipality of Gaza. The Mayor of Gaza has sent a letter of appreciation to the Ambassador of Saint Marino to UNESCO for their generous support. The assistance will help the Municipality in better implementing the cultural programs and services for the children of Gaza, especially at this difficult time where Gaza children have been deprived from their basic rights.
