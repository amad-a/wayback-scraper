An agreement between Gaza Municipality and fishermen association to promote the fishery sector
      Memorandum of Understanding has been signed between Gaza Municipality and fishermen association for developing the needed infrastructure such as the fishery facilities and port in addition to making some financial support to fishermen. The aim of this agreement is to promote the fishery sector and enhance the fishermen steadfastness in front of the current severe crisis they are facing. 13 Aug. 2002

The Municipality of Gaza implements infrastructure projects in Gaza city
In cooperation with PECDAR and various development agencies, the Municipality has recently started implementing infrastructure projects in Gaza City such as tiling Palestine Street. funded by World Bank with a total budget of US$ 245,000 and development of Khaled Al-Hasan St. funded by World Bank under the MIDP program with a total budget of US$ 200,000. 27 Aug.

The availability of raw materials has enabled the municipality to start implementing these projects, which had been impeded as a result of Israeli closure imposed on the Gaza Strip.

 

Signing Memorandum of Understanding with UNDP
On the 25th of August 2002, a memorandum of understanding has been signed between the Municipality of Gaza and UNDP for employment generation project. According to MOU, the German Development Cooperation / KFW will finance the development of Ejdaida neighborhood (Around Water Well 2) including road tiling, water and sewage networks with a total budget of US$ 200,000.

The Acting Mayor of Gaza has thanked the UNDP for their continued support to the Gaza Municipality. During the last 8 years, substantial progress has been made in infrastructure and rehabilitation sectors in Gaza City through the earlier and currently on-going projects implemented in cooperation with UNDP with a total amount of more than US$ 15 million. These projects have generated approximately 80,000 employment opportunities and contributed in alleviating the extreme hardship of the Palestinian people as a result of Israeli measures and closure policy. 25 Aug.

Meeting with the Neighborhood Committee of Beach Refugee Camp
Gaza Municipality held a meeting with the Neighborhood committee of Beach Refugee Camp to discuss the water supply shortage within the Northern Beach Camp in addition to other encountered problem such as insufficient lighting, solid waste problem and sanitation. 23 Aug.

 

Belgian municipal delegation visits Municipality of Gaza
A delegation representing various municipalities and civil organizations in Belgium visited Municipality of Gaza to express their solidarity with Palestinian people and discuss different issues and potential aspects of cooperation in the future. The acting mayor has presented the current difficult situation and the encountered problem that facing the municipality in the meanwhile. After the meeting, the Gaza City Film "The Phoenix Rises" has been shown in the main guest municipal hall then a press conference was held with various press agencies. 19 Aug.

Final ceremony of the third environment summer camp
In cooperation with UNICEF, the environment awareness section has organized the third environment summer camp for 15 days. The summer camp included cultural, theater, sports and recreational programs for participating children. It aimed to alleviate psychological pressure and suffering of Palestinian children and to encourage their active participation in the social life in order to avoid their complete alienation and depression. 18 Aug.

 

Inauguration of “Love Message“ exhibition for drawings made by Norwegian Children
On the 15th of August 2002 “Love message” exhibition was inaugurated at the Arts and Crafts Village. The exhibition includes 120 drawings made by Norwegian children from Twined City of Tromso expressing their solidarity with Palestinian children in this hard situation as result of Israeli occupation and measures imposed on Palestinian people. 16 Aug.

 

Aown Shawa name on one of the main streets in Gaza City
In recognition to the great achievements of the late mayor, Aown Shawa who passed away on the 30th of November 2001, leaving behind him a long record of achievements for Gaza City during his presidency from 1994 – 2001, the municipal council has decided to name one of the main streets (St. No. 8).
14 Aug.
