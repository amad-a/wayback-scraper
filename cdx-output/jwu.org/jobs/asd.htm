Jerusalem Water Undertaking

Performance Guide

 

Title: Director   

A 1

Department:  Administration

No. of People Supervised:

Responsible To:   General Manager

 

 
Job Description

Objective of Position:

Foster professional management practices, including performance-based results system and develops personnel to meet the needs of JWU. Provide prompt and effective services to JWU customers and contribute to revenue generation. Promote a positive image of JWU and community understanding of and interest in JWU.

Summary of Duties:

General Duties :

1.      Overall JWU Management Role: Assist the General Manager in developing and implementing JWU polices, procedures and systems. Undertake any duties requested by the General Manager. Participate in different committees and studies related to his department.

 

2.      Plan:  Recommend department long-range plans designed to meet JWU goals with budget required; accomplish work within the approved budget.

 

3.      Organize:  Maintain necessary communication and ensure good relations with appropriate external bodies and other departments.  Coordinate the activities of divisions. Ensure availability and make most efficient use of human resources, supplies and equipment.

 

4.      Lead:  Delegate authority and set goals. Manage and direct the overall activities of the Department to achieve goals. Make work assignments to staff according to skills and job responsibilities. 

 

5.      Control:  Monitor the performance of the department, ensuring the quality of work and conformance with standards, rules & regulations. Prepare and present reports for the information of the General Manager and the Board of Directors. Monitor performance of staff through regular meetings and work review.

 

6.      Staff Development: Manage by results, encouraging staff to achieve personal and professional goals.  Identify training needs.  Ensure safety of staff. Recommend annual staff raises according to performance.

 

Specific Duties :

 

       1.   Planing, Budgeting and Reporting: identifies desirable capital improvement and maintenance projects to improve the meters installations & maintenance, reading & collection activities; assist the Engineering Director in developing related studies and analysis. Develop, implement, prepare and analyze management reports to track the department performance.

 

   2.  Customer Services: Reviewing customer complaints to identify trends and areas for improvement. Act as a final decision-maker in resolving customer problems. Verifying that installations & maintenance are made according to schedule, and all meters have been read and all bills collected. Ensure the quick response to solve customer problems through his staff.

 

 

 

 

 

 

   3.  Public Relations & Administrative Services: Develop and maintain customer orientation culture through effective meter reading/collecting, customer problem-solving and enhanced procedures throughout JWU that are responsive to customer needs. Prompt a positive image of JWU and engender community understanding and interest in JWU, through effective customer service, positive public relations & community education activities.

 

 

 

   4.  Personnel and Human Resource Development: Develop, implement and maintain effective personnel policies, procedures and programs including confidential maintenance of employee records; Management-by-Results with periodic employee performance evaluation; a compensation system that rewards progress and initiative; and employee skill development through a Training and Development Program. Implement the agreed upon policies and procedures related to recruitment and retain of qualified staff, and update the organization structure & job descriptions according to the new conditions and circumstances through the related procedures. Develop, implement and maintain, with internal control procedures, a materials management system. Ensure of providing effective administrative support to JWU through office services files and records management security and communications services. Ensure maintenance of office equipment including office furniture, building, doors,  etc.

 

   5.  Record-Keeping: Ensure that the Administration records are kept in an orderly and effective fashion.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Internal Relationships and  Communication:

General Manager , department Directors and such others as appropriate

 

 

External Relationships and Communication:

Public , suppliers , customers and media
