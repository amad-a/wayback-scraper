The Municipality Delegation Concludes 

a Visit to United Arab Emirates

 

In order to strengthen the relationship with the Arab Nation , Palestine and Salfeet Communities living in the Diaspora, Salfeet Municipality Delegation visited recently the United Arab Emirates.

The delegation was headed by Dr. Shaher Ishtieh, Mayor of Salfeet. Mr. Jamal Mahboba the City Council Member, and Mr.Waleed Jawdat, Head of Accountancy participated in the visit. Salfeet Son Dr. Fathi AlZubeidi coordinated for the visit and took over the necessary important arrangements . The delegation was hosted by HRC Prince Hamdan Ben Rashed Al Maktoum. The delegation met His Excellency Merza AlSayegh, Prince Secretary. Both parties agreed, in principle, on establishing a cultural Islamic center named after HRC Prince Hamdan. The prince will , partially, cover building a basic school in Salfeet.

The delegation met also Mr. Sa’id Alkatabeh, the Charity Advisor of HRC Prince Zayed. It has been agreed to support youth facilities in Salfeet.

Later on , the delegation visited Arab Youth Forum and met the Arab and Palestinian Community in UAE. Dr. Ishtieh explained the situation in Palestine and Salfeet Governorate. He emphasized on the importance of having strong relationship between Palestine and Salfeet Communities in Diaspora with the mother land. He invited the community for investment in Salfeet, which is growing rapidly.

At the end of the meeting, the participants declared the establishment of Salfeet Community Association in UAE.

After Concluding the visit and return home, Dr. Ishteih sent messages to hosts expressing gratitude and appreciation. The delegation admired the high level of UAE achievements in culture, industry and organization.

The City Council and Salfeet citizens expressed their happiness for this visit and highly appreciated the hospitality.

 

Al – Zaytoona Complex:

Operation Start Point  

 

    Al – Zaytoona Complex has recently been actualized. This pioneering project, owned and implemented by the local contractor engineer Omar Islim , is aimed at meeting the increasing needs of Salfeet and its Governorate for such center . Many governmental  offices have moved to this complex as a headquarter  . Al-Quds Open University chose this complex for its Salfeet Center. Stores of medicine, food , restaurants, stationery , boutique and other services are conducting  their works in this complex. Finally , this complex consists of 8 floors with a total area amounting 8.000 m2 and is located in the western part of the new main commercial center  of Salfeet .

 

 

Al-Quds Open University:

A New Center In Salfeet

 

     Al-Quds Open University has recently opened its Salfeet Center in order to meet the local community need of higher education. For the first semester , 370 students joined the university . Most of the students are from Salfeet Governorate . Now they have the chance to locally receive their need of study in computer , economic , education , and other subjects .

    It is planned for Salfeet Center to take up the governorate students willing to pursue their university education and to give a priority for those eldest who could not  have university study earlier. It is expected for this Center to attract students from the Palestinian bordering towns inside the Green Line to study in as a result for being closer to their residence.

 

Land Confiscation Continues

 

     In spite of Peace Process and the opposite declaration after the victory of The Israeli Labor Party, the Israeli Occupation continues confiscating lands from Salfeet Governorate. The Israelis are seizing more of our lands next to the settlements.

        The Israeli Government has recently called for bids in order to build hundreds of additional apartments in Ariel Colony, the biggest Israeli Settlement in the Occupied Territories. Meanwhile, the work continues for enlarging and expanding Barqan Colony ; the biggest Israeli Industrial Settlement located in Salfeet Governorate.
