Salfeet Municipality

 

Mission Statements

 

Belonging- Equality- Development

 

1.            1.      Salfeet Municipality is a local authority providing the local community with the

                 necessary services. We respect laws and regulations issued by ministry of Local

                Government in the Palestinian National Authority, and work on applying laws and

                preventing violating them.

2.         2.       We organize and plan the urban center of the city and public facilities, and work on

                  expanding our services in order to include the nearby villages.

3.         3.       We aim at expanding and improving our services of electricity, water, solid waste,

                  sewage , and culture for Salfeet inhabitants, and organizing and managing the public

                 facilities under our responsibility for the benefit of the local inhabitants.

4.             4.     According to our decentralized management:

a-                              Each division is responsible for providing its services in its field according to     

                 specialization and in cooperation with the other division in a way that secure the

                 continuity of services and its future development.

b-                             We work hard in order to upgrade continuously the efficiency of the employees,

                 through courses and training in accordance with the local management and to

                secure efficient management.

5.          5.        The current interrelation is based on sincere belonging and positive competition and

                   motivation for the employees who are distinguished in performance and meet the

                  needs of citizens quickly according to the available capacity and in an organized way.

 

We work on developing the financial resources in a way that covers the cost in order to preserve service continuity and its future development.

We look forwards to actualizing our pioneering organization’s objectives depending on the cooperation of the local community  through perceiving the role of the organization in supplying them with services and increasing their confidence in it through involving them in important decisions and making use of their abilities and experience in reinforcing development.
