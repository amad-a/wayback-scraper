NICE IN SUMMER AND MODERATE IN WINTER..

* The City of Salfeet lies between the longitudes 32° 5 north and latitudes 35° 11 east, which gives it a good climate; hot and dry in summer and temperate rainy in winter.

* Winds: in general the direction of the winds is south west and north west, with average velocity of 12 km/h. Usually in spring and summer we observe the hot wind Khamaseen with sand and dust.

* Temperature: the location in the middle north of Palestine gives it moderate temperature degrees: between 36° in summer and 5? in winter.

* Humidity: the average humidity is 62 % and may reach 67 % during winter.

* Rainfall: it is concentrated during wintertime and reaches 660 mm/year and in some years more; such in as 1981/1982 when it reached 1.000 mm.

* Inhabitants: the actual number of inhabitants for the city is 8.000 and 70.000 for the governorate.
