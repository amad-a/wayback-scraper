* Salfeet Municipality is responsible for the area within its organizational borders. The municipality creates the necessary facilities and services for the city citizens. The City Council is the highest board in the municipality and is responsible for laying out the municipality policy and takes different resolutions regarding various local issues in the city and the organization itself.
