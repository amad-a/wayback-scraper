EAST OR WEST SALFEET IS THE BEST... ALWAYS.

* The City of Salfeet is connected with the other cities by 3 main roads:

a) The eastern entrance which connects the city with the main road Nablus-Ramallah of 8 km long. It is an old entrance, asphalted, 4 m wide and passing through the villages of Skaka and Yasoof.

b) The northern entrance which connects the city with the main road of Kufur Qasem – Jericho of 4 km long, newly-asphalted of 20m wide.

c) Southern entrance which connects the city with the Governorate of Ramallah of 10 km long and half-asphalted.
