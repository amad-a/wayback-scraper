Recently we heard from one person who wanted to write about Yaffaa and we had two requests to write about villages (one is ready!). Although we wanted to get done with towns first, we did not expect this level of apathy. We are accepting entries about villages (small, large, destroyed and still standing). The list below (scroll down) is followed by towns/ villages under promised construction (including those which are ready (those with active link)

Welcome to this newly forming, yet very important, corner.
In brief, this is a modest project that aims at compiling an electronic desk reference of Palestinian cities, towns and villages. Doing the work is a matter of belonging and/or an expression of pride in your identity. It is understood that not everyone grew up in their hometown and some never visited, yet they want to learn more. Whether you know about your hometown or not, YOU can still help others to help both you and many others. The least you can do is encourage others to participate even if it takes rewarding them or assisting at some phase (scan a picture, type those manuscripts or sponsor a student who can do a good job documenting a few cities).
How does it work? How can I help?
This project is divided into two phases:
First, we want to document the cities and towns whose names are below (hope none is missing).
Once this is over, or reasonably advanced, we'll take the second step and do the same for villages and the rest of population centers.
What is needed is the following (at least):
  • Location, land and topography
  • Historical background
  • Population (current stats) and economy
  • Education, health and recreation etc
  • Land marks, tourist attractions, heritage, religious sites, industry special and others
  • Optional: if possible, and where appropriate, pictures that are both clear and fast loading (30 - 40 Kb and nothing more)
  • References used: old books will be good for historical background, new ones or direct sources for current updates are equally important. Also, don't forget to include your name, e-mail and homepage (unless you prefer to stay anonymous)
To be part of this honor, please send e-mail indicating which city(ies)/ town(s) you want to work on. If more than one want to work on the same town, we can put you in touch to get to know each other and exchange info to compile a better entry. We are looking for 1000 - 2000 words per town. Two lines is not good and a thesis is too much. So, please pick your choice from the list below and go to the mailbox: enter your full e-mail address (if your username is xyz123 and you are with AOL, your e-mail address is xyz123@aol.com and anything else is wrong, no one can e-mail you if you say xyz123 only). Please enter KTH (for know thy homeland) in the subject and indicate when you expect to finish the work.
Example:
Name: so and so
E-mail: abc@example.com
subject: KTH
Message: I'd like to help with the KTH project. I picked ....... (town) and I hope to be done by ........ (date). If something goes wrong, I'll let you know.
