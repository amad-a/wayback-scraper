Tours and Travel Agencies

  People visit Palestine for various purposes: religious, business, academic, recreation or some combination of these. Some may have already taken care of travel arrangements to Palestine and just need accomodation and a host to show them around. Others may need full packages from A to Z or some custom plan for a family/group visit to various places.

  No matter what your needs are, there are travel or tour agencies that can meet your needs at minimum cost and maximum satisfaction. The agencies' links below are for your own convenience - a listing does not mean endorsement but we'll act fast if we learn of sloppy services by any of them.

  • See you in the Holy Land - Master Tours: offers tour packages to Palestine, Jordan, Lebanon and Syria
  • Guiding Star Ltd Tour company in Jerusalem. Also operates in Syria, Jordan, Lebanon and Egypt
  • United Travel - Jerusalem: tours in Palestine and nearby countries
  • Palestinian Association for Cultural Exchange (PACE): organizes local tours and research trips. Publishers of the book "Tour Guide to West Bank and Gaza"
  • Experience Palestine Tours by Jerusalem Hotel. Tours to different parts of the country plus hotel and other services
