  Al Abed Internet Cafe is one of the new cafe's opening in Ramallah. We found a nice setup with 10 machines running win98 with MS natural keyboards and 17" monitors. We were told that machines have advanced applicatons software installed with partial UPS support. All machines have zip drives and each is in a seperate booth providing a good degree of privacy. Al Abed serves hot drinks and is planning more in the future. The staff person we talked to was not sure of speed but it was reasonably fast. Smoking is allowed and a w.c. unit exists inside.
