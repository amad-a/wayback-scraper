  Carma Cyber Club (CCC) is one of the most populated Internet cafe's in Ramallah. It is situated in a nice building, close to the city center, with a view towards the coast. The space is equipped with 20 pcs running Windows 95, connected on a LAN to a high speed Internet connection. Al workstations are multimedia capable. 13 have 15" monitors and the rest have 17". All machines are supported by a UPS.
The space offers a selection of cold drinks and snacks. Customers' other needs of food or drinks can be secured by the cafe staff from nearby outlets. Smoking is currently allowed near all terminals and there is a restroom unit (W.C) inside.
The TV lounge and Al-Carma free e-mail are add-ons and not available everywhere.

Update: Interview with Mr. Hisham Zarour (CCC manager) about recent expansions at CCC and iCafe's in Palestine in general.
