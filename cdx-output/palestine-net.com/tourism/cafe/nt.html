Date compiled/revised by PN: Novermber 12, 1999
  NT Internet Cafe' is a quiet space on the 6th floor of Burj as-Saa'a building. There are 10 machines running Win98 with a 64K leased line connection to the Internet. All machines have 15" monitors and there is b/w and color printer. The relatively spacious NT office is protected by a UPS, allows smoking and promises a hot/cold drinks machine (in shipping phase). There is a W.C unit inside. There is no copier, digital camera or web cam at NT, but a nice view and a taxi office in front of the building may add value to this cafe'.
