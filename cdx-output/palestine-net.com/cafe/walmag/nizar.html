Mata yu3linuuna wafaata l 3arab? (Nizar Qabbaani)

Sorry to our non-Arabic readers. This is in Arabic and only in graphic format. I did take the liberty and delete a few sections. The poem is, I think, translated into English. As mentioned at the end of the poem, it was first retrieved from a site by Prof Nicholas Heer. You can find the original at: http://weber.u.washington.edu/~heer/ which is also very useful for Arabic computing resources. There were only two minor typos that were corrected below.
