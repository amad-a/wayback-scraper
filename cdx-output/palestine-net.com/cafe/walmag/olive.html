Olive Trees In Palestinian Culture

By CPD member Saad Yasin

Olive is a kind of trees that has a very small elliptical shaped leaves with a color of dark creen. The branch usaully has countless number of leaves. The tree grow like a mushroom cloude, the leaves stays on the branches through out the year's four seasons except for a few leaves that dry out and fall down. the olive itself grows on the branches and depending on the color of the olive and its size the tree in the seasons look like an old man carrying a load that is much larger than his strength . So a visit to an olive garden in the month of late september and early october which is the season for olive's colloction, sees a sight that take breath a way. The trees that where so independent and each has its own area and space a month a go, they are touching and getting closer to each other today as to show support for each other and to compliment each other on the load each is carrying and a bout to deliver. the olive itself look like a grape with varity of color and sizes. They grow separate, i.e each olive is by itself as it is declearing independance from other olives that are a djacent to it, but is not unusaul to see three or more olives sharing the same origin. So this breif introduction a bout the olive tree is just to give a reader who has never seen an olive tree,and beleive me there are some, just a fogy discription because of the language limitation.

Where ever you look in Palestine, and what ever you eat the olive is there to remind us that we are here for you so be there for us. Protect us, take care of us and you never be disappointed. Traviling on any road the only thing that is green in the hot summer months are the olive trees, the sight is so moving and touching that when ever you see the trees you just can't look a way. Olives and olive trees are a part of old generation and the new one a like. Any person that had planted an olive trees knows the reward were so generouse, in my childhood days they where an olive trees planting rush period which took the rest time that the villigers usaully have between seasons and made every body busy and excited. The rush was due to planting more olive trees to make up for the one that had been destroyed or damaged due to occupation or natural cuases.

At the end of this month the harvest season for olive begins and last untile late october, early november. The season start when the whole community a gree that the olive is rib and at certain day we start the harvest season. So when the date set approached, it is like something great is a bout to happen, something as great as a feast , something that make every body full of anticipation and excitment. So that early morning on that set date, the morning that was so quite up untile now, is so a live and the movement of people and animals as well as the voices of the people calling each other and talking, fills the breezy and fresh air of the morning.

The places that where empty and quite yesterday are full of people and noise today. The family that was fighting or arguing yesterday, they are in harmony and happiness today. The faces that seemed so sad and worried yesterday they are smilling and peaceful today. The transformation of the human can never be seen so evident except on those days. The day start by laying down a piece of clothes of plastic cover under the olive tree and the small and older people climb on the top of the tree to seperate the olive from the branch with minimum damage to the branch or the tree. The tree in palestinain cultur is a holy tree, it was mentioned in the Holy Quraan along with the other holy tree, the Fig tree. So people treat the tree as they would treat small baby, with tender and care. The other group stays under the trees collocting what falls and store it in a bag. From tree to tree people and accessares move, and bag after bag get filled with olives. People a cross boundries that were a reasons of a fight or an argument yesterday are talking to each other today, sharing stories and offering helps and goods they just people who never suspect that they are not a part of an extended family. The sellers who carry thier produce on a horse or a donkey, are touring the land selling their produce for olives. it is the only time of the year that the old way of doing busniss which is called mobadalah in arabic, which means i give you orange you give a banana for the same value of the orange. That was old way and the new generation did not witness that we born to see different pictures on a pieces of papers that you can take to a shop and buy whatever needed. So only at this time of the year and only with olives, the seller will sell you Guava, Grape....etc. for olives. Those people who do that are usually people who are seller by trade or people who have no olives or land and they just want to participate in this national feast, and to make some olive for their families on the side. By the end of the day many filled bags that are realy heavy to carry or move a round ,are standing under each tree so they can be collected and moved to storage place

The routine goes on for the harvest season length and every day of the season is just like a holiday. The night life is also transformed so people visit each other, they talk about what else, the olives.

The olive pressers open their doors so the processing to get the olive oil is done there. The front yard of the pressers building is full of people and cars, and the villige which sleeps usualy by nine clock is twenty four hours sleepless on those days. The young people who never thought a bout calories and getting fat, are drinking fresh olive oil that is just dripping from the filteration system . The aroma of the fresh olive oil fills the air. The people waiting their turns and having a fun time just tasting olive oil every time a new person pressed his olives. The noise of the olive oil containers and the continouse movement of the people all night long is so fun to watch and be a part of.

The taste of the fresh olive is in itself an inticer for more food. It works out the appitites and gives a feeling of hunger, a bread soaked with oil is more deliciouce, than a roasted lamb .

The olive trees plays a decoration rule,people use them to decorate new houses and weddings parties. They also play an important rule in providing the shadow on hot summer days, and provider of wood for the cold winter nights. The history of the land of palestine is written on every olive leave, telling the past, the present, and the future. In every country there is something that is a symbol of the people and the land, in palestine olive trees are the symbol, they are people's pride, and history.

During the years of intifada, and even before, the israel's army took out many trees for the reason of hidding stone throwers. The trees they took are thousand of years old, they are called roman trees. By taking them they knew what they were doing, they knew they are trying to wipe out the history, the protection, and pride.

These days people are back to planting more olive trees, even though it takes very long for an olive tree to mature, it needs a lot of caring and hard work, but the rewards can't be measured by any scale. From my personal experience none of the olive oils that have been sold in U.S.A have the smell or the taste of palestine olive oil.

The olive trees has played a big part in palestine culture. The undecleared holidays that olive season bring with it, the oil and olives which fell the empty shelfs of every palestinain home, the sight and beauty the olive trees provide are valuable assists and palestine is blessed to have olive trees on evey hill,mountain and vally. The rule the olive trees played in palesinain struggle for freedom can all be found in the struggle history from 1936 when Alqasam fought the british army in olive gardens in a small town near Jenin called Yaabad, the olive trees provided a cover to few palestinain soilders who were facing a large british army is still talked about in every gathering, the eyewitness account of how the body of Shaheed Al-qasam laying down between branches of olive trees as if they were hugging him good by is still vivid in our minds. In 1987 uprising the olive trees played an important rule in protecting the young intifada soilders. Long before when reading the history of palestine and people who lived there, the olive trees were a part of their culture also, which can be seen in there arts and drawing .
