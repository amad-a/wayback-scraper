School Division

Computer & Printing division


The friends of the blind association has been always
keen on acquiring the latest to facilitate achieving its
goals in serving the blind .
It has therefore provided the school since 1993 with
electronic printers supported by computers to
substitute the manual printing of bralle books .
In this respect , the school was a pionter in the whol
country to provide this electronic braille printing service
not only to its blind students but also to students of other
schools as well as of  colleges and universities .
It is the aim of the association to secure all the
complementary and supporting devices for this division .

The Library division


A building of 240 square meters was established as a
core for a braille library .
This library still needs a number of equipment :
magnifires , braille readers , shelves and a budget for
the purchase , and printing of books .

Educational instruments division


The national school for the blind girls is considered the
pioneer in providing the educational devices that are
specially designed for the sight disabled .
Many of the educational instruments were prepared of
simple materials available .
There is a special hall that includes various devices
such as models , maps , boards , ...etc . Paper , the
students and teachers prepared beds , etc , these
designs of ceramics .

Hostel division

Floor of 950 square meters is dedicated for this
division .
It consists of three parts , one for the elementary
students ,one  for the preparatory , and one for the
secondary .
For each part , there are supervisors and housekeepers
to take care of the students .
Each student has her own bed and cupboard , and
there is a big sitting room to watch television .

Laundry division

There is number of washing machines , automatic
dryers to do the service of the students .

The Kitchen

In 1995 , the association has established a very big hall
for cooking and dining with an area of 240 square
meters to accommodate , the staff prepares 3 meals
daily .

Playing division


Many external playgrounds were prepared by the
association and equipped with recreational tools for
the students .

However , it needs future improvement , especially to
include some sporting devices that accommodate blind
students .

By the end of ecah year , as well as in the international
day for the disabled , the school holds a special
ceremony , where blind students participate in various
activities , acting , singing , dancing and playing music .
These activities are also performed  in a number of public
and private schools at various occasions , which receive
the appreciation and  admiration of the while community .

The association also arrages for a number of trips and
journeys during the year for the recreation of the blind
children where they visit various parts of the country . It
also invites lecturers and welcomes different groups to
visit the school and to take a close look at the significant
achievements .
