School

With a permit from the ministry of education , the friends of
the blind association have established this school since
1978 .

It is located in  AL - Bireh  city    - Al -Shurafa   area , and it
includes  twelve  classes starting  from kindergarten  till the
twelfth grade .

This school is open for female blind students coming from
all around Palestine ( West bank ) and for male students till
the fifth grade .

The school consists of two buildings , and each building
consists of two floors . One building is for teaching and
accommodation , while the other includes the library as
well as the hall for cultural , recreational and sports
activities .
