Friends of the Blind Association
On February 20th night, the National School for Blind Girls, where blind Palestinian students study and sleep, came under attack from Israeli positions in the Psagot settlement located on the hill overlooking the school in Albireh. The school got a direct hit from a tank shell and heavy rounds of bullets penetrated the students dorms.
Some pictures from the aftermath are below. There were no physical injuries thanks to attendants' help in moving the students to iner alleys and under staircases. There were numerous Cases of shock and psycological complications.
