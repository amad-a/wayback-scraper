Local Sites:

  • Defence For Children International/Palestine Section can be found athttp://www.dci-pal.org/ They also have a special website devoted to the clashes at http://www.dci-pal.org/clashes.htm

  • The Palestinian Non-Governmental Organizations Network (PNGO) can be found at http://www.pngo.net/ and their recent press releases on the current situation at http://www.pngo.net/intifada/intifada.htm

  • The Union of Palestinian Medical Relief Committees (UPMRC) is a grassroots, community-based Palestinian health organization founded in 1979 by a group of Palestinian doctors and health professionals seeking to supplement the decayed and inadequate health infrastructure caused by years of Israeli military occupation. An excellent source of information on the current situation. http://www.upmrc.org/

  • LAW is a Palestinian human rights organization with a wide array of publications and press releases. Their website can be found at http://www.lawsociety.org/

  • Palestinian Red Crescent Society http://www.Palestinercs.org/

  • Sign this petition to UN Secretary General, Kofi Annan, to form a special international tribunal to prosecute Israeli officers and soldiers responsible for the murder of Mohammed Al Dura http://www.durra.org/

  • Palestinian National Authority - The official Palestinian Authority website.

  • The Complete Guide To Palestine's Websites - Birzeit University's "Yahoo.com" for local sites.

International Organizations and Sites

  • Hanthala is an international network of Palestinians active around many campaigns. A good source of information on the current events, particularly concerning international actions. http://hanthala.virtualave.net/

  • Amnesty International http://www.amnesty.org/

  • Palestinian Media Watch Campaign http://www.pmwatch.org

  • Ali Abunimah's Bitter Pill is a collection of well-reasoned letters, interviews and media transcripts from this "nagging malcontent" in Chicago. http://www.abunimah.org
