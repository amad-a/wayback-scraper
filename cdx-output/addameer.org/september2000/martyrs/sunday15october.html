Martyrs -Sunday 15 October 2000

 

Name: Ra'ed Ya'coub Hammoudeh,


Age: 30 years old


Place of Residence: Al Bireh


Other information: Died today as a result of injuries sustained 4 days ago. Ra'ed was shot in the head with live ammunition and remained in critical condition for four days until his death today


Place of death: Al Bireh
