Martyrs - Wednesday 18 October 2000

   

Name: Ibrahim Abdel Rahman Da'oud Al Alami


Age: 25 years old


Place of Residence: Beit Ummar, Hebron area


Other information: died as a result of injuries sustained on 12 October at the entrance of his village while traveling home from work. Ibrahim was shot twice in the head by live ammunition, and remained in critical condition at Al Ahli Hospital in Hebron, before he was transferred to Jordan for immediate medical attention, where he died.


Place of death:Beit Ummar, Hebron area

Name: Moussa Shehadeh Haidar


Age: 54 years old


Place of Residence: Gaza City


Other information: died as a result of a heart attack. Moussa suffered a heart attack yesterday during clashes yesterday in Beit Hanoun as he watched his close friend Nabil Sa'ed Abed Khater, 41 years old, from Shaja'iyeh, Gaza, shot and killed beside him by live ammunition to his chest.


Place of death: Gaza City
