Martyrs Saturday 28 October 2000

 

Name: Aziz Youssef Ibrahim Tannah


Age: 32 years old


Place of Residence: Bethlehem


Other information: correspondent and director of the Bethlehem office of WAFA, the official Palestinian news wire, died this morning in Jordan as a result of injuries sustained 2 weeks ago from live ammunition to his chest. He was transferred to Jordan for immediate medical attention, which proved unsuccessful. He was later declared clinically dead, and died this morning in hospital in Jordan.


Place of death: Bethlehem
