Martyrs -Friday 13 October 2000

 

Name: Shadi Mohommad Al Wawi


Age: 24 years old


Place of Residence: Fawar Refugee Camp, Hebron


Other information: shot and killed yesterday by live ammunition to his chest. Shadi was standing on the roof of his home when he was shot. A Red Crescent ambulance trying to transfer him from his home to the hospital was prevented from moving for over an hour, during which Shadi bled to death from his injury.


Place of death: Fawar Refugee Camp, Hebron

Name: Mansour Tahah Sayed Ahmad


Age: 22 years old


Place of Residence: Dura, Hebron


Other information: shot and killed yesterday by a Dum-Dum Bullet to his stomach.


Place of death: Dura, Hebron
