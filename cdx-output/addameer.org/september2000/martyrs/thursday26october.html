Martyrs Thursday 26 October 2000

   

Name: 'Ala Mohammad Mahmoud Jawabreh


Age: 16 years old


Place of Residence: 'Arroub Refugee Camp


Other information: died as a result of injuries sustained on 11 October, when he was shot in the head by an exploding Dum-Dum bullet. He was first given medical treatment in Hebron, then transferred to Saudi Arabia for emergency medical care. He died in hospital in Saudi Arabia yesterday.


Place of death: 'Arroub Refugee Camp

Name: Ahmad Abdel Qader Sbeitan


Age: 64 years old


Place of Residence: Hebron


Other information: died at an Israeli checkpoint while being transported from Hebron to Ramallah Hospital for emergency heart surgery. The ambulance transporting him was detai ned at the checkpoint near Beit Jala for over an hour, prevented from returning or moving forward.


Place of death: Beit Jala
