As of 4 February 2001, nearly 350 Palestinians have been killed by the Israeli military, police or settlers and over 11000 wounded.

The youngest Palestinians to have sustained injuries during clashes include a 6-month-old child and a 2-year-old child. Thirteen children have lost at least one eye as a result of injuries.

(The statistics on this website are collected from various sources including field workers, media, hospitals, and other organizations. We try to ensure the best possible accuracy) - List of those killed from all days available here
At the time of the launch of this website on Sunday 1 October, over 20 Palestinians had been killed by Israeli soldiers and over 700 wounded in the most widespread clashes since September 1996. The clashes have extended throughout all of Palestine - the West Bank, Gaza Strip, and inside the Green Line/1948 areas. This website aims to provide up-to-date information on the clashes from the ground.
