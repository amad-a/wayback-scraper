Latest Developments No. 6 - Tuesday 3 October 2000, 5:00pm

In Gaza, the Israeli army have continued launching rockets from the air at demonstrators and buildings including an attack on Arafat Pedagogical College, near Netzarim junction. Two planes and tanks have launched rockets against the Camp of the National Security and two apartment buildings. Twenty-seven people have been injured including one with a critical injury to the head. Mohammed Abu Ebeid, 18 years, from Nusseirat camp, was killed by a bullet in the head. Rockets have also reportedly been launched at a mosque in al Brazil neighborhood in Rafah.

In Ramallah, clashes continue near the City Inn at the outskirts of the city and injuries from live ammunition have been reported. Ten people have been wounded in Nablus near Joseph's Tomb.

Heavy clashes in Hebron and surrounding villages continue with over 3000 demonstrators participating. Undercover units have reportedly entered PA controlled areas in Hebron.

In the north of the West Bank, clashes continue in Jenin and Tulkarem. Twenty-five people have been wounded, 1 seriously.

Clashes continue inside 1948 areas with many roads closed.
