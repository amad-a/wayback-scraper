Latest Developments No. 46 - Tuesday 31 October 2000,Web posted 11:30pm (GMT+2), Ramallah, West Bank

A strict Israeli imposed closure remains in place throughout the West Bank and Gaza Strip. Israeli settler attacks on Palestinian residents and the bulldozing and destruction of agricultural lands continues in the majority of cities and villages in the West Bank and Gaza. Five Palestinians were killed today and at least 90 injured, 5 of which are in critical condition, in clashes throughout the area.

Shadi Awwad Odeh, 18 years old from Al Zaytoun, Gaza City, was shot and killed today by live ammunition to his head.

Hazem Moussa Abu Daf, 22 years old from Al Zaytoun, Gaza City, was shot and killed today by live ammunition to his head.

Mohammad Hillis, 23 years old from Shuja'iyeh, Gaza City, was shot and killed today by live ammunition to his head.

Mahmoud Ahmad Ghali Abu Al Khair, 20 years old from Shuja'iyeh, Gaza City, was shot and killed today by live ammunition to his head.

Tha'er Ibrahim Zaid, 17 years old from Jalazoune Refugee Camp, near Ramallah was shot and killed today by live ammunition to his abdomen, with the bullet lodging in his heart.

Ramallah
Clashes erupted this afternoon at the northern entrance of Al Bireh, with at least 17 Palestinians injured as a result of live ammunition to the upper body. Eyewitnesses report that the Israeli military shot at an ambulance attempting to reach those injured. Tha'er Ibrahim Zaid, 17 years old from Jalazoune Refugee Camp was shot and killed during clashes by live ammunition to his abdomen. At the time of writing, clashes have erupted at the entrance of Jalazoune Refugee Camp, on the outskirts of Ramallah.

Gaza
Clashes erupted this afternoon throughout the Gaza Strip. At Mentar crossing, 4 Palestinians were killed during clashes, all as a result of live ammunition to the head, including: Shadi Awwad Odeh, 18 years old from Al Zaytoun, Gaza City; Hazem Moussa Abu Daf, 22 years old from Al Zaytoun, Gaza City; Mohammad Hillis, 23 years old from Shuja'iyeh, Gaza City; Mahmoud Ahmad Ghali Abu Al Khair, 20 years old from Shuja'iyeh, Gaza City. At least 15 Palestinians were injured as a result of live ammunition to the upper body. CNN correspondent, Ben Wydmen, was also shot in the abdomen by live ammunition.

In Rafah, clashes resulted in the injury of 25 Palestinians by live ammunition to the upper body. Clashes also erupted in Deir al Balah and Khan Younis, where 5 Palestinians were injures as a result of live ammunition to the upper body.

Bethlehem
Israeli settlers opened fire this morning on residential areas in the Palestinian village of Al Khader, injuring at least 10 Palestinians. Clashes erupted in the village after the Israeli military arrived in the area. At least 4 Palestinians are in critical condition as a result of clashes, with eyewitnesses reporting that Israeli soldiers and settlers were shooting at ambulances attempting to reach those injured. Salim Harbeh Mattariyeh, 34 years old from Al Khader, was announced clinically dead as a result of live ammunition to his head from Israeli settler fire.

Late this evening, the Israeli military used tanks to shoot at residential areas in the village of Beit Jala. A number of homes were damaged, with no injuries reported to date. Israeli tanks also fired at least 10 shells at Beit Sahour this evening, causing severe damage to a number of homes.

Nablus
Israeli settlers attacked Palestinian cars this afternoon on the main road between Nablus and Ramallah, injuring at least 5 Palestinians.

Jenin
Brief clashes erupted this afternoon in the Jenin area. Eyewitnesses report that Ahmad Mustafa Abdel Razeq Wahdan, 19 years old from Tayasir, near Jenin, was kidnapped this afternoon by Israeli settlers from the settlement of Mahouleh. His whereabouts remains unknown.

Jerusalem
Brief clashes erupted this afternoon in Hizmeh, Jerusalem. An arrest campaign conducted by the Israeli authorities in areas of Jerusalem was intensified today.

Jericho
Late this evening, Israeli settlers attacked Palestinian residential areas in the northern section of Jericho. The attacks continue at the time of writing.
