Latest Developments No. 5 - Tuesday 3 October 2000, 2:30pm

Clashes have renewed across the West Bank and Gaza Strip following funeral processions of those killed in yesterday's clashes.

In Gaza, 19 people have been wounded at the Netzarim Junction following further Israeli rocket attacks. One Palestinian, Fahmi Abu Amoni, aged 48 years was killed.

In Nablus, three people have been wounded following clashes at Joseph's Tomb in the heart of Nablus. In Bethelehem, Khadra Salaymee, aged 56, was pronounced dead from injuries received in yesterday's clashes. Clashes have also broken out in Ramallah in the last half-an-hour at the City Inn following a funeral procession earlier this afternoon.
