Christmas Smiles

Over the past month Bethlehem has suffered greatly. Youth and concerned individuals have continued to protest the new checkpoint, which is currently being built to separate Palestinians from tourists and Israelis. After an Israeli soldier unjustly killed a young man, there have been daily clashes resulting in numerous injuries.

Wi'am would like to continue extending bright Smiles to the Children of Bethlehem this Christmas, even though the conditions and situation seem dim. For the last few years the Center has worked diligently to provide gifts to children, who otherwise would be without. To make this project of giving a success we depend on generous contributions from the outside. Last year we handed out gifts to over 1,200 children during the 3 Christmas periods here in the Middle East. We look forward to reaching more children this year with your help, to bring smiles and hope to children and families for the coming of the New Millennium.
