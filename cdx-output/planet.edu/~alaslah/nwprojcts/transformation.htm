A Message of Transformation


Oh God!!
Will I address you with courage!
Oh Lord!!
Thanks for creating us in your image!
Apology Lord!
We have created you in our image!

Oh! Lord
Why should the child of Bethlehem continue to suffer?
No peace! No justice! No one to offer!
No refuge! No haven! Even no buffer!
Herod's orders! Our dreams to shatter.

Child of Bethlehem! Tired of Silence
Massacres, demolitions and menace!
Rockets tanks, snipers and vengeance!
Siege, poverty, checkpoints are extravagance!

Almighty God,

The voice is heard not only in Rama!
Rachel's tears! Renewal for a new drama!
Why does Palestine become a Panorama?
Holiness: Where? Neither love nor aroma!


Oh Lord!

The baby has nightmares!
Bedwetting for the child! Herod scares.
Responsibility and burden the Child bears.
Where are the US / Europe? Arabs? No one cares.


Merciful Lord,

Our blood and tears are shed!
Our feelings are suppressed!
Our fears are expressed!
Our prayers are said!


Oh Lord,

We are waiting for your miracles!
No more waiting! Send more oracles!
Neither false prophets nor Real Estate agents, but those who follow righteousness!
Justice, safety, for children restore happiness.

Dear Savior!
No one will quell our thirst except you!
Thirst for security hunger for justice! Fear adieu.
Workers and harvest through rain and dew!
Send your love and Mercy and our mentality will be new!

Loving God!

Transform Herods and the like!
Not through airplanes, tanks or snipers, but on a bike,
Of love and humility are two wheels alike!
The Baby is born and the Magi on a hike.
