THE PALESTINIAN ASSOCIATION FOR CULTURAL EXCHANGE (PACE)

A Message from the General Manager.

Dear Friends of Palestine:

If you plan to be visiting Palestine soon or if you are already there The Palestinian Association For Cultural Exchange (PACE) welcomes you and invites you to a voyage of discovery through the country, and helps you on your way. Just simply call or write and we will take you on a visit to one of the lovely corners of the West Bank, Gaza and Jerusalem.

You are bound to profit from the experience and find a lot of interesting, curious and beautiful things to look at, no matter what time of year it is. Getting to know Palestine means experiencing history, art, culture and also the way of life, hospitality and natural warmth of the people.

So make use of your visit to Palestine, and let yourself be captivated by the atmosphere and the charm of the country and the people. We bid you welcome and wish you a happy and interesting visit to Palestine.

Dr. Adel Yahya,

General Manager
