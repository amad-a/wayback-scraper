Million Letters Project

With an initiative from INTERX organization that is specialized in Technology & Telecommunication, the INTERX in the USA launched on the occasion of Land Day a project of Million Letters to support the firmed and the legal national Palestinian rights in Jerusalem. Those letters to be send to the Palestinian Ministry of Information, Israeli government, the White House, and many other countries.

Following is the full text of the letter:


Million letter campaign to support Palestinian rights in Jerusalem
