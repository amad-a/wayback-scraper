Community Development Organizations and Link Sites   
    ANTENNA, Association for Progressive Communication  
      Antenna is a Dutch organization that provides an internet forum for organizations that represent various social movements and has a great link sites related to various thematic areas (e.g. peace, youth, women, etc.) 
       
    Civic Practices Network  
      Civic Practices Network (CPN) is a collaborative and nonpartisan project bringing together a diverse array of organizations and perspectives within the new citizenship movement in the US. This site has a searchable index to find stories, case studies, and essays by topic area, key word, and geographic area 
       
    Community Associations Institute Online  
       It is an American multidisciplinary alliance dedicated to the development of effective community associations.  Their homepage has information about how to develop community organizations and has links to other resources 
       
    Community Development Society (CDS) 
      CDS is a professional association for community development practitioners and citizen leaders from around the world, however, at this point its membership and resources are primarily US-focused.  This homepage has information about CDS, and its members, news updates about issues related to community development, and an on-line library and journal. 
       
    COMM-ORG  
      COMM-ORG, the ON-Line Conference on Community Organizing and Development is a US-based organization that helps to connect people who "care about community organizing" and that provides information to help people with community organizing.  This homepage has information about COMM-ORG, a whole host of on-line resources (e.g. working papers, a list-serve), and link sites arranged by topic.  COMM-ORG currently has 500 members from 12 different countries 
       
    Community Tool Box 
      This organize seeks to promote community health and development by connecting people, ideas and resources.  Of note on this internet site is the community building tools section with information and publications on strategic planning, advocacy, grant writing, evaluation, etc.  This internet site also has a section on information about broad strategies for community building,  links to other web pages and resources, and a searchable database that is very helpful.  . 
       
      The Grassroots Organizer  
      Provides links to community organizing internet sites.  In addition, this site provides information about tools and resources for community based action 
       
      The Groundswell Project  
      This web site, currently under construction, promotes and develops self-help initiatives with homeless, landless and socially excluded people 
       
    Idealist  
      This internet site provides an on-line resource for non-profit and community organization.  The resource is an on-line searchable databse with links to, information about, and resources from over 15,000 NGOs and community groups from around the world 
       
    IDSNET, University of Guelph 
      In addition to links to Canadian development studies programs, this site has links grouped into various thematic sections (gender, governance, media, etc.) 
       
    Non-profit and Non Governmental Organizations on the Net 
      Site that provides links to Nonprofit and Non Governmental Organizations (NGOs) who are using the World Wide Web as a means of informing the public about the major issues they are concerned with. 
       
    OneWorld Online 
      A clearing house of information and links to organizations worldwide.  This site not only provides news about current events, analysis of world events, and a forum for discussion, but it also provides links to other organizations and resource sites 
       
    Participatory Development Online Resources Web Site 
      PDOR is currently being re-developed; however it has some very helpful links to sites with 'Participatory Development Resources' 
       
    The New Social Movements Network  
      This site was developed based on a conference tracing conceptual links between new social movement theory and practice, and poverty.  This site is used as a "medium for dialogue across space".  This site has some interesting discussion papers, and links to other related sites 
       
      Society for International Development (SID) 
       SID is a global network of individuals and institutions concerned with development which is participative, pluralistic and sustainable. This site consists primarily of information about SID. 
       
      UNDP 
      This link site provides information about the UNDP programs, projects and field offices from around the world, and provides information about its areas of focus: poverty, gender, environment and governance. 
     
    The Washington Office on Latin America (WOLA)  
      WOLA promotes human rights, democracy and social and economic justice in Latin America and the Caribbean.  WOLA facilitates dialogue between governmental and non governmental actors, monitors the impact of policies and programs of governments and international organizations, and promotes alternatives through reporting, education, training and advocacy

Community Development Research Institutes   
      Center for Development Research Copenhagen 
      Has information about its research activities that are grouped into four categories: agricultural development and the environment, local institutions and rural development, gender and social inequality, gender and social inequality, and globalization and developing countries. 
       
    Community Development On-Line 
      This site is an information clearing house that is easily accessible by a searchable database.  The database has information about case studies, potential funding sources, etc.  The one crimp with respecting to accessing this resource is that there is a subscription fee. 
       
      The Development and Project Planning Center Library, Bradford  
      This site has very useful links relating to development studies and research 
       
    European Association of Development Research and Training Institutes (EADI) 
      EADI is an independent and non-profit international non-governmental organization.  Their homepage provides information about them and has link sites to other development organizations and research institutes from Europe and around the world
     
    International Development Research Center  
      IDRC is a public corporation created by the Canadian government to help communities in the developing world find solutions to social, economic, and environmental problems through research. This site not only provides information about IDRC and its programs, but it also has some of its research materials available online 
       
      Institute for Development Research 
      The Institute for Development Research (IDR), a non-profit research, education and consulting organization, is dedicated to promoting the participation of underrepresented and impoverished groups in social, political and economic development processes. 
        
      Overseas Development Institute 
      The Overseas Development Institute is an independent  non-governmental center for the study of development and humanitarian issues and a forum for discussion of the problems facing developing countries. This site provides information about their areas of research 
        
    Third World Network (TWN) 
      TWN is an independent non-profit international network of organizations and individuals involved in issues relating to development, the Third World and North-South relations.  TWN's site has information that relates to their major activities: research work, organizing seminars, and providing "a platform representing Southern interests and perspectives in the international fora".  This site has a current focus section which is a discussion and analysis of a specific issues, and it has links to resources on various thematic areas (e.g. trade, health, human rights, Thrid World economics, etc.) 
       
    UNRISD 
      UNRISD is an autonomous agency engaging in multi-disciplinary research on the social dimensions of contemporary problems affecting development.  This site has a collections of research papers about various dimensions of 'development'
     
    Virtual Library on International Development, CIDA 
      A comprehensive site with information grouped by organizations, subjects, regions and countries, reports and publications 
        
    The World Wide Web Virtual Library on Sustainable Development  
      Has numerous link sites to research centers, organizations and activities related to sustainable development

Research and Publications on the Internet   
      Community Development Journal 
      The Community Development Journal provides information about the theory and practice of the policies, programmes and methods relating to community action, village, town and regional planning, community studies and rural development.  This site has summaries of articles that appear in the journal 
     
      Devline 
      Provides information services and systems for people working on issues of economic, social and sustainable development.  It has links to various developing research engines on the internet such as ELDIS, ID21, BLDS, etc. 
       
    Information for Development in the 21st Century 
      ID21 Development Research reporting service has a selection of the latest UK-based development research.  This online service has information drawn from over 40 top UK research centers, conference papers and unpublished report, and research by aid and development agencies and pressure groups, and finally research by consultants. 
       
      New Social Movements Network--Papers on-line 
      Has papers submitted and discussed at the New Social Movement and Community Organizing conference. 
       
      UNRISD Publications 
       Provides links to numerous on-line research papers about a variety of topics related to social and economic development.
     
       World Bank Develoment Report 1998/9 
      The theme of this report is "Knowledge for Development"
