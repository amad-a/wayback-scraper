Training of Trainers
 
        With the generous assistance of the Palestinian Microproject Center, Panorama will be undertaking a project to train trainers with respect to subjects related to democracy.   Starting November 1998, and continuing through until February 1999 Panorama will offer four sets of training courses to youth, and four to the administration of Youth Clubs.  The training courses include discussions about democracy, experimenting with different training techniques, and information about ensuring internal democratic principles within organizations and groups.  For more specific information, please contact the Panorama office in Jerusalem
