Call for Solidarity Visit

Dear Friends and Colleagues,

The Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) calls upon its friends for an on site solidarity visit to Palestine Technical College/Khadouri in Tulkarim to witness the destructive results of the latest Israeli shelling.

The gathering is scheduled on Thursday, November 16th, 2000 at 11:00 AM. The Minister, Deputy Minister of MOHE and other officials will be present at the site.

Your presence will be a clear sign of your continued support to the Palestinian people and Authority and will be greatly appreciated. 

Regards,

Deputy Minister

Hisham Kuhail
