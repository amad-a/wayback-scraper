Appeal For Solidarity

The Palestinian Ministry of Higher Education as it strongly condemns Israel’s latest terrorist and brutal attacks against Palestinian institutions of the College of Education in Gaza and Palestine Technical College/Kaduri in Tulkarem urges the international community and Palestinian friends everywhere to condemn such attacks and to pressure the Israeli government to stop its aggression and violence against the Palestinian people and their institutions.

Israel, in its latest attempt to destroy Palestinian will, used all means available tanks, planes and man-power to shell two Palestinian Higher Education institutions in the West Bank and Gaza destroying their buildings.

This attack is only one of the many daily oppressive measures the Israeli government exercises on Palestinians that reveal time and time again Israel’s lack of commitment to peace and their violation of Palestinian basic human rights.

The Ministry of Higher Education cannot emphasize enough the need for all supporters of justice and human rights to put an end to all Israeli aggressions against Palestinian institutions and people.
