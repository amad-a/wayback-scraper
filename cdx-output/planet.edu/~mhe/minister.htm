Minister

Mohammad Munther Salah                      

Was appointed Minister of Higher Education in August 1998. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Glasgow University and is well published in Computer Sciences and its applications. Dr. Salah has been involved in Education for more than three decades. He has held prominent positions as President of An-Najah University in Nablus, President of Al-Quds Open University, Vice President of the Royal Scientific Society in Jordan. Currently he is also the Chairman of the PEACE Program and has been involved in promoting academic and scientific cooperation between Palestinian, European and American universities.
