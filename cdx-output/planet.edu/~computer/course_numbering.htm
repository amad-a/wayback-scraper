XYZ Course number in computer science department
X: is 1,2,3 or 4 indicating the year level of this course
Y: is a topic indicator
Z: is a sequence number usually odd for the first semester courses and even for second semester courses.

Topic indicators are as follows:
0: General and service courses
1: Programming languages, Data structures, and Algorithms
2: Mathematical analysis and its applications
3: Logic, microprocessors, and computer org. & architecture
4: System and data management
5: Compiler and language processors
6: Artificial intelligence and pattern recognition
7: Operating Systems & Computer Networks
8: Graphics
9: Special and advanced topics
