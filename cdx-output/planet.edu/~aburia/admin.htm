ADMINISTRATIVE (NON-PATIENT SERVICES) The main goals of administrative services are:
1. To develop an adequate communication system.
2. To establish adequate records to be used as a basis of control.
3. To lay down proper rules, regulations, and policies.
4. To exercise effective leadership.

Support services at ARRC are:
1. Personal
2. Accounting
3. Secretarial activities (computer services and data processing)
4. Public Relations
5. Medical records
6. Medical Library (being developed by library group)
7. Laundry services
8. Food services for patients and employees
9. Purchase of non-medical supplies
10. Equipment maintenance
11. Patient transportation services
