The Medical Department consists of 3 doctors, 2 of them are specialist in Rehabilitation.

The doctors beside their clinical work both In-Patients and out-Patients supervise the rehabilitation work and try to implement a sense of team work.

The medical department with other departments organize the admissions and discharges. They link rehabilitation work with other rehabilitation programs in the community.
