Timetable for the Elections of the Palestinian Council and of the President of the Palestinian National Authority

Registration

November 12 - December 2, 1995 Voters registration. 7000 teachers going from door to door to register all persons over 17 years old.
December 10 Publication of the Draft Electoral Register
December 10-14 Period for registration claims and appeals to be submitted
December 30 Publication of Final Electoral Register
December 30 - January 5, 1996 Possibility for additional registrations
January 17 Publication of Supplementary Electoral Register

Issuing of ID Cards

November 20 Advertisement by the Palestinian National Authority for applications for ID cards by Palestinians without cards e.g. those holding foreign passports
December 5 Final date for applications for ID cards

Nominations and election campaign

December 14 - 23 Nominations for the Palestinian Council to be presented to the District Election Commissions (DECs); nominations for the President to be presented to the Central Election Commission (CEC)
December 24 Publication of Provisional Statement of Persons Nominated
December 26 Final date for appeals on the Provisional Statement
December 30-31 Additional nominations period
January 5, 1996 Publication of Final Statement of Persons Nominated
January 5 - January 18 Election campaign
January 20 Polling Day
