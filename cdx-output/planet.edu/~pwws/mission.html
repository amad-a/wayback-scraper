Mission

The PWWS mission is the empowerment of women through their participation within the social and developmental aspects of both public and private life as to build a civic society based on democracy and the respect of human being. It consists in achieving total equality between men and women in all aspects as well as dignity, peace, security and justice for all the Palestinians.

The Organization's Aims

  1. To activate women's participation in building a democratic society where social justice and equality of opportunity reigns supremely between men and women in all the domains.
  2. To raise women's consciousness of their legislative rights in all fields and the forming of a supportive Public Opinion to women's rights.
  3. To provide supportive social services to women in all domains.
  4. Influencing the policies of official organizations in order to eradicate all forms of discriminaton against the woman.

 

The Organization's objectives

  1. To encourage women to actively participate in the political and social mainstreams;
  2. To insure equal opportunities in education and professional promotion;
  3. To provide women with needed assistance and support.
