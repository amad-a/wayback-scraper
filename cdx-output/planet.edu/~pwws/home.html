Welcome

The Palestinian Working Women Society (PWWS) is a community based non-governmental organization that was established in 1981 as the Union of Palestinian Working Women Committees. At that time, the main focus of the union was to integrate Palestinian women in the historical national struggle of the Palestinian people. In 1993, the Society was registered as a non-governmental organization. Due to the rich experience both in the national struggle and in the community intervention, we had realized that we should perform a kink of a balance between the national and the social struggle. We took into consideration, indeed, the deep interaction between both claims in order to achieve our goals as women and as a nation, especially with the emerging development of the Palestinian society after the peace process. Since then, the philosophy of the organization has thus been developed to wider women's involvement in building a Palestinian democratic civil society.
