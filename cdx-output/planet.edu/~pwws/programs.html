CHAIN OF SOLIDARITY AND COMPASSION

The Palestinian people are going through a difficult period on the political, economical, and social level.. This is due to the complexity of the political situation and the invasion of the market economy ethics and globalization.

These new characteristics of the Palestinian Society have become to threaten the requirements of the social security and the values of social justice, equality, and solidarity. This accelerates the process of class separation toward a greater marginalization even to the middle classes.

The Palestinian Working Women Society-PWWS- through its intimate & direct link with grassroots women in villages, camps, cities, and its role as a societal organization has adopted the 'Series of Solidarity & Compassion' project. The idea is to revive the notion of cooperation and a societal participation based on solidarity among all society sectors. This is done by rebuilding the bridges in the Palestinian society classes in stressing the dependence on locale resources instead of foreign aid.

Also, to encourage the idea of the voluntary work.

The chain Aims

The initiative of the 'Chain of Solidarity & Compassion' seeks to realize the following aims:

  • To consolidate the notion of give and take principle and the exchange and the cooperation based on dignity & respect and to activate the spirit of voluntary work;
  • Offering help and support to needy people in the spirit of solidarity and national belongingness. It is done with a dimension of strategic development especially in the realm of female education;
  • Building a self-supportive societal system that relies on locale human and material resources as an alternative to foreign aid;
  • This initiative is a modest attempt to confront abusive policies and the impoverishment of the Palestinian People. The imposing of an economic and social reality would loosen the values and the positive social norms in exchange of selfish values and abuse.

The Nature of the Chain's Activities

  • The Chain of Solidarity & Compassion starts with the readiness of individuals to offer help and support to the needy. This help can be either financial or moral. In exchange the beneficiary concerned offers, in turn, another form of help to others in the form of social service within his/her possibility.
  • Solidarity in the exchange of material items: This form of solidarity is based on the exchange of materials, furniture, and equipment that are of no use to the owner. He or she would like to give them to those who need them. the items are in condition- The PWWS, through its contacts, is the connecting factor between the contributor and the beneficiaries.

Ways of Support.

A.  Financial support

The donor is committed to a certain monthly sum of money -in accordance to his capacity- to be given to the needy person through the series fund, which is run by the PWWS. This monthly sum might help in the following:

  • School materials.
  • Students transport.
  • Schools renovation.
  • Houses renovation of poor & numerous families.
  • The purchase of clothes to poor people on feast occasion.

B.  The volunteering in Efforts.

Those who don't have the means to contribute financially but wish to give part of their time to voluntary work to the needy. They can do the following:

  • Helping old people by visiting them. Reading to them or cleaning their homes.
  • Offering private lessons to students.
  • Societal participation on different occasions like olive-picking, renovation of homes and schools and the cleaning of neglected areas.
  • Moral support and national participation in visiting families whose homes were demolished by the occupation authorities or whose lands were confiscated. Also, to visit families whose sons are in prison.

How to Participate in the Chain.

Any citizen can join the Chain different activities by either financial donation or voluntary participation or moral support. He or she has to have the conviction and a sincere wish to do a voluntary work. He or she can join the campaign activities by contacting the PWWS and asking for a request form.

Similarly, any poor individual or family can request the help from the PWWS provided he is willing to offer other kinds of help in the service of others.
