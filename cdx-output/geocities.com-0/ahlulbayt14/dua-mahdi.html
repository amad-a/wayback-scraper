Du'a of Imam al-Mahdi (a)

Oh Allah, provide us success of obedience and distance from the disobedience, And truthful intention and sacred knowledge and honor us with guidance and steadfastness, and direct our tongues with truthfulness and wisdom and fill our hearts with knowledge and awareness, and purify our stomachs from the unlawful and doubtful and restrain our hands from injustice and  theft, and avert our sight from indecency and deception and cover our ears from foolish talk and slander, and confer favor on our scholars with renunciation and sincere advice and on the students with endeavor and desire, And on those that seek help with adherence and admonition, and on the sick Muslims with recovery and comfort and on their deceased with compassion and mercy and on our spiritual leaders with dignity and tranquillity, and on the youth with representation and repentance and on the women with modesty and chastity, and on the wealthy with humbleness and abundance and on the poor with patience and contentment, And on those on military campaigns with help and victory and on the captives with freedom and comfort, And on the commanders with justice and sympathy and on the citizens with fair treatment and good behavior, And bless th pilgrims and visitors in provisions and expenses and fulfill what you have made obligatory on them in the Hajj and the Umrah, with favor and your mercy, Oh the Most Merciful of those who has mercy.
