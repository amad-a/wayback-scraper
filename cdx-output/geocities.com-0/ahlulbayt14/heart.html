IN THE NAME OF ALLAH, THE BENEFICENT, THE MERCIFUL

SICKNESS OF THE HEART


  • You believe in the existence of Allah, but you do not fulfill his commandments.

  • You say you love prophet Muhammad (s), but you do not follow his sunnah.

  • You read the Holy Quran, but you do not put it into practice.

  • You enjoy all the benefits from Allah, but you are not grateful to him.

  • Yo acknowledge shaitan as your enemy, but you do not work for it.

  • You want to enter the paradise, but you do not work for it.

  • You do not want to be thrown in the hell-fire, but you do not try to run away from it.

  • You believe that every-living thing will face death, but you do not prepare for it.

  • You gossip and find fault in others, but you forget your own faults and bad habits.

  • You bury the dead, but you do not take a lesson from it.

  • You purify your body, but you do not purify your heart.

  • You convey the message of Islam, but you do not act upon it.

  • You show others that you are a Muslim, but you are not a Muslim alone.

  • You fear mankind, but you do not fear Allah (swt).

  • You must fear Allah (swt), but you choose to ignore him.

  • You must remember death because will face it one day, but you are not putting into practice.

  • The Holy Quran is an ornament and decoration for your home, but your home is devoid of its teachings.

  • Back in the Holy Prophet's (s) time: it was very hard to live, but very easy to go to heaven, because there was were struggle and fear of Allah (swt).

  • Today's Mulsim Ummah: it is very easy to live but very hard to go to heaven, because there are violence and fear of mankind.

  • Back in the Holy Prophet's (s) time: people concerned about the hereafter, but today, you concerned about life.

  • Today, you have hardened your heart, because you lead yourself astray.

  • The Muslim Ummah should unite, but it is shattered.
