Hadith al-Thaqalayn *

A Study of Its Tawatur

The Messenger of Allah (s) said: "Verily, I am leaving behind two precious things (thaqalayn) among you: the Book of God and my kindred (`itrah), my household (Ahlul-Bayt), for indeed, the two will never separate until they come back to me by the Pond (of al-Kawthar on the Judgement's Day)."
