O Thou that answereth the prayers of the disconsolate in the depth of darkness;

O Thou that wardeth off the baneful influences of calamities and diseases. Lo, those who surrounded the Ka'abah sleep and wake up; But Thou, Exalted be thy Name, never sleepest. Forgive me by the Grace of Thy Bounty and Thy Mercy.

O Though towards whom all creation bows down in this Sanctuary! If Thy Mercy be not such as giveth hope to the transgressors, Then who is there to take pity on the sinners by His Bounty?

O Thou that didst fortify Yahya, the Son of Zakaria against sin; and abated the wrath for Musa;

O Thou that gave glad tidings of (the Birth of) Yahya unto Zakaria;
