Tayseer Ahmed Al-Ajrami

It is noteworthy that the martyr hero had repeatedly asked for martyrdom in the past few months after giving his back to all worldly gains to the extent that he surveyed the target by himself then launched the attack. He managed to penetrate the Zionist security lines while attacking the most secured and the most fortified Zionist guarding posts.

The Qassam Brigades vow before you to continue along the road of Jihad and martyrdom until ejection of occupation from our lands Palestine.

And it is a Jihad until either victory or martyrdom
