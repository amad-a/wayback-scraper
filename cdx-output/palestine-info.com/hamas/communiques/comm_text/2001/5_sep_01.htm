With the grace of Allah the Qassam Mujahid hero:

 Ra’ed Nabil Al-Barguthi

From Abud village, Ramallah district

launched a martyrdom operation on Tuesday 4/9/01 at Al-Anbiyaa street in the heart of occupied Al-Quds after penetrating all enemy security lines and smashing the myth of its security apparatuses that claim incessant successes. The reprisal is merely the first in a series of Qassam operations that would force terrorist Sharon and his General Mofaz to admit their defeat in face of the Jihad and resistance determination. We tell Mofaz that although he escaped from Qassam bullets near Al-Quds last week, next week he won’t survive, God willing.

Finally, we present this operation as a special gift to Qassam Mujahid martyr Bilal Al-Ghoul who sacrificed himself to protect his father … wait for Qassam’s next surprise.

Wallahu Akbar wa Lillah Al-Hamad
