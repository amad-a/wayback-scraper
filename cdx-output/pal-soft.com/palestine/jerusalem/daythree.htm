Day Three

 
Day 3 : 30.09.2000

The  total  of  Palestinian casualties in the third day of the Al-Aqsa
Intifada has risen to 22 martyrs, seven on Friday and 15 on Saturday ,
Four others clinically dead. The number of injuries is more then 500 .

Israeli  occupation authorities used heavy machine guns and anti-tanks
missiles against peaceful Palestinian protesters near Gaza town.

The  French  Television  today prodcast a video shows Israeli soldiers
opened  fire for more then 45 minutes at an 12 Years old boy (Mohammed
el-Durah)  and  his  father  who was trying to shield his son from the
Israeli soldiers bullets , both has been directly shoed , the son died
in  the spot and the father critically wounded . An ambulance driver (
Bassam  el-Balbesi ) was also killed while trying to rescue them , and
a second ambulance driver was wounded.

In  the  West  Bank,  violent  demonstrations  have  been  reported in
Al-Qodus, Ramallah, al-Khalil (Hebron), Bethlehem and Ramallah.

A  general  strike and mourning prevailed throughout the West Bank and
Gaza Strip in protest against the Zionist crimes at Al-Aqsa mosque .
