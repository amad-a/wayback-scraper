Day Forty Eight 

Day 48 : Tuesday 14.11.2000

The  Israeli  occupation army has imposed a hermetic closure on Palestinian
towns in the West Bank and Gaza Strip, barring Palestinians from travelling
to or from major population centers.

Since   the  start  of  Al-Aqsa  Intifada,  the  Israeli  army  killed  213
Palestinians and wounded as many as 9000 people.
