Day Fifty Seven

Day 57 : Thursday 23.11.2000

Israeli  army  targeted  a  car  on  a main street in the West Bank city of
Nablus  by  a  missile  fired from a helicopter and hit the car killing its
driver,  the  Hamas  activist  Ibrahim  Bani Odeh. Oded was freed yesterday
after three years in a Palestinian prison.

Two  Palestinians  were  killed  and  ten  others  were  injured in renewed
confrontations  with  the  Israeli occupation forces in the Gaza Strip. One
Palestinian was killed near the Beit Hanun crossing at the northern edge of
the  strip  while  the  second  died  of  gunshot  wounds in Rafah near the
Palestine-Egypt  borders.  Fifteen other Palestinians were reported injured
in the Gaza Strip, one of them seriously.

One  Israeli  soldier  was  killed and two others wounded when a small bomb
exploded near an Israeli army outpost.
