Day Forty 

Day 40 : Monday 06.11.2000

Three  more Palestinians were martyred by Israeli soldiers, Muahmmed Nawwaf
al  Ta'aban  (17  years  old) from Deir al-Balah, Wajdi Allam al Hattab (14
year  old)  from  Tulkarm,  Muhammed  al-Jazzar  (17  year  old ) died from
succumbed injuries he sustained last week.

Over  sixty  Palestinians  were  injured in confrontations with the Israeli
occupiers, three of them seriously.

Shifa  al-Hindi (16 years old), a Palestinian school girl lost her eyesight
after she was beaten mercilessly by two Israeli soldiers in East Jerusalem.

Here are some photos of today :

the  West  bank city of Hebron. (Photo 1)

Palestinians youths throw stones at an Israeli army post at the crossing of
Netzarim  Junction, southern Gaza City. (Photo 2)
