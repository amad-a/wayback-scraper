Day Thirty Seven

Day 37 : Friday 03.11.2000

Demonstration  continued  in  most  towns of Gaza Strip and west Bank after
Friday pray , some of this demonstration lead to clashes and live fire from
the Israeli army soldiers against the Palestinian protesters.

Three Palestinian martyrs, killed by Israeli soldiers, were laid to rest in
Jerusalem  and Gaza.

The  three  martyrs  are:  Mahmoud Abdul Jawad al Said (19 years old), Rami
Ahmed  Abdul  Fatah  Mutawi from village of Hezma (15 years old), and Nahed
Fathi Ahmed Al-louh (21 years old) from Deir Al-Balah in the Gaza Strip.

More  than 400 Palestinians were injured , seven of them sustaining serious
injuries.

For  the  five week , Israeli police and army prevent Palestinian aged less
then  45  years old from entering al-Haram al-Shareef for Friday pray and a
huge  number  of Palestinian have to pray of the streets of the old town of
Al-Qouds.
