PICTURES AND WORDS ABOUT OUR HOME IN PALESTINE

Welcome! Here I begin to record the incredible story. Few believe our witness of Israel's criminal actions against us. It has now been going on for over fifty years and has become a holocaust of monumental proportions. I hope the future will remember the intense hostility and fire we Palestinians receive for telling it. It will not always be so.
