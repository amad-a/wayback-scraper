Farewell Party for Mrs. Kristen Oren, Director of the NPA Office in Gaza
 
 
GCMHP organized a farewell party for Mrs. Kristen Oren, the Director of the Norwegian People’s Aid Office in Gaza, who will end her tenure soon.  Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj praised Mrs. Oren’s efforts in helping GCMHP and in the solidarity work with the Palestinian people.  He described her as a friend of the Program and the Palestinian people.

Mrs. Oren thanked Dr. Sarraj for his nice words and for the party.  She said that GCMHP is NPA’s biggest local partner, and that it has shown a high level of professionalism and efficiency.

The NPA new Director, Ms. Nora Ingdal attended the party in which everyone wished her good luck in her new post and a pleasant stay in Gaza.
