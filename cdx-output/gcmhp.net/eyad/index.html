Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj
 Welcome

Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj is the founder and director of the Gaza Community Mental Health
Programme (GCMHP). He is also the Commissioner-General of the Palestinian
Independent Commission for Citizens' Rights. He was born in Beersheva, Palestine,
and currently lives in Gaza. He is married and has two children, Sayf & Wasseem.
