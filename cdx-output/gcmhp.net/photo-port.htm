Views of Gaza Pier : 

 
Gaza old pier was the place from where residents and goods went in and out of Gaza via  sea . 
The roof of the old customs  building  can be seen in the middle of the picture .
 
 
This is a  new project  aiming  to provide a docking area for Gaza fishermen boats .
