An Australian Delegation visits GCMHP
 
On 19 October, an Australian delegation headed by Sir Ronald Wilson, a retired judge and Head of the Australian Council for Overseas Aid, visited the Gaza Community Mental Health Program. The guest met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Director, and Mr. Husam El-Nounou, PR Coordinator. Dr. El-Sarraj gave the visitors an overview of GCMHP activities and the different projects serving the Gaza population. In addition, he put the guest in light of the various psychosocial problems affecting the Palestinians as a result of the events of recent Palestinian history and the Israeli violations of Palestinian rights. Moreover, Dr. El-Sarraj answered questions on the daily life of Palestinians and the hardships they currently face. Finally, the visiting delegation toured the Program to get a first hand look at the activities of GCMHP and its different departments and projects.
