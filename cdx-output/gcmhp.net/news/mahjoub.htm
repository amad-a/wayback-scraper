Dr. Abdel Wahab Mahjoub
Professor in Social Psychology 
Head of the Psychology Department
Faculty of Human & Social Science
Tunis University I 
94 Bd. du 9 Avril Tunis, Tunisia
 
 
On 21 March 1998, Dr. Mahjoub started   a nother visit to GCMHP in the process of continuing the cooperation between Gaza Community Mental Health Programme (GCMHP) & Tunis University I .
