Policewomen's Graduation Ceremony
 
 
On the 27th September, 1998 the graduation ceremony for the "Training Police Women" course took place in the Palestinian Police headquarters.  The 8-month course, organized by the Women's Empowerment Project and Australian Red Cross, trained members of the female police force in areas related to women's issues and professional skills needed in dealing with women's problems in particular and community issues in general.

Gen. Ghazi El-Gabali, Head of Palestinian Police; Col. Fatima El-Birnawi, Head of the Female Police Force; Brig. Abdel Mo'ti El-Sab'awi; Ms. Shadia El-Sarraj, Director of the WEP, and Ms. Barbara Bill, Australian Red Cross, presided over the ceremony.
