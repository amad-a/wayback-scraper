A Delegation from the Danish International Development Agency (DANIDA) visit GCMHP
 

A Delegation form the Danish International Development Agency (DANIDA) concluded a visit to GCMHP. This visit was carried out to evaluate the "Rehabilitating Survivors of Human Rights Violations Project", one of the international projects funded by the Royal Danish Government.
The visiting delegation held extensive meetings with the administrative and medical staff at GCMHP to discuss the various work mechanisms and therapy techniques utilized. In addition, the guests discussed the training and research aspects of the work as well as conducting home visits to victims of torture and violence.
Moreover, the DANIDA delegation met PNA official among whom were General Nassir Yousef, the Head of the Palestinian Security Forces, and Colonel Mohammad El-Riffie, Head of the Prisons Service. In addition, they met representatives of local human rights organizations, notably the Palestinian Center for Human Rights and the Palestinian Independent Commission for Citizens' Rights. Moreover, the delegation visited some health care centers and human rights organizations in the West Bank.
