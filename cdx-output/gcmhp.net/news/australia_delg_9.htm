A Delegation from Australian Council of Churches visits GCMHP
 
 
On 26 September 1998, a 17-member delegation from the Australian Council of Churches visited the Gaza Community Mental Health Program.  The delegation met Mr. Husam El-Nounou, the Public Relations Coordinator, who briefed them on the different activities of the Program.  Mr. El-Nounou also put the visitors in view of the various psychosocial problems caused by the Israeli occupation and practices, afflicting the Palestinian community.
The guests expressed their admiration of the services that GCMHP offers.  They also conveyed their support to the Palestinian people in these difficult times.
