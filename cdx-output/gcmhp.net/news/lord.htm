Delegation from Britain visit GCMHP


 
 The delegation had a meeting with Dr. Eyad Sarraj, Director general of the Gaza community Mental Health Programme. They discussed the current peace process and its consequences on Palestinians every day life.They expressed their sympathy with people suffering and support for GCMHP's activities. The delegation were impressed by the progress achieved in the past few years.

The group went for a tour in Gaza Strip with Mr. Husam El-Nounou, PR Coordinator, to acquaint them with first hand information about the changes in Palestinian's living conditions in Gaza after Oslo.
