Delegation from SIDA & Palestine Solidarity Ass. , Sweden visit GCMHP


 
The visit aimed to discuss the ways of cooperation & support for the activities directed to Palestinian children and their families by GCMHP. 
The delegation have met Dr. Sarraj, the General Director of the programme and members of the Children Committee. They have also attended educational session for mothers about how to treat their children. 
The group went for a tour in Gaza Strip with Mr. Husam El-Nounou, PR Coordinator, to acquaint them with first hand information about the Palestinian's living conditions in Gaza. 
 
A Swedish delegation from SIDA  & Palestine Solidarity Association in Sweden visited   GCMHP on 24-25 April, 1998 .
