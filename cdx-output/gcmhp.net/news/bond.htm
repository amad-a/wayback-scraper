Dr. Barbara Harrell-Bond visits GCMHP
 
 
Dr. Barbara Harrell-Bond 
University of Oxford, teaches in the Post-graduate Diploma in Community Mental Health 
 
 
Dr. Barbara Harrell-Bond, university research lecturer and the founder of the Refugee Studies Program at the University of Oxford, came to Gaza to teach the “Culture and Mental Health” course in GCMHP’s two-year post-graduate diploma in community mental health.  Dr. Bond stayed in Gaza from 27 May – 25 June.

“All the staff of GCMHP can be extremely proud of the impressive achievements in eight short years.  The Diploma course is an important next step,” Dr. Barbara Harrell-Bond.
 
 
 Dr. Barbara Harrell-Bond, University Research Lecturer, Founder of the Refugee Studies Programme at the University of Oxford.
