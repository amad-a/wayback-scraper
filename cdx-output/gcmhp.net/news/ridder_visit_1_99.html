Dr. Denise De Ridder from Utrecht University visits GCMHP

 
Dr. Denise De Ridder, Associate Professor in Psychology, from the Department of Health Psychology from Utrecht University visited GCMHP from January 21to 26.

Dr. De Ridder has taught a course in Mental Health Promotion for the 2nd group of students at the Post graduate Diploma in Community Mental Health at GCMHP.
