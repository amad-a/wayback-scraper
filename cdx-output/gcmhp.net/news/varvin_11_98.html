Dr. Sverre Varvin, Psychological Center for Refugees,
University of Oslo Visits GCMHP
 
 
 
  
On 13 November 1998 Dr. Sverre Varvin from the Psychological Center for Refugees, University of Oslo, visited GCMHP.  For 5 days he was teaching the module entitled “Trauma and Stress” in the post graduate diploma in Community Mental Health, which is the module that Oslo University has agreed to teach as part of their contribution to the diploma program. 

In his comments Dr Varvin described the class as “a very good class.”
