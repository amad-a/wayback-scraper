Professors W. H. G. Walters and Henny Lodewijks of Utrecht University Present the "Child Psychology" Course in the Diploma

From 20 February - 3 March 1999, Professor W.H.G. Walters and Professor Henny Lodewijks of Utrecht University in the Netherlands presented the "Child Psychology" module to the 1999 group in GCMHP's Two-year Post-graduate Diploma in Community Mental Health.  This module was the first given to the new group of diploma students.  Local Trainer Mr. Rafiek Awadallah assisted Professors Walters and Lodewijks during the course.
