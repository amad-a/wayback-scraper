Graduation Ceremony for a Course
Organized by GCMHP and the Gaza College of Education in
“Mental Health Nursing”
 
 
On 2 November, the Gaza Community Mental Health Program and the Gaza College of Education held the graduation ceremony for the “Mental Health Nursing” course organized by the two institutions.

In his speech at the ceremony, Palestinian Minister of Health, Dr. Riyad El-Za’noun, stressed the importance of training health professionals in governmental and non-governmental organizations in order be able to cope with the problem of 80,000 disabled people in Palestine.  Also, Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana, Director of Training and Education at GCMHP, noted that statistics show that 70% of patients at primary health care centers suffer from psychosomatic symptoms of mental health problems.  He stressed the importance of coordination between organizations in training health professionals.

In addition to this course, which was funded by Bread for the World (Germany), GCMHP will organize similar course next year.
