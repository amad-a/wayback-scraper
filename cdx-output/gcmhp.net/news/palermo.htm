Research cooperation between GCMHP and  Palermo University in Italy
 

Dr. Stefania Vozza, Maria Patrizia Salatiello, Maria Pia Callari, and Dr. Francesco Grimald from Palermo University in Italy visited the Program from 20-25 July to discuss the possibilities of cooperation with the Research Department. The group discussed conducting a research project on “An Emotional Elaboration of Violence among Children of Two Uncomfortable Districts”. This research is part of the Italian Palestinian project to protect and cure children and adolescents who have been or might be victims of violence.
 
Dr. Samir Qouta, Research Director, met the group and agreed to conduct the research starting next year. A proposal for the project will be submitted to the European Union.
