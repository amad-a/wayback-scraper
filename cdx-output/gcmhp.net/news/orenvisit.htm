The New Director of the Gaza Office of Norwegian People’s Aid Visits GCMHP
 
Ms. Kristen Oren, who has just ended her tenure as the Director of the Norwegian People’s Aid (NPA) Office in Gaza, accompanied the new Director, Ms. Nora Ingdal on a visit to the Gaza Community Mental Health Program. The visit one of a series of visits to NPA partners by the new Director.
 
Ms. Oren and Ingdal met GCMHP Director Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj and the heads of departments at the Program. Current cooperation between the two organizations in addition to future cooperation were discussed in the meeting.
