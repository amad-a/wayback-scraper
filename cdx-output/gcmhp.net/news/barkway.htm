Dr. Pat Barkway from Flinders University Visits GCMHP
 

 Dr. Pat Barkway from Flinders University visited the Gaza Community Mental Health Program (GCMHP) from 27 June – 8 July to teach the “Community Mental Health” module in GCMHP’s post-graduate diploma in community mental health. The local trainer with Dr. Barkway was Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana, the Director of Training and Education Department.
