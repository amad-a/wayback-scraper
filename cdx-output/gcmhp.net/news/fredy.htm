Dr. Freddy Reddy Visits GCMHP
 
 
Freddy Reddy, M.D.
(Norway)
A member of the International Group Therapy Association
 
 
   
Dr. Freddy Reddy visited GCMHP from 10-18 April, 1998 to continue the process of conducting and supervising a 5-year "Group Psychotherapy Course" to train mental health professionals in group psychotherapy. All participants have high degrees in sociology, psychology, nursing, and medicine. The course started in late 1993.
