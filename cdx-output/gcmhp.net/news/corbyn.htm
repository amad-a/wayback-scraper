Jeremy Corbyn (British MP) Visits GCMHP

 
Mr. Jeremy Corbyn
A  member of Parliament, U.K.
Representing Parliamentary Human Rights Group
 
 
 
 
 
Mr. Jeremy Corbyn visited GCMHP to show solidarity for the Palestinian people and human rights activists, and to get firsthand information on the Palestinian situation.

In his visit to the Programme, He said that he was impressed with the strength of commitment and understanding of the huge problems faced by Palestinians.
