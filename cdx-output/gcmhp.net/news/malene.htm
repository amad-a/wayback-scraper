RCT Program Coordinator Visits GCMHP
 
From 4-7 October, Ms. Melene Mikkelsen, the Program Coordinator in the Research Council for Torture Victims (RCT) in Denmark, visited GCMHP.  During her visit, Ms. Mikkelsen conducted a series of meetings and discussions with the senior staff of the Program in order to enhance the cooperation between the RCT and GCMHP over the next two years.  In addition, Ms. Mikkelsen and members of the "Rehabilitating Survivors of Human Rights Violations" project developed a three-year proposal for the project.  Moreover, agreement was reached over cooperation between GCMHP and RCT in the fields of therapy, Training, and research.  It is worth mentioning that RCT has been one of the major donors for the "Rehabilitating Survivors of Human Rights Violations" project.
