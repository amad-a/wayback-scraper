Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj  participated in the
 Conference on the recent situation in the Middle East and Palestine on the 4th of April 1998 in Milan.
 


Mr. Lino Zambrano, representative of Milan Committee in Italy, has invited
Dr. El-Sarraj to participate in the Salam Children of the Olive Tree Organization's conference on the recent situation in the Middle East and Palestine on the 4th of April 1998 in Milan.
The conference took place for one whole day; a number of Italian and Palestinian figures gave numerous speeches. Around one hundred people have attended this conference.
Dr. Sarraj spoke about civil and political rights, the Peace Process, role of the governmental organizations, Legislative Council and civil society.

Dr. Sarraj has commented that the peace process was worse than a war of attrition with deteriorating economical and social life. He asserted that the Israeli government of Netanyahu is determined to kill the peace process in order to keep the land. If that happens, Netanyahu will only be honest to himself and his intentions, he added.

Dr. Sarraj has stressed the importance of the immediate separation of authorities and criticized the legislative branch control over the legislative and judiciary; he also called for the freedom of the press. Dr. Sarraj mentioned the possible scenarios of establishing police state after the death of Arafat, the re-entry of Israeli military occupation and the Jordanian option. In all, Dr. Sarraj emphasized; Palestinians will have to endure yet more suffering and violation of their basic human and political rights.

Dr. Sarraj mentioned that Mr. El Shereef killers are well known if we follow the Israeli track record.
