The Federal Republic of Germany Signs an Agreement to Fund the Palestinian Independent Commission for Citizens’ Rights
 
On 30 June, Dr. Horst Freitag, the Head of the Representative Office of the Federal Republic of Germany, met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Commissioner-General of the Palestinian Independent Commission for Citizens’ Rights (PICCR), to sign an agreement to fund the Commission. Under the terms of the agreement, the Government of Germany will fund PICCR activities in the areas of defending citizens’ rights and fighting human rights violations in the Palestinian areas in order to ensure the respect of law and public freedoms.
 
This is the second agreement between the Government of Germany and PICCR signed in the last two years. Dr. Freitag stated that Germany has great faith in the Commission’s work in human rights; and indicated that Germany will always extend moral and financial support to ensure the rule of law and respect of people’s rights.
 
Dr. El-Sarraj thank the Government of Germany for its continuous support and stated that there will be no stopping to the work to ensure justice, the release of prisoners, respecting court decisions, preventing torture, freedom of expression, and other basic rights. At the end of the meeting, Dr. El-Sarraj gave the guest PICCR’s Third Annual Report.
