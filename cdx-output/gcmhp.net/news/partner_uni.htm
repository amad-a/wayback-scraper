GCMHP signs a Partnership Agreement with the Suez Canal University
 

A GCMHP delegation consisting of Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana, and Mr. Haitham Abu Shaaban signed a partnership agreement with the School of Medicine at the Suez Canal University in Egypt.

The five-year agreement outlines coordination between the two institutions in research and post-graduate studies in the fields of psychiatry and neuropsychiatry.  It was agreed to facilitate the enrollment of students of GCMHPâ€™s post-graduate diploma in community mental health in the post-graduate programs in psychiatry and neuropsychiatry at the Suez Canal University.

In addition, the two parties agreed to strengthen academic expertise and exchange in the areas of community mental health.  The University agreed to allow GCMHP staff to train at University hospitals, to transfer chronic cases to University hospitals, and offered to treat GCMHP staff members and their families at the University hospitals on the same scale as Egyptian citizens.
