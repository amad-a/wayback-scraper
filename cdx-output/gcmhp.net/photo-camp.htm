The Beach Camp : 

 
 
Beach Camp lies on the northwestern  corner of  Gaza city . It is one of the largest  refugee Camps housing approximately 60,000  refugees . The Camp was established  by the UN and most of its buildings  one temporary  asbestos - covered dwellings .
