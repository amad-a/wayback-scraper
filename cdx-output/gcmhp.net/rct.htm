RCT Visit to GCMHP


 
From 8 March to 12 March 1998, a delegation from the Rehabilitation and Research Centre for Torture Victims (RCT) in Denmark visited GCMHP as a step in the continuing cooperation between the two organizations; especially in the field of rehabilitating  survivors of human rights violations. 
 
 

Dr. Edith Montogomery and Ms. Malene Mikkelsen had the following comments at the end of their visit:
 
Gaza Community Mental Health Programme   
is on of the most dynamic and well-driven projects I have ever visited.
