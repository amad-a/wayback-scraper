This institution is a Palestinian medical, developmental NGO working in the West Bank and Gaza Strip. Since 1979 it has been providing services to the poor in remote places who have no access to medical establishments in their areas of residence. Its projects include primary health care centers, women and child health projects, and rehabilitation. It has more than 30 centers in the West Bank and Gaza
