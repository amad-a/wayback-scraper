A Delegation from the European Parliament Visits Palestine

As part of their visit to Palestine, a delegation from the European Parliament visited Gaza on 21 November 2000. The delegation consisted of the following members of the United Left-Nordic Green Left group of the Parliament: Mrs. Luisa Morgantini, Mr. Francis Wurtz, Mr. Kostas Alyssandrakis, Mrs. Yasmine Boudjennah, Mrs. Pernilla Frahm, and Mr. Alain Krivine. In the headquarter of the Palestinian Red Crescent Society for the Gaza Strip, the guests met Dr. Haidar Abdel Shafi, Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Mr. Raji Sourani, Mr. Faisal Hourani, and Mr. Jamal Zaqout. The meeting centered on the situation in the Palestinian areas and the Israeli atrocities committed against the Palestinian people. The meeting pointed to the occupation and Israeli racism as the root causes of the crisis in the region.

The European delegation expressed its solidarity with the Palestinian people and their struggle for freedom and just peace, pointed to their intention to more action in the European Parliament to support Palestinians.
