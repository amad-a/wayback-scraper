Dr. Kristi Oskarsson Teaches the "Culture" Module in the Diploma

From 7-18 April, Dr. Kristi Oskarsson from Olso University (Norway) lectured in the "Culture" module for first-year students in the postgraduate diploma in community mental health and human rights. Mr. Samir Zaqout was the local trainer in the lectures, which covered the following topics:

  • What is culture?
  • How does a culture define mental health?
  • Ethnocentrism
  • Gender issues
  • Social network
  • Culture-bound disorders
  • Traditional understanding of mental health disorders
  • Traditional healers
