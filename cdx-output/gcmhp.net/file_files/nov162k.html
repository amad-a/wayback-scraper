Delegation from the South African Representative Office to the PNA Visits GCMHP

Dr. Bishara Shaheen and Mr. Ashraf Suleiman from the Representative Office of South Africa to the PNA visited GCMHP on 16 November 2000. The guests met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj and expressed their solidarity with the Palestinian people during the current crisis. Dr. El-Sarraj talked to the visitors about the difficulties facing the Palestinian people and the effects of the closures and violence on the psychosocial well being of the population.

The guests expressed the official stance of South Africa towards the Palestinian-Israeli conflict in the following points:
- The importance of resolving the conflict through negotiations.
- The final status solution should be based on UN resolutions 242 and 338.
- The need for an international inquiry into the causes of the clashes as stated in UN resolution 1322.
- The withdrawal of Israeli forces to the positions of 28 September 2000.
- Respecting the sanctity of holy sites.
- Stopping all forms of collective punishment.
- The UN plays an important role in protecting world peace; and the
Secretary General should play an active role in the Middle East.
- European and Middle Eastern Countries should make solid contributions towards the peace process.
