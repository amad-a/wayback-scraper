American Delegation Visits GCMHP

On 25 February 2001, Ms. Souad Dajani, Project Coordinator for Grassroots International, and Mr. Iain Guest, Project Coordinator for Advocacy Project, visited GCMHP. They met Dr. Eyad El-Sarraj, Director-General, Ms. Christina Abdel Shafi, Project Officer, Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana, Deputy Director for Administrative and Financial Affairs, and Mr. Husam El-Nounou, PR Coordinator. Dr. El-Sarraj gave the guests an overview about the activities of the Programme in general including the activities of the recently established crisis intervention team. The meeting also discussed the psychological and social effects of Israeli violence, the Intifada, and the policy of closures on Palestinians.

The guests are planned to visit the GCMHP clinic in Jabalia to meet the team there, and conduct a home visit to a victim of human rights violations in Jabalia. It is worth mentioning that Grassroots International partially funds the activities of the human rights activities in the Jabalia clinic
