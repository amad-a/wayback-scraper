"Democracy and Human Rights" Workshop at GCMHP

On 27 and 28 March, the Human Rights team at GCMHP and the Palestinian Independent Commission for Citizen's Rights (PICCR) held a two-day workshop entitled "Democracy and Human Rights" at the Programme's main office in Gaza City. The workshop was held for members of GCMHP's human rights teams.

The first day included the following lectures:

  • "Psychological Effects on Human Rights Professionals" by Anwar Wadi (GCMHP Psychologist).
  • "International Human Rights Law" by Mazen Shaqoura (PICCR Director in Gaza)
  • "International Law regarding People" by Isam Younis (Director of Al-Mezan Center for Human Rights)

The second day included the following lectures:

  • "International Mechanisms for Protecting Human Rights" by Mazen Shaqoura
  • "Separation of Authorities" by Hamdi Shaqoura (Head of the Democracy Unit in the Palestinian Center for Human Rights)
  • "Rule of Law" by Basim Bushnaq (PICCR lawyer)
