Let peace come to our time

Dear Friends
Has time arrived at last for us to live in peace?.
Is it a coincidence that the three religions of the Middle east are simultaneously celebrating, Xmas, Hanukah, and Eid Alfitr Almubarak.?
Let us make it happen
Don't let the forces of evil take it from us.
Call on leaders to show courage and vision.
We had enough of war, brutality, humiliation, and death.
This is a beautiful country, now divided with hatred and fear. One day it will be reunited.
And we will be
Tall with dignity, not with guns
Flying in freedom , but within law
Content in peace, away from surrender

For all that to happen, we have first to learn a new kind of power , the power of winning through losing, the power of grieving not dying.
God bless the people who are the makers of peace.

Eyad El-Sarraj
