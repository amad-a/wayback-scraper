Delegation from Catholic Relief Services Visits GCMHP

On 3 April 2001, a delegation from "Catholic Relief Services" headed by Ms. Christine Tucker visited GCMHP. The guests met Mr. Abdel Hamid Afana and Husam El-Nounou. Discussion centered on the general situation of the Palestinian people and the difficulties they face as a result of the military occupation and economic hardships, and their effects on the mental health of the population, especially children. In addition, Mr. Afana and Mr. El-Nounou informed the guests of the Program's pressing needs, such as training teachers, student counselors, and parents on how to deal with the psychological and behavioral problems of children in the preschool and school levels.
