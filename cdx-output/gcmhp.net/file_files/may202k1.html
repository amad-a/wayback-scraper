Since the eruption of Al-Aqsa Intifada, the economic and social hardships of the Palestinian people have been accentuated. This has deprived many people from the ability to cover the expenses of their treatment at UHWC's Al-Awda Hospital in Jabalia, which offers its services at low prices.

Driven by our responsibility towards our people and to ensure medical care for all that need it, UHWC has doubled the percentage of cases treated for free at the hospital. However, this measure has taken its toll on the Al-Awda's budget, and is threatening its ability to continue to provide services.

We urgently turn to you to help us through our "Patients' Support Fund" in order to ensure the continuity of our services in an area that suffers from a severe lack of qualified medical services. The daily operating cost for one bed is 200 New Israeli Shekels (NIS); and the monthly cost is 6,000 NIS (approximately $US 1,500).
