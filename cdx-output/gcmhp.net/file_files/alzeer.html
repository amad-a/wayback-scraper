GCMHP's Mohammad El-Zeer Attains MA from the Islamic University

On 26 May 2001, Mohammad El-Zeer (GCMHP Community Mental Health Specialist) successfully defended his MA entitled "Long-term Effects of Torture on Palestinian Prisoners and their Relation to some Variables" in the Islamic University. After the defense, Mr. El-Zeer was awarded the MA degree in psychology. The study called for strict laws outlawing torture in Palestinian society. It also called for periodic checkups on survivors of torture in order to make sure that they do not suffer from the effects of torture.
