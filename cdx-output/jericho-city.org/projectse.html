Reconstruction of the old bridge near the ministry of local government in Tawfiq Zayad Street:

 this was as part of a grant donated by the German Development Fund KFW, within the United Nations development program UNDP, the total cost for this project will reach up to 200000 $ and it will be executed in 4 months. This step was one of the prime objectives of the Municipality especially after many residents living near by suffered a lot from water floods during wintertime.

Rehabilitation of the Bridge terminal:

This project will be executed with funding of the Japanese government within the UNDP, the total cost will be 750000$ for executing the first phase of the project

And that is the reconstruction and extension of the departure hall and the construction of Public facilities and public services and information offices.

This project will contribute in facilitating the process of travelling through Al-Karama Bridge, and reduce the overstuffing of people, as the new hall will be prepared to receive a larger number of travelers. 20% of the work has been executed till now where the old hall was demolished and infrastructure for the new hall was established.

The Construction of Classrooms in Al-Buhturi school:

The Jericho Municipality has signed an agreement with PICDAR for funding the construction of three classrooms in Al- Buhturi School with total cost of 60000 $, this was after The Municipality has prepared all the schemes and tenders needed for the execution. During this period, the Municipality forwarded a proposal for the world bank through PICDAR for an additional funding of this project where an additional class was added, so the total cost reached up to 72000$.

This project will contribute in reducing the problem of overcrowds of students in classrooms that lead to the creation of a night shift which increased the hardships of students especially during the summer.

The rehabilitation and the paving of the eastern entrance to Jericho (Amman Street):

after obtaining a donation of 294000 Euro donated by the European Union through PICDAR. The Municipality commenced the execution of paving the eastern entrance of Jericho, this project includes the repaving and widening of the street until it reaches the entrance to the bridge terminal. In addition to that, there will be sidewalks and street islands installed, after the execution of this project the Municipality will be finishing the rehabilitation of the whole street where the other part was paved during the last years. This project is considered the first phase of the whole plan to rehabilitate and pave Ein El-Sultan Street until it reaches the old Jericho Site.
