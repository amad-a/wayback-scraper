Honorable chairman

Ladies and Gentlemen

Dear colleagues and honorable Mayors,

Back in the ancient times, they used to say that health is a crown placed on the heads of healthy men and visible only to ill people. The reason behind the establishment of these checkpoints is to increase the pressure on the Palestinian people through creating more sufferings and tortures.

Honorable, ladies and gentlemen,

What is the next step now after more than 807 UN resolution in favor of the Palestinian cause, and what to do next after the recent poll showed 60% of the European people believe Israel to be as top threat to world peace more than Korea and Iran.

Honorable, ladies and Gentlemen,

In Palestine, we have found the lights of freedom through the twinning relations established between Palestinian and international cities especially the European cities. This light that we are seeking is at the end of the dark tunnel, an outlet of hope by which we could ease the pressure and lessen the suffering of our people. fulfill some of its humanitarian needs from one side and inform the world about the brutality of the Israeli practices against our people and the real amount of suffering from another side.

We seek to mobilize the efforts of peace and freedom lovers to support our legitimate rights that are recognized by the United Nations.

We in Palestine consider Europe and specially the northern European countries to be at the head of this effort that in the end should lead to recognizing the lost rights and the implementation of the international resolutions.

 

Thank you all

And may the light of freedom shines

The Opening Of CD Section In Jericho Municipal Library

In March 18 , 2003, and as part of the Municipal library in Jericho, a CD section in the Municipal library was officially opened under the disposal of the subscribers; the opening was attended by a number of concerned parties, school teachers, library subscribers, municipal council members, and municipality personnel. Deputy Mayor, Dr. Ali Husseini, has cut the strip declaring the opening of the CD section and all the guests took a tour around.

Project concept:
The idea came up as there was a rising need among the young people in the city of Jericho of using Hi- Tec and a necessity of facilitating information access for everyone. Especially to an important group and that is children and youth, the municipality desire was to be in a leading position in the Information, Technology, and Computer Field. For this reason the administration thought that adding a CD section in the municipal library is an essential step, as this kind of technology is distinguished from any other being the language of the world. During a visit of the head of the information technology section to the city of Nantes in France within a cooperation program with French municipalities, the idea was proposed and adopted by the Jerusalem- Gaza Loirre association. This association kindly donated a sum of 15000 French Franks for implementing this project.

 

Procedures for implementing this project:
As soon as the invoice for purchasing the CDs was issued and was sent to the Loirre association for approval, a set of steps were taken as soon as money was transferred, where there was an invitation for a bid. And under the supervision of The Mayor Mr.Abdel-Karim Sider and in cooperation with the IT and purchasing section a set of exclusive CDs was purchased from a high standard CD company. In addition to a CD writer and Empty CDs.
500 different CDs where purchased covering various topics and each one had a similar copy issued.
From the other hand, the Jericho Municipality contributed in paying a sum of 3000 NIS about 600$ US for designing and working out a CD cabinet, for exhibiting the CDs and facilitating the process of Choosing and borrowing. Two computers were put under the disposal of the library subscribers for the use of these CDs inside the library.


The Targeted Group:
The CDs where chosen to suit the users from the ages of 6 – and above.

Types of CDs:
1- Scientific – 25 CD
2- Cultural and recreational – 54 CD
3- Recreational for children – 15 CD
4- Children – oriented ( educational, games) -53 CD
5- Applied programs and photos – 27 CD
6- religious – 50 CD
7- Historical and Geographical 15 CD
8- Medical – 12
9- children And Fun – 8 CD

The borrowing procedures:
A special procedure for lending the CDs was used , it was adopted as to suit the municipal library policies, it involves the necessity of the borrower to be a member in the library.
The membership card will be claimed and the fact that this particular member intending to borrow a CD is listed in his membership card. In case this CD was damaged or lost the borrower should pay a fine of about 12 NIS (2.5 $ U.S) a copy of this damaged CD will be made in the Library, in the Future, the Municipality thinks of installing a digital lending system.

Classification of the CDs:
The CDs are classified according to the topic and programs. In accordance with a special classification system that was implemented by the Library personnel, a special colored sticker was fixed on the CD cover to indicate the topic it belongs to, which will help reaching the wanted CD.

This step has made the Jericho Municipality a pioneer as the municipal library will be the only one in the city that offers such services, this section will help in expanding the intellectual capacities of children, students, and adults and will help in communicating information.

Future plans:

As the Jericho Municipality has recently signed an agreement for the establishment of a new library with ANERA (Near east refugee Aid) project funded by USAID it is expected that such a CD section will be expanded more and will include more CD topics and specialized programs.
