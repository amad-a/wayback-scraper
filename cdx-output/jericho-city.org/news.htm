Including to all that, special Cultural and Tourist schedule has been discussed with The French Culture Minister that's to say, training tourists Guides, marketing for Jericho City in all over France, start in a special programs to train hotel owners and workers, to train souvenirs, and to establish a special French/Palestinian Friendship house for youths.

Even though the discussion took on its consideration the Jericho Pubic Library which will be supported by French Government through the special agreement signed with Benny Municipality in France.

Palestinian uprising (Intifada)


The Palestinian uprising is a public Revolution which includes all the parties of Palestinian society who participate in this uprising against Israeli Occupation to the Arab land for more than 50 years a go and continued until today.

Throughout these long years Palestinians suffered all kinds of oppression, Depression and torture, but the last crime made by Sharon was the reason for the new Uprising..

Actually, Palestinian people wanted peace particularly, when the "Oslo" agreement was signed between Israelis and Palestinians in 1993, during that time Palestinians believed that the Israeli occupation on the Palestinian land will be vanished and removed therefore Palestinians will achieve their dream of establishing their independent state on all Palestinian land which has been occupied since 1967 and Jerusalem.its Capital

On the contrary, since the Oslo agreement Israelis had increased their oppression on Palestinian people. Settlements were increased, Israelis demolished our houses, and also they refused to release Palestinian prisoners.

Nowadays because of this uprising the Israelis soldiers put the Palestinians under siege, also Israelis denied Palestinian workers from entering the Green line and restricted their movement.

Actually, all of these measures made by Israeli Government caused disappointments and depressions among Palestinian people who preferred being dead and to be martyrs than this difficult situation, so the second uprising started it thus will continue until the freedom and liberation, which all the Palestinians aspire.

We hope that this Intifada give us the opportunities to achieve our goals in establishing our independent State.

Jericho Municipality participates in Italian week in Palestine for Peace in the Middle East

On Sep 5-6. 2000 Jericho Municipality participated in the Italian Week in Palestine, the events took place in Palestine on Sep 2. 2000 until Sep 9. 2000, Jericho Municipality gave its effort, solidarity and support to the project which was organized by the Italian Coordination of Local Authorities for Peace and it was planned to support the Peace in the Middle East and Bethlehem 2000. Events in Jericho Municipality took place on Sep 5 and Sep 6. 2000 in which many Italian groups, Musicians, actors and other groups had part in the events.

 

Jericho Municipality sings a Twinning Agreement with Pisa Municipality of Italy

On Sep 26. 2000 a Delegation from Jericho Municipality headed by the Mayor Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr visited the Municipality of Pisa in Italy in a official visit to sign a Twinning agreement between the two Municipalities, the signing ceremony took place in the Belliari hall on Sep 28. 2000, the ceremony was attended by many high ranking officials from both Military and Civilian sectors including the Diplomatic bodies in Pisa.
In his speech Mayor of Jericho presented the importance of such agreement in all levels for the sake of the the two peoples, also he expressed his sincere thanks on behalf of the Jericho City Council and the people of Jericho, including to that Mayor of Jericho gave a wide and extensive explanation about the current Political, Economical situations in Jericho. He assured the historical and religious importance of Jericho which is the Lowest and the Oldest City in the World and it was the passageway of Jesus Christ for five times and he resides in it for forty days where he challenged the evil and fasted for forty days. thus Jericho gained its reputation because its the passageway of Jordan River and the Dead Sea.

Police School & College was bombed in Jericho

On Oct 12. 2000 the IDF Warriors and Military Abachi Choppers fired 4 missiles at the Police College in Jericho City, three missiles hit the main buildings of the college and one missed its target, luckely no one was injured since everybody was evacuated after hearing the choppers flying above the buildings, this act by the Israeli Forces was condemned by the Palestinian Authority and its branches all over.



Jericho Municipality has been awarded the Total Quality Management Certificate ISO 9002

From our faith in improvement and continous development of the services that are provided to the residents of Jericho by using modern administrative procedures under the umbrella of the International High Quality the Municipality started to take steps into implementing the idea and working it out. at the beginning of 1999 the idea was formulated and actions took place, in June. 1999 the First official contact was made with the Industrial Details Company " a specialized company in the ISO issues". Many training courses were made to the Departments Heads, officials and workers, they attended lots of sessions, and many paper work was made, new plans and new procedures depending on the rules of Total Quality Management ISO 9002.

On Thursday May 18. 2000 The Final inspection on implementing the rules and instructions of the Total Quality Administrations was made in all Departments and Sections by a specialized Commitee formed from SGS International for the ISO and Total High Quality and then the Commitee recomended that Jericho Municipality should gain the Certificate of the ISO 9002.

 

Jericho Municipality Signs the contract of Ein Sultan Irrigation Rehabilitation Project

In a special ceremony held at the Municipal Theater on May 23. 2000 and was attended by Dr. Saeb Ereqat the Minister of Local Government, His Excellency Mr. Wu Jiuhong The Chinese Ambassador for Palestine, Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr Mayor of Jericho, Representatives of the Chinese Company for Water and Electricity, Municipality Staff and many farmers from Jericho and the surrounding areas, during the Ceremony Dr. Ereqat eulogized the efforts and assistance that is given to the Palestinians by the Donor Countries and he cherished the roll of the Belgium Government in financing this essential and important project.

The Chinese Ambassador expressed the position of the Chinese Government in supporting the Palestinian Rights and he re-assured the great and Historical relation between China and Palestine.

Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr the Mayor of Jericho said that the Project will be implemented in cooperation with IFAD and within two months the Chinese Company for Water and Electricity will start the Project which is expected to be over in ten months. and he added that this project will increase the share of water for every farmer in Jericho thus increasing the products.

 

UNICEF signs the Agreement of Financing the activities of the Children Municipal Council for the year 2000

On June 1. 2000 Ms. Marelina Vivianni the Special Representative of UNICEF in Palestine signed together with Mr. Abdel-Karim Sidr Mayor of Jericho the agreement of financing the activities of the Children Municipal Council for the year 2000, Mr. Sidr expressed his sincere gratitude for the perfect and marvelous action taken by UNICEF in supporting the idea of the Children Municipal Council which considered the first in Palestine and was transformed into reality.

He added that the budget for the year 2000 reach the amount of $40,000 which will be distributed on several activities and programs made and planned by the Children City Council's Members.

12 year old Ismat Ali Huseini, a member of the Children Municipal Council reviewed the idea from the beginning, the importance of establishing the Children Municipal Council and its positive effect on both children and participants.
