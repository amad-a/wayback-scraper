29/10/2003

Is’af Al- Nashashibi library donates a copy of the glossary script for the Jericho Municipality

Dar Is’af Al- Nashashibi A library for cultures, arts, and literature donated a copy of the script glossary to the Jericho Municipality, this came as a way to strengthen the relations between the researches institution and to be able to lead the students during their searches for any rare scripts in the Library.

These scripts are considered one of the valuable and rare books that could facilitate the work of researchers and students by providing the titles of all books in the library in addition to the author, date of the publishing, Publisher, and the introduction and ending. It also provides notes about the book status and specifications pertaining to number of pages, measurements, writing style, cover, and the resources.

Currently, these scripts are considered part of the Palestinian cultural heritage especially in Jerusalem with a total number of titles reaching up to 780 . whether a text or a letter within a group of 294 registered under 780 serial numbers. It is worth mentioning that Dr. Issac Al- Huseini had donated these manuscripts gathered from a number of Jerusalem families and placed at Al-Dar Library in 1986.

Its also worth mentioning that a lot of books and old and valuable references were added to the Jericho Municipal library in many different languages in addition to establishing a CD library containing discs on many interesting subjects.

This step was undertaken to enrich the library in important books that will help students and researchers to prepare their studies and researches.

The Maintenance department in Jericho Municipality has conducted some adjustments on the designs of some offices in the Municipality building aiming at extending them for creating a work relieving atmosphere and increasing administrative monitoring.

This adjustment included demolishing the walls that separate the employees attending these offices, which makes it more convenient for them and makes it possible to increase the administrative monitoring which will reflect on reaching a high performance and providing the best services and creating an atmosphere for encouraging creativity.

9-7-2003

A delegation from Bonuille conclude a visit to the Jericho Municipality and commitment to carry on cooperation between the two sides

The delegation from Bonuille declared in the end of the visit they made to the Jericho Municipality a will to organize a week for support and solidarity with the Palestinian people in November.

These steps for enhancing the relation with the city of Jericho were declared in the evaluation meeting convened with participation of the Mayor of Jericho. This meeting was organized after some visits made for a number of local organizations and institutions in the city.

The final meeting convened was with presence of the Mayor Mr. Abdel- Karim Sider and the French delegation composed of Mr.John Michelle Deputy Mayor of Bonneuille for Youth and sports affairs, Daniella Deputy Mayor for Social affairs, Patrick the technical Consultant, and Mrs. Nuha Rishmawi Head of the Palestinian Antiquities friendship association. This meeting resulted in some steps regarding cooperation between both parties where an agreement was reached to consider the idea of providing study grants for two students finishing school. In addition to that the Municipality of Bonneuille is willing to recruit a technical consultant for the Municipality of Jericho. The delegation also proposed organizing the solidarity and support week for the p[alestinian people where the city of Jericho will be the focal point.

From his side, the Mayor of Jericho said that this visit is considered the first step on the way of starting real cooperation and he explained the hardships that are faced by the Palestinian people and added that the struggle of the Palestinian people will continue regardless the pressure it is subjected to in order to establish a civil and institutional community that will realize social welfare for the citizens.

In this regard, Mr . Sider also said that the Palestinian people are lacking the experience for the institution of a civil society and here comes the role of French community in offering their help and experiences to help us achieving our goals and state. Mr. Sider commended the special relation that connects the two people hoping that it will extend to reach all sectors of the community.

In order to enhance the relation between the two Municipalities the Mayor of Jericho made a suggestion to form a joint committee to support the twinning agreement signed between both parties. This committee as he said may be declared in the Solidarity week. This committee will be assigned to follow up the conjoint projects and issues.

From his side, the French delegation expressed its happiness for this visit assuring the continuation in the cooperation relations regardless of the Israeli measures determining to go forward in the cooperation projects with the city of Jericho.

The delegation also added that their belief in working for the benefit of the Palestinian has increased especially after they have observed the Palestinian reality where they were found to have experiences that will make him come over any obstacle.

From the other hand, the delegation had carried out visits within the city where they met with a lot of institutions and local associations with coordination of the municipality. These were the Child municipal council, Workers union, Y.W.C.A, SIRA school, Peasant union, Woman Peasant union, Jericho Woman Society. They observed the needs of these associations and agreed on continuing cooperation and reciprocal visits by the coordination with other similar associations in France.

Regarding the cooperation with the child Municipal council, the delegation suggested conducting a meeting via the videoconference that will combine the child municipal council in Jericho and Bonnuille in a step that will enhance the cooperation and create a strong bond among the children in the two cities.

From the other side, the French delegation showed their willingness to assist the Municipality in qualifying employees in the municipality of Jericho in various aspects and in the field of water resources.

This visit that lasted for three days as an indication for the cooperation relations and as an implementation of the twinning agreement that bond the city of Jericho and Bonnuilee.

8/7/2003

Aiming at coordinating efforts to utilize the Municipality international relations, The Mayor of Jericho meets with a number of local institutions

The Mayor of Jericho Mr. Abdel- Karim Sider stressed in a meeting with the Local institutions in Jericho on the importance to explore new avenues and channels with the private community institutions in order to engage them in decision making.

This was considered a way for implementing the Municipality policy in insuring the participation of the institutions of the local community in the projects, activities and relations of the Municipality.

The Palestinian labor union, the Y.W.C.A, the peasant union association, the school of SIRA for disabled children, the Child Municipal Council, the Jericho women society, and the women’s Peasant association attended this meeting where they showed their willingness to work together with the Municipality.

Mr. Sider pointed out to the expected visit of a delegation from the city of Bonneuil from France to the city of Jericho, and he discussed with the attendees the organization of a visiting program that will include a visit to the above mentioned institutions. This will be for observing closely the needs and the nature of work these institutions are engaged in.

Within the cooperation relations that link both the Jericho Municipality and the Municipality of Bonneuil, Mr. Sider declared that two persons representing these institutions will be given the opportunity to participate in training courses in various fields.

In this regard, the Jericho Municipality is concerned in reaching the participation of the local community. Whether institutions or individuals, in the ideas and visions, that will result in the outcome of a mutual vision that will positively be reflected on the projects and services provided by the Municipality. This was enlisted within the Municipality Priorities, and broad lines of the Municipality policy.
