31-5-2003

Jericho Child Municipal Council Camps in the Jericho Stadium

 

The Jericho Municipality Personnel have set up tents on the Jericho Stadium for the Child Municipal Council camping activities. The 32 members of the council were engaged in a training course during the camping activities that lasted for two successive days and will be held at the end of each week. The training course consists of 30 training hours tackling subjects such as citizenship, children’s rights, human rights, communication, leadership skills, identifying the needs and priorities, and organizing and evaluation. This activity is carried out within the efforts exerted by the Municipality for supporting and backing up the elected child council and implanting the democratic values within children and assuring their participation in taking the responsibility since early stages of childhood. These ideas and hopes were declared in the workshop titled Jericho is a child friendly city that was organized by the Municipality in the beginning of February.where the Mayor of Jericho Mr. Abdel-Karim Sider launched some projects oriented towards children’s in the presence of the special representative of UNICEF Pierre Poupard , these projects included some recommendations in the field of childcare some of which are forming committees emerging from the child municipal council for monitoring the extent of implementing the special principles related to the child friendly city agreement in the city of Jericho.

Mr. Sider pointed out during this training course that the Municipality is concerned in developing the skills of the child municipal council members. And fulfilling their needs and interests and developing their talents and capacities making them capable of taking up responsibility for the community, aware of their rights as effective and equal citizens in the community. On the other hand, children expressed their strong interest in this activity, where they acquired some of the necessary life experiences. Such as the ability to chair and facilitates meeting efficiently, the ability to set plans and implement them and evaluate them, and the ability to identify the needs and priorities and evaluate activities and accomplishments. The children also expressed their understanding of the Municipality continuos efforts to support them and develop their talents whether through this training course or through supporting childcare programs and the establishment of Childcare Centers and clubs.

20/2/2003

The Jericho Municipality organizes a Workshop titled “Jericho, a Child Friendly City”

The participants in the workshop organized by the Jericho Municipality titled “ Jericho is a child friendly city” outlined the importance of the participation of the local community in all its institutions and activities in setting a large –scale strategy that will take into consideration children’s rights and needs. More than 30 participants representing all the institutions in Jericho city presented work sheets and suggestions about proposed ways for enhancing the concept of a child friendly city in Jericho, and the way that Jericho city could be distinguished from any other city in taking this path. And how these hopes and concepts and desire could be translated into real implemented projects. The workshop that was conducted in The Jericho resort hall for two successive days dealt with the main principles of a child friendly city conception. And its connection to Family and Parents, Health, Nutrition, Education, Environment, Security and Protection in the public spaces and facilities, Efficient Participation, Recreation, and Protection of children with special needs. It also discussed the impact of these factors on achieving the objectives linked to the idea of child Friendly City and the ability to set a broad strategy that will fulfill this goal and desired hopes.

 

Abdel- Karim Sider the Mayor of Jericho:

 

On the other hand, Mr. Abdel-Karim Sider the Mayor of Jericho assured that the Municipality would continue its efforts for implanting the concept of Jericho being a child friendly city. This is by launching projects concerned in children and assuring the participation of this group in the process of community build up, Also respecting their opinions and recognizing their rights and reinforcing the cooperation with all the parties and institution operating in this domain. Mr. Sider declared a number of steps that will be carried out by the Municipality for supporting children. These will include a meeting that will be conducted with the USAID to discuss the construction of a modern library on a piece of land about 1500 M2, the old library building with all its equipment as he said will become the responsibility of the Child Municipal Council in Jericho. He also pointed out that the Municipality would pay for the running cost for the old library.

Mr.Sider also added that the Municipality will start with a comprehensive survey for monitoring all the city’s pavements for rehabilitating and altering them as to be suitable for the use of handicaps and people with special needs. it will also dedicate a writing space in its own journal for the use of children and their activities. He also said that a study to rehabilitate the Municipality building is being conducted now to suit the needs of people with special needs and that a commission formed of children will assume its duties in monitoring the violations of children’s rights in the local community.

 

Special representative of UNICEF Mr. Pierre Poupard,

 

Mr. Pierre Poupard, the special representative of UNICEF in Palestine praised the outcome of this workshop, its goals and recommendations. He assured the willingness of his organization to fund some of the projects that were recommended in the workshop, also supporting the municipality in improving its capacities and personnel training and planning connected to children. He added that the UNICEF will help the child municipal council in Jericho in implementing of its proposed projects especially the project of installing safe signs in the streets of Jericho that is being implemented by the municipality. And improving the creativity atmosphere for children as general by funding internal and external child-oriented activities.

 

20/2/2003

 

The Jericho Municipality organizes a theatre show in the Kids Club

In the activities and performances hall in the Kids club building, the Jericho Municipality organized a theatre show for children entitled “Jerusalem and the young prince”. The idea of the show was about a small child dreaming about visiting the city of Jerusalem and getting aquatinted to its markets and holy and archeological sites. In that dream, he truly visited the city and met its people and was charmed by the magnificent places and he decided to settle down there and look for a job. And so, he collected a lot of information about the city’s religious and holy places such as dome of the rock and the resurrection church and the Burak wall. Finally, he worked as a tour guide in the city.

The show that took place on two periods was attended by 98 children of ages between 5-13 years old.

The show took the entire attention of the kids and strengthened their attachment and admiration of the city of Jericho and their dream to visit it without any restrictions.

1/2/2003

The Jericho Municipality Organizes an Open meeting that combined municipal employees ,The Mayor and municipal Council members of the city of Jericho

 

In an open meeting that combined the Municipal personnel and workers, and the Mayor and Municipal council members. And was attended by some of the officials and personalities in the city, Mr. Abdel-Karim Sider the Mayor of Jericho, emphasized on the uniqueness of this meeting that was designated for the personnel and workers who do not occupy high managerial positions. This is in order to express their problems and suggestions and points of views and directly forward them to the Mayor and Municipal Council Members for the purpose of reaching the sense of belonging of the workers and employees to their institution that will reflect on improving their job performance.

Mr. Sider also emphasized that this meeting will become a periodical meeting convened twice a year. And that this commitment from the Municipality side will be documented within the ISO procedures, he indicated that this meeting will be the beginning of other periodic meetings conducted with the residents in the city to list their problems as well.

From the other hand, the municipality personnel considered this meeting to be of great importance and emphasized the necessity of conducting more meetings of this kind.

Some of the important issues that were discussed by the employees were the wearing out of their wages and the necessity to link it with the high cost of living tabulate in addition to other necessary needs for the field workers such as special outfits and transportation and communication means. On the other hand, the directors of departments and heads of sections in the Municipality documented the meeting and demands and some of these needs were approved instantly and also later on an evaluation paper was made to evaluate this meeting. This meeting included many parts, some of which was a lottery for a large number of prizes, tribute prizes for the best internal magazine that is issued by the different departments in the Municipality and distribution of the municipality football series trophies and cups on the first and second leagues.

When the meeting was concluded, everybody had a dinner meal in the Spanish garden.

 

26/1/2003

The Jericho Municipality organizes a celebration on the occasion of announcing the results of the elections of the children municipal council
Within the activities organized by the Municipality in relation to the child municipal council, the Jericho Municipality has sponsored a party on the occasion of announcing the final results of the elections, which was held to elect the children’s council.
Many children attended the recital most of them were kids who took part in the elections as voters or nominees, these were students of the 7th, 8th, 9th grades in the city schools.
Furthermore, among the people who attended the party were the headmasters, supervisors, teachers, and Municipality employees who helped in facilitating the election process.
In the beginning of the celebration, the chairman Ali Jalaytah a student in the 9th grade in al Buhturi School presented a speech.
He thanked the UNICEF and the Jericho Municipality for funding and sponsoring this project. According to him, this project is in the benefit of children; it contributes in the creation of an open minded and educated generation capable of expressing itself clearly to the community it belongs to.

The party was organized in the Spanish park, and 500 kids attended it to listen to the recital performed by the English band “heart to heart”.
The band performed many Arabic and English songs for children followed by many entertainment and fun games in addition to offering small gifts for children.
 

The Municipality employees complete a training course in financial management and budget making

The Jericho Municipality finalizes a training course aiming at the development of the employee's skills working in different departments and sections in the Municipality. A graduation party was organized in the Municipality hall where employees were graduated in formal graduation ceremony sponsored by the mayor, Mr. Abdel-Karim Sider and the director of the Palestinian institute for the development of cadres, Dr. Osama samannah who supervised the course.
The course encompassed subjects such as financial management and analyses and practical applications in budget making in the amount of 24 training hours.
It was oriented to the directors and executives in the Municipality of which number reached up to 28 personnel.
