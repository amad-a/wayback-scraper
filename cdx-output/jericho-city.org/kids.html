Welcome to kids club

The clubs:

The Performances and activities club:

It will be concerned in organizing different activities for children (sports, musical, traditional, recreational, theatre, scouting, rangers, in addition to different and various social activities).

This club is equipped with a set of devices and educational means some of which are a TV set, Video, puppet theatre, and plenty of educational and mind urging games.

The arts club:

The main concern of this club is to develop and refine children's creative skills in the field of arts this will include (painting, handicrafts, acting, and theatre) there is a lot of workshops, painting studios, puppet theatre, and a number of different helping tools necessary for reaching this objective.

The computer club:

It aims on teaching children how to use computer devices and programming, developing their abilities in this field to make them capable of using the services and Hi- Tech such as the internet and e-mail, there is also 16 computers installed with printers and internet service.

The Culture club:

It concentrates on organizing different cultural activities and performances such as reading, narration, informational competitions, scientific research, cultural seminars, the art of writing, and dialogues. There are also books and educational means to encourage children to affiliate and be creative.
