City: Yache/ Romania

Date of signing the Twinning agreement: 16/7/2003

Fields of cooperation: Culture, science, education, exchanging students and technical specialists, economic and commercial cooperation, tourism.

 

City: Santa Barbara/ Brazil
 Date of signing Twining agreement: January 15, 1998
 Terms of agreement: Exchanging knowledge in the aspects of economy, trade, education, culture, sport, tourism, environment, health, and correspondences between the children of the two cities.

 City: Lardal/ Norway.
 Date of signing Twinning agreement: The start was in the spring of 1997,Agreement signed In July 20, 1998
 Terms of agreement: Mutual cooperation in all different aspects of life, social, cultural, youthful, athletic, educational, and agricultural. Also, exchanging experiences and skills among the two cities. In addition, focusing on the different activities related to youth, including hosting youthful delegations from Lardal and Jericho.
The agreement includes also the encouragement of the inhabitants of the two cities to spend vacations and activate tourism in the two cities. Also, the exchange of athletic delegations, cultural exchange, and exchanging experiences in the agricultural aspect.
Another point is to interchange experiences in the municipal services of the two municipalities.

 City: Ilyon/Greece.
 Date of signing Twinning agreement: September 8, 1999.
 Terms of agreement: Exchanging all aspects of life among the inhabitants of the two cities.


 City: Pisa/ Italy.
 Date of signing Twinning agreement: September 28, 2000.
 Terms of agreement: The exchange of cooperation Programs concerning economic, social, and cultural aspects. Also, the commencement of cooperation programs concerning the cultural, artistic, archaeological, and environmental heritage. Especially in science and sports and tourism. In addition to activating the youth role through cultural exchange.
 

City: San Giovanni Valdarno/ Northern Italy

Date of signing the twinning agreement: 18/10/2003

Fields of cooperation: Cultural- educational- handicrafts skills- sport delegations mainly football- childcare – Promoting tourism. 

City: Alessandria / Italy

Date of signing the agreement: 15/6/2004

Terms of agreement: cooperation in the field of public administration, economy, culture, education, tourism, cultural exchange, delegation exchange, social institutions, and school delegations



 City: Campinas/ Brazil.
 Date of signing Twinning agreement: November 28, 2001.
 Terms of agreement: On the popular level, to set an example for peaceful coexistence and development. Also to encourage solidarity through mutual programs to expand acquaintanceship. Another point is to exchange economic, educational, scientific, technological, health, cultural, athletic, and political aspects. In addition, to encourage commercial accords, to improve markets, and to implement productive projects prepared by the two municipalities. Also, to coordinate technical cooperation projects in addition to commercial marketing.
