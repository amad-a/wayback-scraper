Being the oldest city in the world, Jericho is very rich in historical sites. To visit all sites in Jericho and get adequate Infomation, you need at least two days tour program.

Jericho is unique because:

  • It is the oldest city in the world.

  • It is the lowest city in altitude in the world.

  • It is very rich in historical sites.

  • It's hot in summer for those who like hot weather, and warm and pleasant in winter.

  • Best dates, citrus fruits and banana in the world.

  • The first Palestinian city to gain independence.
