Jericho Municipality Welcomes you in the city of the moon....the oldest city in the world.....Jericho


     

               

 

City: Hiroshima/ Japan.
Beginning of relation: October 10, 1994.
Nature of relation: The municipality of Jericho is a member in “ mayors for peace organization” lead by the mayor of Hiroshima.
 


City: Fizbaden/ Germany.
Beginning of relation: 1994.
Nature of relation: Relations began with the city of Fizbaden when the city donated a Sum of money to the municipality of Jericho, the municipality of Jericho in turn offered this amount of money to the Jericho crescent football club, to contribute in building the club residency. It also donated a sum of money of about $50000 to help rehabilitating the city after the tornado that stormed the city of Jericho in 1997.this money was used to renew the pavements in the city. It also donated firefight-equipped clothing. This was also transferred to the firefight department and civil defense in Jericho.
The municipality of Jericho also hosted a delegation from the city of fizbaden together with a delegation from the Israeli City of (keffar saba) in the year 1997. Noting that the two cities (fizbaden and keffar saba ) are binded with sisterhood agreement. The delegation consisted of 100 representatives, where a friendly meeting was held to enforce relationships between the two cities.
