Jericho: the third term elections for the CMC conducted in a democratic way

The elections for the third term CMC in Jericho was organized with a lot of students participating reaching a percentage of 93% of all students in the Jericho city schools.

These students were 7th graders who have been engaged in the elections process and supervised by the Jericho Municipality, ministry of local authorities represented by the general director of the elections dept Mr. Abdel- Naser Mikki, the education dept, and supervisors from the participant 6 schools teachers. details
