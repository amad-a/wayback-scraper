The Jericho CMC participates in a workshop on Sports and development

The Jericho CMC participated in a workshop on Sports and development organized by the Ministry of higher education and UNICEF in Ramallah, the Canadian representative office and the Ministry of local government.

On her part, Aseel Al- Fityani the CMC chairwoman called in a speech on all children of the world to establish new cooperation relations and link with each other for the purpose of protecting the interests and rights of the Palestinian children.

Al- Fityani gave an illustration about the accomplishments achieved for children in Jericho. She also praised the Municipality role for supporting this democratic project as she said and complimented the UNICEF the official sponsor for this long march.

Besides this participation the CMC members accompanied by the Head of the activities department in the Jericho Municipality made a visit to the legislative council to observe the Library in it and the groups it serves.

Graduated by the minister of negotiations Dr. Sa'eb Erekat

The Jericho CMC conclude training on consolidating parliamentary education among youth

The children of the Jericho CMC concluded a 30 hour training course on the parliamentary education among youth which took place in the Jericho resort village and supervised by a team from the legislative council and coordinated by the Jericho Municipality activities department.

Dr. Sa'eb Erakat the minister of negotiations and the PLC member graduated the 40 students who attended the course and composed of ex and official CMC members and children from the kid's club. An open discussion took place between children and the minister and encompassed inquires about how the PLC operates, integrity, democracy and parliamentary work.

Esmat Al- Husseini an ex CMC member gave a speech in which she thanked the PLC on behalf the graduates for the efforts exerted in adding this useful information to the youth perception and thanked the Municipality for allowing the chance to participate.

She also commended Dr. Erekat presence and the free discussion that was allowed which reinforced democratic way and the lifting of barriers between children and political officials.

This training aimed at adding to the children's perception of the parliamentary life and the nature of operations taking place at the PLC and the formation of committees and the convening of sessions. Furthermore, they were briefed about the legislative elections, democracy, and integrity.

16/8/2004

 

The Mayor of Jericho discusses the reinforcement of cooperation between the Jericho and Lyon Municipalities

The mayor of Jericho Mr. Abdel- Karim Sider discussed with Mayor of the French city of Lyon Mr. Gerard Colomb during the last visit to France the possibilities to reinforce the reciprocal cooperation relations between the two cities. The focus points were in the field of health care training and benefiting from the Lyon experience in many aspects such as light technology and the possibility to send a technical team to make the necessary studies for lighting one of the city's prominent archeological sites. 

On the other hand, both parties have stressed on the need to reinforce the cooperation in the field of water filtration which is the field that the French municipality was committed to support funds for the establishment of a project in the city of Jericho.

The Mayor was escorted by the director of Public Relations in the Municipality of Jericho Miss Weam Erekat in addition to four Jericho CMC members who visited Paris and met with the Lyon CMC members and discussed various issues such as water and consumption rationalization.

7/7/2004

 

The Jericho CMC concludes schools workshops on Three CMC themes

The Jericho CMC concluded the first part of the UNICEF funded project "let's protects our water heritage and make Jericho healthy and green" which includes conducting workshops in the city schools.

This part included working on three themes, water, cleaning, and trees. The CMC members were split into six groups working in six classrooms in each school for two successive weeks where they offered information about these topics to student's from different age groups.

The Workshops encompassed all basic and secondary city schools and CMC members have set substantial examples to simplify the information about the three themes. This was done by posing questions about the different topics in a way that will urge students to follow up good behaviors that will make the city cleaner.

It is worth mentioning that the CMC members in Jericho were affiliated into a training course also funded by UNICEF which gave them the skills to conduct these types of workshops with the Jericho Municipality supervision. Miss Jumana Al- Ahmad UNICEF's youth project manager and Ms. Weam Erekat PR manager in the Jericho Municipality have conducted a visit to one of the schools during which the CMC members were making one of the workshops. Mr. Sulaiman Al Fahed the activities head section and Mr. Muhammad Al- Azmouty a PR officer in the Jericho Municipality have accompanied the Members throughout the whole activity.

9/5/2004

The Jericho CMC concludes a training session on various topics

The CMC in Jericho concluded 20 hour training on team work, planning, work shop organization, child psychology, cooperation, problem identification procedures, and the use of research. The course took place in the Municipality administered kid's club with supervision of Dr. Ibrahim Makawi and Raja' Al- Bargouthi, two trainers from the Teacher creativity center with coordination of the Jericho Municipality and Unicef funding.

As post- training a field visit was organized encompassing interviews conducted with citizens about water and environment issues. The CMC also intends to conduct a workshop for the city schools on these issues for the sake of implementing the skills acquired in this course and as part of the plan of action articulated by the Child council.

It is worth mentioning that the CMC members have been engaged in a number of training courses aiming at squiring various skills on all topics which reflected positively on the Council's performance and transfer of experiences to other children which explains the Unicef's selection of the city of Jericho to be a child friendly.

10/4/2004

The CMC children conclude a training course on water and environment

The Jericho CMC concluded a 4 day training course conducted in the Jericho Municipality and tackled subjects about water and environment. In the theoretical perspective of this course, Mr. Khaled Jaber the Director of Water department in Jericho municipality gave a presentation about the necessity of reducing water loss, keeping its resources and saving this heritage from being lost. This refers to the fact that there is water abundance in Jericho. In the field, children have visited Ein Al- Sultan water spring which is the main source of water and examined the water purification process taking place in the municipality lab.

Regarding environment, the subject was discussed by members of the wild life society whereas they clarified the importance of environment protection and the responsibility which falls on the local community in keeping a clean and healthy environment. Subjects like the bird immigration to and from Palestine were also discussed and the children made a field visit to the plant garden in Jericho. They also practiced the bird part ridging named after the society and Palestine. This course is part of a sequence of activities organized by Municipality executives in Jericho for the benefit of the CMC. These activities will continue to the end of the school year and will include workshops and lectures under the supervision of the CMC.
