Objectives:

1-Creating an encouraging and motivating atmosphere for a normal growth for children

2-Implanting values and culture of the society

3-refining the talents of children and developing their capacity

The building:

The building consists of three primary floors and an external garden.

The first floor: its northern part(the main entrance) combines a reception and registration area where all records and information related to the members are found, there is also a place for the parents to wait for their children.

In the southern part of the first floor there are two halls, the first one will be designated for the activities club and the second one for the art club.

The second floor: The office of the director of the club is located in the north eastern part of the second floor, in the north western part there is the office of the child municipal council. The southern part combines two halls, the first one will be designated for the computer club and the second one for the culture club.

The third floor: It combines a multipurpose meeting and lecture hall for children.

The garden:

The garden was designed in a way to be fit for the different types of activities carried out in the club; it combines a green area with beautiful flowers and a passageway with shadowed areas allowing the facilitators to perform some of the shows and outdoor activities in the greenery.

The clubs:

The Performances and activities club:

It will be concerned in organizing different activities for children (sports, musical, traditional, recreational, theatre, scouting, rangers, in addition to different and various social activities).

This club is equipped with a set of devices and educational means some of which are a TV set, Video, puppet theatre, and plenty of educational and mind urging games.

The arts club:

The main concern of this club is to develop and refine children's creative skills in the field of arts this will include (painting, handicrafts, acting, and theatre) there is a lot of workshops, painting studios, puppet theatre, and a number of different helping tools necessary for reaching this objective.

 

The computer club:

It aims on teaching children how to use computer devices and programming, developing their abilities in this field to make them capable of using the services and Hi- Tech such as the internet and e-mail, there is also 16 computers installed with printers and internet service.

 

The Culture club:

It concentrates on organizing different cultural activities and performances such as reading, narration, informational competitions, scientific research, cultural seminars, the art of writing, and dialogues. There are also books and educational means to encourage children to affiliate and be creative.

 

The targeted group:

Children from the ages of 6- 15 years old

These will be divided into 3 groups:

1-the mandarins group 6-9 years

2-the lemons group 10-12 years

3-the bananas group 13-15 years

Members will be issued a membership card that will indicate by its color and title the age group and consequently what kind of activities they will be engaged in.

Parents:

The kids club will arrange a number of activities and lectures for the parents in the clubs meeting hall to develop and improve their recognition of their children's needs and the best ways to deal with them.

Decision makers:

The club will conduct seminars and workshops with participation of the policy makers in the city in order to make them recognize the needs of children also to make them aware of the importance of making these needs on top of their priorities and concerns during planning, legislation, organizing, and project execution.

Advantages of subscription:

1. Every Child who is a member in the Kids Club is considered a member in the Municipal Library.

2. Every Child member in the Kids Club obtains a 25% discount on the fees paid for the different training courses conducted at the Club.

3. Every member will have the advantage of participating in Child-Oriented activities organized by the Municipality.

The opening hours:

The Kids Club will be open for members from 10:30 in the Morning until 5 O,clock in the afternoon. There will be an adjustment for the opening hours in the summer time and during school time. However, during the winter and in school vacation there will not be any adjustment for the opening hours.

The administration will receive feed-back from parents and kids about the best time for the opening hours.

Future annexed buildings:

of "The Dome of Scheherazad" for story narration. This dome will be equipped with audio and video and light effects. Its total cost will be 100000$, these schemes were submitted to the French Consulate in order to fund the execution of this project.

Future Plans:

The Jericho Municipality is now considering the annexation of a piece of land to be part of the building premises; this is to establish a closed auditorium for sports activities that need wide spaces

.Location:

The kids Club is located in Deir-yaseen Street, before the intersection with Jamal Abdel- Naser Street that leads to the city center. It is also behind the Jericho Chamber of commerce, opposite to the new mosque.
