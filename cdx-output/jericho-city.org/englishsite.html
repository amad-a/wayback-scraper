33.6 mm the amount of rain falling in The Jericho governorate

the amount of rain that have fallen on the Jericho governorate until the first of November for this year was 33.6 mm scoring a remarkable increase in the amounts of rain compared to last year. A 2.2 mm of rain was the amount in the same period of last year, the year before scored 41.6mm. This amount predicts a good season of farming as Jericho is greatly dependable on agriculture. The weather forecast department in Jericho stated that the annual normal levels of rain in the last 40 years was 26 mm until the beginning of December and until January will reach  60 mm. in February 96mm and in March 130mm and until may 152mm.

The 500 cubic meter water tank project supplies water for the south western part of the city of Jericho

The construction works at the 500 cubic meters water tank continue for the sake of improving the drinking water supply services for the south western part of the city and to increase the amount of water flow in that relatively high area. The tank is circularly shaped and placed on shafts 15m high. An iron ladder was placed in the middle and a pump close to the tank will also be included to pump water up the tank after which water will flow to the household using gravity. details
