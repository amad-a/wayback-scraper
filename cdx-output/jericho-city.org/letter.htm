The head of the Norway – Palestine friendship Committee delivers a letter from the Mayor and residents of Laerdal to the Mayor and residents of Jericho

Dear friends in Jericho                                                                                          Laerdal,April 23th 2003

From the people of Laerdal we send our warmest greetings to the people of Jericho, together with our most sincere congratulations on Laerdal Park in Jericho! We are happy and proud of our friends in Jericho, we admire your courage and willingness to do your best, in spite of all the difficulties your people have to stand.

We look forward to the day when a bigger delegation from Laerdal can visit Jericho and Laerdal Park. Until that day, let this book be a new symbol of our friendship and the bonds between our two communities. This book, by two artists from Laerdal, shows in photographs and poems something that our two peoples have in common- Stone: you are born in an Oasis with desert sand all around you, we are born with stone and steep mountains around us in all directions. Perhaps this background- different, but common, all the same- is part of the reality that makes us friends. Please, read the poem at page 22 as a special symbol of these common bonds:

We don’t lie flat

We who grew on stony ground

Even when we sleep

We have a stone in our back

It forces us

To sleep sitting

Gives our dream sharp edges

 

Yours Sincerely,

                                                                  

Olav Wendelbo 

Mayor of Laerdal                                                                                  Head of the friendship Committee
