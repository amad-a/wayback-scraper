What is IPYL?

Founded in May 1997, IPYL is non profit,independent and non-governmental organization offering development services to Palestinian Youth. It currently operates from a temporary office in Hebron, although its headguarters are to be in Jerusalem. It was officially registered by the Ministry of the Interior as a non-governmental organization in October 1997.

IPYL : INTERNATIONAL PALESTINIAN YOUTH LEAGUE


Why IPYL?

Young people wish to be in involved in the determination of their future and building of civic society. We want to help mobilise these young people to take an active role in this process. With the establishment of the Palestinian National Authority we can start to adress this long-felt need. We see opportunities in culture, the environment, health,housing and education.
