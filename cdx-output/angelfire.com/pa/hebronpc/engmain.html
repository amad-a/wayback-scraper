...Welcome to the International Palestinian Youth League
 

...more info
The International Palestinian Youth League is a pilot initiative, proposed in order to train young Palestinian leaders in the necessary skills to facilitate and develop a Palestinian "youth society" through activities and international relations. This project will provide the local community with a core group of individuals to promote and facilitate further outreach activities, initiate new projects, and encourage the formation of a solid and encompassing network composed of different Palestinian and International youth organizations.
