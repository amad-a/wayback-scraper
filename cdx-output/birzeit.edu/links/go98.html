GOLDEN
OLIVE AWARDS

There were 15 Golden Olive Award winners in 1998, out of a possible total of 113 (Internet service providers and computer companies are excluded from consideration), which represents only 13 percent of Palestinian websites. For a discussion of why there were so few this year, see the 12 February 1998 e-mail edition of Birzeit, Palestine and the World Wide Web.



academic & research

  • Applied Research Institute - Jerusalem - for offer high quality, up-to-date information on the continuing process of Israeli settlement during the peace process.
  • Center for Palestine Research and Studies (CPRS) - for continuing to offer up-to-date information on the development of Palestinian public opinion.
  • Landscape Perspectives on Palestine and International Conference in November 1998 - for the most interesting and entertaining conference webpage ever seen in Palestine.
  • Palestinian Academic Society for the Study of International Affairs (PASSIA) - for an excellent collection of images and other material on Jerusalem.


arts & culture

  • Khalil Sakakini Cultural Centre - for offerings an excellent introduction to Palestinian culture and current cultural events in Palestine.


business & economy

  • No successful websites. Tut, tut, all that money.


government & services

  • Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics - for providing such an important, regularly-updated resource.
  • Palestinian National Authority Official Website - for constantly striving to improve an already very useful service.
  • Population, Housing and Establishments Census 1997 website - for putting up an information-packed website at the same time the issue was prominent in the media.


human rights

  • LAW - The Palestinian Society for the Protection of Human Rights and the Environment - for regular updates in such a critical arena of information.


industry & products

  • No successful websites. The idea is to sell the product, guys.

news & media

  • Al-Hayat Al-Jadedah (in Arabic) - for an attractive, daily Arabic news service from Palestine.
  • Al-Quds Online (in Arabic) - for an attractive, daily Arabic news service from Palestine.
  • A Personal Diary of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict (in English) - for continuing to offer a well-presented, alternative perspective on the situation here.
