Fatah: http://www.fateh.org/ Fatah's online page. A site which is a mixture of Arabic and English contains daily news, communiques from Fatah, a guestbook and promises a biography section.Added to Complete Guide: 2 November 1998. Top of page.


Hamas: http://www.palestine-info.org/hamas/index-h.html The official Hamas website which is very regularly updated with news from the viewpoint of Hamas - only available in ArabicAdded to Complete Guide: 2 November 1998. Top of page.
