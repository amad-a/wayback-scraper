Bandak International Trading Co. Ltd: http://www.bandak.com/ - "The Palestinian Coffee Company", Bethlehem-based Bandak International Trading Co. Ltd., "was established many years ago for the purpose of doing international commerce. Ever since, we have been successful in maintaining our markets and developing new ones." A sparse homepage appealing for distributers of green coffee beans to sell to Bandak. It does this by proclaiming, We buy green coffee beans all the time." Added to Complete Guide: 6 February 1997. Review last updated: 16 December 1997. Top of page.


Bethlehem Star Olivewood Factory: http://www.bethlehem-olivewood.com/ - A wholesaler (minimum orders of US$400) dealing in "top quality handmade olivewood crafts from Bethlehem in the Holy Land." The factory deals in Nativity sets, crosses, boxes, Christmas ornaments, figures, animals, and other olivewood goods. Not the only olivewood factory in Bethlehem by any means but the first on the Web. Photos of all the items are available. Added to Complete Guide: 19 December 1997. Top of page.


Birzeit-Palestine Pharmaceutical Co.: http://www.birzeit-pharm.com/ - Owned by 450 shareholders withy a working capital of US$12 million and annual sales of $9 million, the comapny is the result of a merger in March 1993 between the Birzeit Pharmaceutical Co. and the Palestine Medical Co. The homepage offers links to information about its history and products. Diazepam it does, but Prozac is not on the list. Added to Complete Guide: 19 December 1997. Top of page.


Holy Land Mosaics - Bethlehem: http://www.stone-mosaics.com/ - This site gives some basic background information about Holy Land Mosaics, and features a few examples of their work. It doesn't list prices, purchase information, etc. In essence, when shopping for that perfect wedding gift for your cousin, this site doesn't help you. Nothing has changed since this website was added to the Guide. Added to Complete Guide: 19 May 1997. Review last updated: 16 December 1997. Top of page.


The Jordan Vegetable Oil Industries Co. Ltd.: http://www.zaytona.com/JVOIC/ - I visited this Nablus-based factory in 1989 when it, in common with all other Palestinian industries, was going through a hard time. Today, this company, established 44 years ago seems to be doing okay. The website, a single page product list, isn't doing as well. With a factory in Jordan established in 1992, this company supplies the West Bank, Jordan, the Gulf, Eastern Europe and the USA with pure palm and corn oil, ghee products, and margarine. Added to Complete Guide: 19 December 1997. Top of page.


Management Consulting Services: http://www.mcs-palestine.com - MCS is a company which offers consulting services for companies wishing to invest in Palestine. Carries out feasibility studies, market research and financial management amongst other things.Added to Complete Guide: 14 January 2000 Top of page.


San Advertising: http://www.san-ad.com/ - When you visit Palestine you'll be struck by the big billboards everywhere advertising Coke, Marlboro and various personal hygiene products. These are the people who design them.Added to Complete Guide: 19 September 1999. Top of page.


Tamimi Ceramics: http://www.tamimi-ceramics.com/ - A one-page website for this Hebron based ceramics company. A collection of samples of their work which they encourage you to order. Added to Complete Guide: 19 September 1999. Top of page.


Taqaddom Scales: http://www.taqaddom.com - You really know the Palestine web is moving forward when a Hebron based company that sells electric scales goes online.Added to Complete Guide: 19 September 1999. Top of page.


Taybeh Beer: http://www.taybehbeer.com/ - A simple website for this Palestinian company based in the West Bank village of Taybeh. Taybeh's Golden Beer has recently begun to be brewed in Germany, and the Palestinian factory near Ramallah welcomes visitors. Hey, we may not have a state, but we do have a beer. And it's good. Added to Complete Guide: 19 December 1997. Top of page.
