Photo Diary

Day 1: Wednesday 25th September 1996, "Intensive care unit"

Yasser Abdul Ghani, Birzeit student, hangs on to life by a thread in the Ramallah Hospital ICU. Shot in the chest, the bullet damaged his heart and lung. His condition stablised but it is not yet confirmed (30/9/96) that his brain was not damaged by the loss of blood. Photo by Saleem Daoud

Another injured demonstrator - Ayman Abdual Jawad (not completely confirmed that this is his photo) - in Ramallah ICU. Head injury. He later died. Photo by Saleem Daoud

His name, "Hisham", written on his arm, another ICU patient with a head injury. "Possibly brain dead" said the administrator of the hospital. Photo by Saleem Daoud
