LINKS TO OTHER INFORMATION ON THE GROUND

  • Palestinian newspaper sites
    Local newspapers in Arabic and English (http://www.amin.org) and links to other information in Arabic and English are available. The latter is not marked on the homepage in English, so use this link for convenience if you don't read Arabic: http://www.amin.org/anba.htm (this link updated 6/10/96)

  • Human Rights Information
    Local NGOs and media organisations (http://www.baraka.org/updates.html). Use this alternative link if access is difficult. Baraka is continuing to update their site with information. Especially good is the PHRIC press release with a zipped MS Word document listing the names of the martyrs and how they were killed. Live ammunition accounts for almost all of the 59 deaths but two. (Baraka page updated 4/10/96)

  • BT'selem, the Israeli Information Center for Human Rights in The Occupied Territories
    (http://btselem.netgate.net/press/index.htm) has some press releases about the situation. (new 6/10/96)

  • Bethlehem University Press Releases
    Some Bethlehem students were killed in the clashes. This link leads to their press releases (http://www.bethlehem.edu/press/press.htm). (new 2/10/96).

  • Palestinian Ministry of Health
    Some excellent material including a list of martyrs, casualties, and reports on latest developments (http://www.palnet.com/health/welcome.html). (Added 2/10/96)
