Trouble reading or printing out the information here?

The black background may make reading or printing out these pages difficult. Follow these steps for a quick solution:

  1. In Netscape (other browsers should have a similar option), goto the Options menu and select Preferences.
  2. Choose the preferences for Colours.
  3. Choose white or grey as the default colour for background.
  4. Click the checkbox that says "Always use my colours - overide document colours"
  5. Try printing again! Viewing will now be easier on the eyes.
