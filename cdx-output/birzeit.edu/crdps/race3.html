The Race Against Time

The Race Against Time is literally the race to preserve and document integral facets of Palestinian identity--a race which the Center for Research & Documentation of Palestinian Society can win only if it is able to raise over US$500,000 in the next several years to finance the project.

The Center hopes to obtain funds in a number of areas, including private and public grants, various funding institutes and other governmental organizations. Yet, without the help of individuals, such as yourselves, who care enough to generate the interest, funds and momentum needed to sustain this project, we will not succeed.
