Archeological and Historical Tour

Palestine - Home of history and Al-Aqsa - Archeological and Historical Tour, is a site project dedicated to the current history of Palestine from 1900 and up to today and a detailed archeological and historical tour inside Al-Aqsa mosque in the old city of Jerusalem . We in this project aiming to have a ready reference site for all information about the background and current history of the land of Palestine and the conflict on it between Arab and Jews and the city of Jerusalem and Al-Aqsa mosque.

This site is an on-line encyclopaedia about Palestine and Al-Aqsa mosque.

There is a lot of information to find in this site , kindly revisit and inform your friends about it . Your visit will make us more interested to develop the site and add more information .

We are also listing bellow our contact address and we are ready to replay to any question you have about the history of Palestine, Jerusalem , Al-Aqsa mosque and any other general information .
