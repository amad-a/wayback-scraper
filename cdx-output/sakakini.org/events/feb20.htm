AUGEST PROGRAM

Greetings:
Our August program of events features an art exhibit, two films, a Summer Evening concert, a poetry reading, a book reading and an open day for kids... If you would like us to send you our monthly program please e-mail us with the request…

** SUMMER EVENING CONCERT: JALAZON POPULAR ARTS GROUP
** TUESDAY 7/8 @ 6:30 PM
Dabka, singing and sketches will be performed in this months summer concert. To encourage the geographical continuity between camps, villages and cities in Palestine, we present to you The Jalazon Popular Arts Group, which will perform numerous dabkas by their Youth groups. Popular singer Mohammed Nakhla will sing traditional songs & songs of his own on the sounds of the "Tabla" drums. Rania Isaac from Bethlehem will sing for Marsayl Khaleefi and Fairuz, and Mohammed Jaber will perform for the third time at the Sakakini, this time a sarcastic play sketch…

**POETRY READING: MOHAMMED YAQOOB
** THURSDAY 9/8 @ 7:00 PM
Yaqoob, head of the translation department at "Bayt Ishir" House of Poetry will read from his new collection "Impersonating a life" (antahil hayat) which will be published soon. In it he talks about his struggle to continue living after he was hit by a bullet in his office, which has been the target of subsequent shelling.

**EXHIBIT: MOHAMMAD ABU-SILL "IDENTITY BETWEEN THE
WARMTH OF COLOR & THE DREAM OF GENERATIONS"
**OPENING: WEDNESDAY 15/8 @ 6:30 PM
A website designer from Gaza, Mohammad has participated in Darat Al Funun Artists Academy exhibit in 1999-2000. He has also participated in numerous group exhibits in Jordan and Gaza. His works represent a window that oversees a different time and place, the hero is unlimited, his enemies shaping his life, depicting him unwanted, a persona non grata; expelled, a foreigner, a prisoner. This is the artists first exhibit at the Centre it's part of a new program of the Sakakini's to showcase & support promising young artists.

**BOOK CLUB: "KHOTOWAT" (STEPS ) BY IZZAT GHAZZAWI
**MONDAY 20/8 @ 6:30 PM
Izzat Ghazawi will be here at our garden to talk about his new best-selling book "Khotwat" at this months Book Club. The book discusses issues of the Palestinian Diaspora and how it changed over time. We invite you to read the book and engage in lively discussion with the writer..

** FILM: "LE PAYS DE BLANCHE" & "THE SATTELLITE SHOOTERS"
**SUNDAY 26/8 @ 7:00 PM
The film "Le pays de Blanche" is the story of Blanche, a Palestinian woman in her 90's originally from Jaffa who had to leave with her family to the USA after the 1948 Nakba. In the film she talks about Palestine and immigration, and about the Palestinian longing for a homeland, the film also deals with the subject of Palestinians who live abroad and how they inherit the dream for a homeland.. 28 min. directed by Maryse Gargour 2000. (Non fiction, French, Eng. subtitles)

The film "The Satellite Shooters"- which has been shown in Arab Screen Independent Film Festival in Qatar-tells the story of Tawfiq, a young Palestinian boy in Texas trying to find his place in America. It is a story of assimilation and the immigrant experience. Orientalism meets Occidentalism when Tawfiq and an American friend embark upon a mission to change the world. But things don't turn out like they do in the movies.. 15 min directed by Annemarie Jacir. (Fiction, English)

Annmarie Jacir will be there to discuss her film and answer questions.
