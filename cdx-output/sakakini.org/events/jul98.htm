Current Activities

July, 1998:

This month, renovation work began inside and outside the Centre, and events were limited to two Nakba survivors testimonies on the shelling and fall of the city of Lydda in July 1948, the exodus of most if its population, and the internment and killing of those who remained. Two participants in the coastal area battles of July 1948, and witnesses to the fall of the village of Muzeira'a also spoke of their experiences.
