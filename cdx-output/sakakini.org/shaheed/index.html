Welcome to the online version of the new Sakakini exhibit "100 SHAHEED-100 LIVES", the memorial exhibit to the fallen of the Aqsa Intifada. The exhibit will showcase 100 personal photographs and objects belonging to the first 100 Shaheed (martyrs) of this Intifada. An accompanying exhibit catalogue will feature the personal photographs, as well as photographs of the personal objects, and short biographies in Arabic and English.

This memorial will be on display in 7 Palestinian cities. It will open at the Sakakini Centre in February 2001, and -if the current siege is eased- will travel to Bethlehem, Gaza City, Hebron, Jerusalem, Nablus, and Nazareth.

The project concept is by Adila Laïdi, Sakakini Centre Director. The exhibit is curated by prominent Palestinian artist Samir Salameh in a minimalist fashion, honoring the individuality of each Shaheed. Catalogue and Web Photographs of the objects are by Isabel de la Cruz.

For more information on this project, if you wish to obtain a catalogue, or host the exhibit in your city, please Email us. For feedback, please leave a message in the Guestbook
