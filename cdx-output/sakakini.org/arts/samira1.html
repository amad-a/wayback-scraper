Samira Badran


1994, (Outil) Drawing on photo
1993,(Al-Drass, Door of Jerusalem) Acrylic on paper
Her work:
Badran’s works are filled with an “explosive anger characterized by a savage psychodrama of paint”. She has recently experimented with works based on photocopies and with painting on photographs.
