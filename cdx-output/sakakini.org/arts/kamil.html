Kamel Mughanni


The Artist:
Mughanni was born in Gaza in 1943, he received his BA from the College of Fine Arts in Alexandria, Egypt in 1966, and his MA in the Psychology of symbols and colors in 1987. Mughanni has won medals for his work in Russia, Scotland, Kuwait, Jordan and Jerusalem. Mughanni is a founding member of the College of Fine Arts at Al-Najah National University in Nablus, and retired from his position as the Chairman of the Visual Arts Department in 2000. He now lives in Gaza City.
