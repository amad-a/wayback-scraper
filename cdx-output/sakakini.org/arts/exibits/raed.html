“Spirit of the Earth”
Raed Issa
4/2001

The first solo exhibit of the artist in twenty years, the exhibit showcases work born out of the artist’s 10 year long experience with the works of clay, mud and natural dyes. The exhibit features medium scale works, large-scale mural paintings and mud sculptures of human figures. Some of the works are part of the award winning series “Ana Ismaeel” that won the first prize at the Cairo Biennial Art fair ’98.
