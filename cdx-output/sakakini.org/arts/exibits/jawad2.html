Pandemonium
6/2002
Jawad Ibrahim

The artist:
This is the 8th solo exhibit of the artist Jawwad Ibrahim, and the 2nd solo exhibit at the Sakakini Centre. We behold works in acrylic on fabric and stone sculptures that accompany the exhibit. Pandemonium is manifest through his colors, the patterns and the movement that truly transmit clamor and turmoil.
