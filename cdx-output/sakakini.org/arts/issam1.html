Issam Bader


1997 Ceramic Mural

His work:
Bader’s earlier works consisted of mostly ceramic individual works and oil paintings, although he did execute a giant bronze mural adorning the façade of the Ramallah municipality. Most recently he has been busy providing mural works for private homes and commissions for public buildings such as The Grand Park Hotel and the Al-Ayyam daily Newspaper in Ramallah.
