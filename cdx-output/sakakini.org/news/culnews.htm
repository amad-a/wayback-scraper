News of Palestinian Culture

This section features a record of the cultural activities that have taken place in Palestine from 1996 to December 1998. It reflects the cultural renaissance that took place in the country with the liberation of most of its towns and cities from Israeli military occupation. From January 1999, the section will only feature information on major and significant events,  instead of listing all events
