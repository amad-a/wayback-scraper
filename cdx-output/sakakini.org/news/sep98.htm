Other News of Palestinian Culture

September, 1998:

* Visual Arts:

- Visual artist Hussni Radwan held his 8th exhibit at the Ramallah Matall  gallery. The exhibit featured paintings on canvas and assemblages of recycled furniture, doors, etc..

- Visual artist Ali Kleibo held an exhibit of his new works “ Dreamt Cities” at Gaza City’s Arts & Crafts Village.

- Sixteen-year old Hani Amra held his first photo exhibit of Jerusalem’s Old City at Ramallah’s French Cultural Center.

* Literature:

- Poet Mutawakel Taha edited a new book titled Al-Kunuz (treasures), devoted to newly discovered texts by poet Ibrahim Tuqan. The book features 32 unpublished poems, his letters to his sister, poet Fadwa Tuqan, as well as a collection of articles published in the Palestinian press of the 1930’s and 40s. The book is published by Acre’s Dar El Aswar.

- Poet Ali el Khalili published a collection of his newspaper columns that have appeared in the local press between 1977-1997: Kharaet wa Khuyul  (Maps and Horses) at Acre’s Dar el Aswar.
