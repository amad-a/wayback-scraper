Other News of Palestinian Culture

August, 1998:
 

* Special Events:

The Ramallah Festival concentrated this year on hosting Arab singers from Morocco, Egypt and Tunisia, such as Samira Saïd, Azza Balbah, etc.. as well as a number of local, European and African folklore troops.
 

* Visual Arts:

Visual artist Tayseer Barakat held two shows this month, first, the transfer of his new Exhibit at the Wasiti was exhibited to his Ramallah Ziryab gallery, and an exhibit of his older works was held in Gaza City, at the newly opened Gaza Arts & Crafts Village.
 

* Literature:

- A large book fair was held at El Bireh’s Baladna cultural and community center, focusing on new publications by the Bayareq publishing House.

- Short story writer Mahmoud Chqeir published a new autobiographical novel titled “Another Shadow to the City” -Dhill Akhar lil-Madina- recounting his childhood in Jerusalem, schooldays,  political activism and subsequent imprisonment and exile, and return. The book is published by Dar el Quds.
