Palestinian Poets

Mureed Barghouti 

Barghouti is one of the most famous Palestinian poets. He was born in 1944, and lived most of his life in the Diaspora, he now lives in Cairo. His works started as patriotic poems, and evolved into a modernist sensibility and produced some of the most compelling poetic experiments among Arab poets of his generation. He has several collections of poetry. He was awarded Cairo's American University 1997 Mahfouz Najib Literature Prize.
