Palestinian Poets

May Sayigh

Sayigh was born in 1940 in Gaza. She obtained in BA in sociology from Cairo University. She was the president of the Union of Palestinian Women and has been very active in the cause of Palestinian women. She has published a number of poetry collections, as well as a prose account of the 1982 Israeli invasion of Lebanon in "The Siege". She lives in Paris.
